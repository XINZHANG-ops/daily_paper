# WorldMirror 2.0

## What It Is

WorldMirror 2.0 is the world composition component of HY-World 2.0. Its key innovation is normalized position encoding, which enables flexible resolution inference without performance degradation. Combined with 3D Gaussian Splatting and MaskGaussian, it produces navigable 3D assets.
