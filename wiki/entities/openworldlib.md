# OpenWorldLib

## What It Is

OpenWorldLib is a unified world model framework providing a formal definition and modular architecture. A world model requires three capabilities: perception, interaction, and long-term memory. The framework has six components: Operator, Synthesis, Reasoning, Representation, Memory, and Pipeline. OpenWorldLib reorganizes prior work (Dreamer, V-JEPA, etc.) under this definition and evaluates on AI2-THOR and LIBERO with models like π0 and Cosmos.
