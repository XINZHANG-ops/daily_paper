# Video-MME-v2

## What It Is

Video-MME-v2 is a comprehensive video understanding benchmark with 800 videos and 3,200 questions. It introduces three key innovations: a tri-level capability hierarchy (information aggregation, temporal dynamics, complex reasoning), group-based consistency evaluation, and a non-linear scoring mechanism. The benchmark reveals that the best model (Gemini-3-Pro at 49.4 Non-Lin Score) has a 41-point gap vs human experts (90.7), driven by hierarchical error propagation and language prior over-reliance.
