# NLP

## Overview

Natural Language Processing research in this wiki focuses on fundamental properties like textual frequency that affect LLM performance across translation, reasoning, and tool use.

## Evolution

Early April 2026, Adam's Law (2604.02176) established textual frequency as a fundamental dimension affecting LLM performance. The key insight was that high-frequency data improves both prompting and fine-tuning, suggesting that frequency-aware training recipes could be a simple but effective improvement over standard approaches. This complements the data-centric AI theme while providing a specific lens (textual frequency) on the broader question of what makes training data effective.
