# HY-Embodied-0.5: Embodied Foundation Models for Real-World Agents

**Published**: 2026-04-07 | **Added**: 2026-04-10 | **arXiv**: [2604.07430](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.07430)

## Summary

HY-Embodied-0.5 is a family of embodied foundation models targeting real-world agent deployment. Key innovations include: (1) **Mixture-of-Transformers (MoT)** architecture enabling modality-adaptive computing with non-shared parameters for visual and text tokens, (2) **HY-ViT 2.0** native-resolution visual encoder, (3) iterative self-evolving post-training with RL and rejection sampling fine-tuning, and (4) large-to-small on-policy distillation.

The architecture addresses the gap between general VLMs and embodied agent requirements—specifically fine-grained visual perception for physical grounding and embodied prediction, interaction, and planning capabilities.

## Key Contributions

- **MoT architecture**: Modality-adaptive computing using non-shared parameters for visual and text tokens
- **Large-scale pre-training**: 600B+ tokens with embodied-spatial mid-training
- **Task-aware RL with hybrid rewards**: Combines GRPO with task-specific reward shaping
- **On-policy distillation**: Large-to-small model transfer maintaining embodied capabilities

## Method

Architecture uses MoT enabling modality-adaptive computing, HY-ViT 2.0 for native-resolution encoding, 600B+ tokens pre-training, embodied-spatial mid-training, task-aware GRPO with hybrid rewards, iterative self-evolution, and large-to-small on-policy distillation.

## Results

- **MoT-2B**: State-of-the-art on 16/22 benchmarks, outperforming Qwen3-VL-4B by 10.2% and RoboBrain2.5-4B by 8.6%
- **MoE-A32B**: 67.0% average, surpassing Gemini 3.0 Pro (63.6%)
- **Robot experiments**: 85%, 80%, 75% success rates on packing, stacking, and mug hanging tasks
- Strong generalization to unseen scenarios and objects
