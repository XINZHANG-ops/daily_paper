# Seedance 2.0: Advancing Video Generation for World Complexity

**Published**: 2026-04-14 | **Added**: 2026-04-16 | **arXiv**: [2604.14148](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14148)

## Summary

Seedance 2.0 advances video generation for real-world complexity through a native multi-modal audio-video generation model. The unified architecture jointly processes text, image, audio, and video inputs for greater controllability. Key focus areas include enhanced motion modeling, physics compliance, binaural audio generation, and the comprehensive SeedVideoBench 2.0 evaluation framework.

The goal is generating temporally coherent, physically plausible, and highly controllable video content with synchronized high-fidelity audio across diverse scenarios.

## Key Contributions

- **Native multi-modal audio-video**: Joint processing of text, image, audio, and video inputs
- **Enhanced motion modeling**: Better temporal coherence and physical plausibility
- **Binaural audio generation**: Synchronized high-fidelity audio with video
- **SeedVideoBench 2.0**: Comprehensive evaluation framework for video generation

## Method

Large-scale multimodal architecture with enhanced motion modeling, physics compliance, and binaural audio generation. Comprehensive evaluation framework covering motion quality, prompt following, aesthetics, audio quality, audio-visual sync, and audio prompt following.

## Results

State-of-the-art performance on T2V, I2V, and R2V tasks ranking first in:
- Motion quality
- Prompt following
- Aesthetics
- Audio quality
- Audio-visual sync
- Audio prompt following
