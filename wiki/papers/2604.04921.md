# TriAttention: Efficient Long Reasoning with Trigonometric KV Compression

**Published**: 2026-04-05 | **Added**: 2026-04-07 | **arXiv**: [2604.04921](http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.04921)

## Summary

TriAttention addresses the memory bottleneck created by massive KV caches during long chain-of-thought reasoning in LLMs. Existing post-RoPE KV compression methods are unstable because query rotation after RoPE limits the observation window—important keys that would score high on importance end up with low similarity to current queries. TriAttention exploits a pre-RoPE Q/K concentration property: before rotation, queries and keys cluster around fixed non-zero centers, allowing importance scoring to work on a much larger context. A trigonometric series derived from pre-RoPE Q/K centers plus norm-based auxiliary signals enables efficient KV cache pruning.

## Key Contributions

- Identifies pre-RoPE Q/K concentration as the key property enabling stable long-context importance estimation
- Introduces trigonometric series-based importance scoring using pre-RoPE Q/K centers
- Achieves 2.5x throughput improvement or 10.7x KV memory reduction at equivalent accuracy on AIME25 and MATH 500
- Outperforms SnapKV and R-KV baselines while maintaining full attention accuracy

## Method

TriAttention operates in three steps. First, it computes trigonometric series values from pre-RoPE Q/K centers—this captures the geometric structure of attention patterns before rotation. Second, it combines these with norm-based auxiliary signals for importance scoring. Third, it applies concentration-adaptive weighting to determine which KV entries to retain. The key insight is that pre-RoPE concentration means queries and keys from similar contexts cluster together even before rotation, providing a stable signal for importance scoring across the full context.

## Results

On AIME25 and MATH 500, TriAttention matches Full Attention accuracy while delivering 2.5x throughput gain or 10.7x KV memory reduction. It consistently outperforms SnapKV and R-KV, particularly on tasks requiring reasoning over very long contexts.

## Limitations & Future Work

TriAttention requires knowing the maximum context length at initialization for pre-RoPE center computation. The trigonometric series adds computational overhead for importance scoring that may offset gains for short sequences.
