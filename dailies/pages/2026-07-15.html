Xiaomi-Robotics-U0: Unified Embodied Synthesis with World Foundation Model

Published: 2026-07-13

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.11643

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents a unified embodied synthesis model for robotics, situated at the intersection of foundation generative models, world models, and embodied AI. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** The work builds upon foundation image/video generation models (e.g., EMU3.5) and embodied world models; its new idea is reformulating embodied synthesis as a natural extension of foundation generation within a single unified autoregressive framework, rather than adapting foundation models solely on robot data. 3. **❓ Problem:** It addresses the limitations of directly applying foundation generative models to embodied scenarios, specifically the lack of multi-view consistency, geometric coherence, robot embodiment constraints, and the loss of generalization from pre-training when models are adapted with limited robot data. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** The paper proposes Xiaomi-Robotics-U0, a 38-billion-parameter multimodal autoregressive model based on EMU3.5/Qwen-3-32B, trained jointly on text-to-image, image editing, embodied scene generation, embodied transfer, and embodied video generation under a unified next-token prediction objective, with FlashAR+ and vLLM integration for inference acceleration. 5. **📊 Results and Evaluation:** The model achieves state-of-the-art results, outperforming GPT-Image-2.0 in human evaluation on embodied scene generation and transfer, ranking first on the World Arena benchmark for embodied video generation, and improving the out-of-distribution success rate of π0.5 from 36.9% to 63.2% on real-world manipulation tasks.

ABot-AgentOS: A General Robotic Agent OS with Lifelong Multi-modal Memory

Published: 2026-07-11

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.10350

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents a general-purpose robotic Agent Operating System (ABot-AgentOS) for embodied intelligence, situated at the intersection of robotics, vision-language models, multi-modal memory, and long-horizon agent reasoning.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on dual-system theory, VLA models, agent frameworks (e.g., ReAct, Toolformer), and memory systems (e.g., MemGPT, Mem0, MIRIX), it introduces a unified modular Agent OS with a typed source-grounded multi-modal graph memory and a failure-driven lifelong self-evolution loop with no ground-truth leakage.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses three gaps in long-horizon embodied agents: the reasoning-execution gap (lack of intermediate agent layer between VLMs and controllers), the embodiment-generalization gap (tight coupling to specific hardware), and the persistent embodied-memory gap (inability to preserve multi-modal, relational, source-grounded experience across long-term interaction).
4. 🛠️ Methods: The system employs a hierarchical Agent Harness separating a main LLM, a context-isolated Skill Runner, and a multi-stage Verifier; a Universal Multi-modal Graph Memory with typed nodes/edges and hybrid retrieval; edge-cloud collaborative routing and privacy-aware memory gating; a split-wise failure-driven self-evolution protocol that compiles gated JSON DSL evo-assets; and a four-stage training pipeline (text-based sandbox, teacher trajectory distillation, SFT, and RL with a self-evolving LLM-as-a-Judge reward engine using GiGPO).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: On memory benchmarks, ABot-AgentOS Static achieves 87.5 on LoCoMo, 59.9 on OpenEQA EM-EQA, 88.6 on Mem-Gallery, and 76.5 Acc@All on NExT-QA, with self-evolution further lifting LoCoMo to 88.7, OpenEQA to 60.4, and Mem-Gallery to 89.0; on the EmbodiedWorldBench subset, it improves TSR by 11.99% and GCR by 10.84% over a ReAct single-controller baseline, and the self-evolving reward engine boosts human alignment of the LLM judge from ~60% to above 90%.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文提出了一种面向具身智能的通用机器人智能体操作系统（ABot-AgentOS），涵盖机器人技术、视觉-语言模型、多模态记忆与长时程智能体推理等交叉领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于双系统理论、VLA 模型、ReAct/Toolformer 等智能体框架以及 MemGPT、Mem0、MIRIX 等记忆系统，论文提出了一个统一的模块化 Agent OS，结合类型化的多模态图记忆与无监督标签泄漏的失败驱动的终身自演化机制。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文针对长时程具身智能体存在的三个差距：推理-执行鸿沟（VLM 与底层控制器之间缺乏中间智能体层）、跨具身泛化鸿沟（与特定硬件紧耦合）以及持久化具身记忆鸿沟（无法在长期交互中保存多模态、关系化、源溯源的经验）。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 系统采用分层 Agent Harness，分离主 LLM、上下文隔离的 Skill Runner 与多阶段 Verifier；构建带类型化节点/边与混合检索的通用多模态图记忆；设计边-云协同路由与隐私感知的记忆门控；提出基于分片的失败驱动自演化协议以生成门控 JSON DSL 演化资产；并构建四阶段训练流水线（文本沙盒、教师轨迹蒸馏、SFT 与基于自演化 LLM-as-a-Judge 奖励引擎和 GiGPO 优势的强化学习）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在记忆基准上，ABot-AgentOS Static 在 LoCoMo 上达到 87.5、OpenEQA EM-EQA 达到 59.9、Mem-Gallery 达到 88.6、NExT-QA Acc@All 达到 76.5，自演化进一步将其提升至 88.7、60.4 与 89.0；在 EmbodiedWorldBench 子集上，相较 ReAct 单控制器基线 TSR 提升 11.99%、GCR 提升 10.84%；自演化奖励引擎将 LLM 评判器与人类一致性由约 60% 提升至 90% 以上。

Read It Back: Pretrained MLLMs Are Zero-Shot Reward Models for Text-to-Image Generation

Published: 2026-07-12

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.11886

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on reward modeling for text-to-image (T2I) generation reinforcement learning, at the intersection of multimodal large language models (MLLMs) and image synthesis.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on prior reward modeling approaches that use human preference annotations (e.g., ImageReward, HPSv3) and zero-shot MLLM-based judges via scalar scoring (e.g., VIEScore) or VQA decomposition (e.g., AlphaGRPO/DVReward). The new idea is to repurpose a frozen pretrained MLLM's image-conditioned prompt log-likelihood as a training-free, off-the-shelf reward signal, avoiding preference labels, fine-tuning, and question decomposition.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the challenge of designing an efficient, reliable, and practical reward model for T2I RL that eliminates the need for costly preference annotations, reward-model fine-tuning, and complex multi-stage pipelines, while still providing strong supervision for compositional generation.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method introduces **SpectraReward**, which measures the mean image-conditioned prompt log-likelihood (the "semantic spectrum") via a single teacher-forced forward pass through a frozen MLLM. It further proposes **Self-SpectraReward**, a special case for unified multimodal models where the policy's own understanding branch serves as the reward model for its generation branch, creating a closed-loop self-improving framework with intrinsic reward-policy alignment.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Across two diffusion backbones (SD3.5-M and BAGEL), three RL algorithms (AWM, FlowGRPO, DiffusionNFT), nine reward MLLMs from four families (4B–235B parameters), and five out-of-distribution benchmarks, SpectraReward and Self-SpectraReward consistently and significantly outperform the BAGEL baseline (e.g., +10.0 on TIIF-Bench) and the prior AlphaGRPO method. Notably, Self-SpectraReward matches or surpasses reward models 4x–30x larger, demonstrating that reward-policy alignment matters more than scale.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文聚焦于文生图（T2I）强化学习中的奖励建模，跨足多模态大语言模型（MLLM）与图像合成领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于此前利用人类偏好标注（如 ImageReward、HPSv3）训练奖励模型，以及通过标量打分（如 VIEScore）或 VQA 分解（如 AlphaGRPO/DVReward）将预训练 MLLM 作为零样本评估器的研究。其新思路在于复用冻结 MLLM 的图像条件文本对数似然作为训练无关、开箱即用的奖励信号，无需偏好标签、奖励微调或问题分解。
3. ❓ 问题: 本文致力于解决如何设计一种高效、可靠且实用的 T2I 强化学习奖励模型，从而避免昂贵的偏好标注、奖励模型微调以及复杂的多阶段流程，同时仍能提供强大的组合生成监督信号。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该方法提出了 **SpectraReward**，通过冻结 MLLM 单次教师前向传播计算图像条件下提示词的平均对数似然（即"语义谱"）作为奖励。同时引入 **Self-SpectraReward**，针对统一多模态模型，将策略模型自身的理解分支作为生成分支的奖励模型，形成具有内在奖励-策略对齐的闭环自我提升框架。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在两种扩散模型骨干（SD3.5-M 和 BAGEL）、三种强化学习算法（AWM、FlowGRPO、DiffusionNFT）、九种来自四个家族（4B–235B 参数）的奖励 MLLM 以及五个分布外基准测试上的实验表明，SpectraReward 和 Self-SpectraReward 一致且显著地优于 BAGEL 基线（如 TIIF-Bench 提升 +10.0）和先前的 AlphaGRPO 方法。值得注意的是，Self-SpectraReward 能够匹敌甚至超越规模大 4 至 30 倍的奖励模型，证明奖励-策略的对齐比模型规模更为关键。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with the Xiaomi-Robotics-U0 paper (Paper 1) as it provides the broadest foundation—unifying generative modeling with embodied AI—and its impressive 38B-parameter results establish a strong baseline context. Then read SpectraReward (Paper 3), as its training-free, log-likelihood-based reward signal could be readily plugged into U0's RL/alignment pipeline to improve compositional generation without extra annotations. Finally, ABot-AgentOS (Paper 2) complements both by tackling the downstream reasoning and long-horizon execution side, showing how a generation-capable backbone like U0 could be wrapped in a typed multi-modal memory and self-evolving agent harness for real-world deployment.
建议首先阅读 Xiaomi-Robotics-U0（论文1），因为它覆盖面最广，将生成式建模与具身智能统一起来，且其 38B 参数的出色结果奠定了坚实的研究背景。接着读 SpectraReward（论文3），其免训练、基于对数似然的奖励信号可直接嵌入 U0 的 RL/对齐流程，无需额外标注即可提升组合生成质量。最后阅读 ABot-AgentOS（论文2），它从下游推理与长时程执行的角度对前两篇形成补充，展示了如何将具备生成能力的主干（如 U0）封装在类型化多模态记忆与自进化智能体框架中，从而实现真实场景部署。
