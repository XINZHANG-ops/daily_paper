Published: 2025-06-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.18254

1. üìò Topic and Domain: Reinforcement learning for language models, specifically extending RLVR (Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards) to general domains beyond mathematics and code.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on RLVR which uses domain-specific verifiers for reward signals, proposes using LLM's intrinsic probability of generating correct answers as reward signals instead of external verifiers.
