Paper 1

MolmoAct2: Action Reasoning Models for Real-world Deployment

Published: 2026-05-03

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.02881

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces MolmoAct2, a fully open-source vision-language-action reasoning model designed for real-world deployment on accessible robotic platforms.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on prior VLA models like MolmoAct, it advances the field with Molmo2-ER, an embodied-reasoning VLM backbone, an open-weight FAST action tokenizer, a per-layer KV-cache action expert architecture, and adaptive depth reasoning.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the limitations of existing VLA systems that are either closed-source, tied to expensive hardware, suffer from prohibitive reasoning latency, or achieve success rates below the threshold for dependable real-world use.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors train a specialized VLM via a two-stage recipe, graft a flow-matching continuous action expert onto discrete VLM layers through per-layer KV-cache conditioning, release an open FAST tokenizer and three curated datasets, and introduce adaptive depth tokens that only update changed scene regions.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: MolmoAct2 outperforms strong baselines including π0.5 across seven simulation and real-world benchmarks, while Molmo2-ER surpasses GPT-5 and Gemini Robotics ER-1.5 on 13 embodied-reasoning tasks, demonstrating robust zero-shot deployment and efficient fine-tuning.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文介绍了MolmoAct2，一种专为在现实世界中低成本至中成本机器人平台上部署而设计的完全开源的视觉-语言-动作（VLA）推理模型。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 它在MolmoAct等先前VLA研究基础上，提出了面向空间与具身推理的VLM主干Molmo2-ER、开源FAST动作分词器、基于每层KV缓存的连续动作专家架构，以及仅重预测变化区域的自适应深度推理机制。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有VLA系统多为闭源、依赖昂贵硬件、显式推理导致延迟过高、且微调成功率不足以支撑可靠实际部署的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包括通过“先专项化后复习”的两阶段方案训练VLM主干，利用每层KV缓存连接将流匹配连续动作专家嫁接至离散VLM，基于多具身轨迹训练开源FAST分词器，整理三个跨平台数据集，并引入仅对变化区域重新预测的自适应深度token。
5. 📊 结果与评估: MolmoAct2在涵盖仿真与真实世界的7项基准上超越π0.5等强基线，而Molmo2-ER在13项具身推理基准上超过GPT-5与Gemini Robotics ER-1.5，验证了模型的零样本部署与高效微调能力。

Paper 2

Co-Evolving Policy Distillation

Published: 2026-04-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.27083

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces Co-Evolving Policy Distillation (CoPD) in the machine learning domain, specifically as a post-training paradigm for consolidating multiple reasoning capabilities (text, image, and video) into a single large multimodal model.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds upon RLVR and OPD/MOPD, proposing that parallel expert branches should interleave domain-specific RLVR with bidirectional mutual OPD during training to co-evolve, rather than isolating expert training from distillation.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the capability divergence cost in mixed-data RLVR and the poor absorption efficiency in static OPD caused by excessive behavioral drift between independently converged teachers and the student.
4. 🛠️ Methods: CoPD initializes parallel branches from a shared base, alternately applying branch-specific GRPO to deepen expertise and mutual cross-branch OPD (with token-level KL divergence) to transfer knowledge while maintaining behavioral proximity, finally merging the co-evolved parameters.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: CoPD consistently outperforms mixed RLVR, static OPD, and MOPD across text, image, and video benchmarks, achieving superior all-in-one integration that even surpasses domain-specific experts.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文在机器学习与大型多模态模型后训练领域，提出了一种名为协同进化策略蒸馏（CoPD）的新范式，旨在将文本、图像和视频等多种推理能力整合到单一模型中。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于RLVR和OPD/MOPD范式，提出让多个专家分支在训练过程中并行进行领域特定的RLVR和双向互蒸馏，使各分支互为教师协同进化，而非先独立训练专家再事后蒸馏。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了混合数据RLVR中存在的能力发散（梯度冲突）问题，以及静态OPD流程中由于专家独立收敛导致与学生行为模式差异过大、知识难以被有效吸收的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法为从共享基模型初始化并行分支，交替执行分支特定的GRPO强化学习以深化领域能力，以及双向互在线策略蒸馏（基于token级KL散度）进行跨领域知识迁移并保持行为接近，最终合并参数得到统一模型。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 实验结果表明，CoPD在文本、图像和视频推理的多个基准测试中均优于混合RLVR、静态OPD和MOPD基线，实现了超越单一领域专家的全能一体化整合，消融实验验证了双向蒸馏和协同进化的必要性。

Large Language Models Explore by Latent Distilling

Published: 2026-04-26

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24927

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper proposes Exploratory Sampling (ESamp), a decoding method that encourages semantic diversity in large language models via online latent distillation, situated in the domain of natural language processing and LLM test-time scaling. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** Building upon stochastic sampling, structured search, logit-level control, and Random Network Distillation (RND), the paper introduces using an online-trained lightweight Latent Distiller to estimate novelty in continuous representation space rather than token space. 3. **❓ Problem:** It solves the problem that standard LLM decoding produces superficial lexical variation without genuine semantic exploration, causing redundant reasoning strategies and diminishing returns during test-time scaling. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** The method trains a lightweight Latent Distiller online during inference to predict deep-layer hidden states from shallow-layer ones, uses the prediction error as an intrinsic novelty reward to reweight logits under a KL-regularized framework, and deploys this via an asynchronous pipeline. 5. **📊 Results and Evaluation:** ESamp significantly improves Pass@k efficiency and semantic diversity across mathematical, scientific, coding, and creative writing benchmarks while maintaining coherence, generalizing robustly across models, and incurring less than 5% throughput overhead.

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

I recommend starting with MolmoAct2, as its tangible robotics deployment provides the clearest motivation and architecture for multimodal reasoning. It naturally connects to CoPD, which tackles the harder training-level problem of consolidating diverse multimodal experts into one model, while ESamp offers a complementary inference-time decoding strategy that could boost reasoning diversity in either system.
建议首先阅读 MolmoAct2，其真实的机器人部署场景为多模态推理提供了最直观的切入点。该工作与 CoPD 形成上下呼应：CoPD 从训练范式层面解决了如何将多样的多模态专家能力蒸馏整合到单一模型中，而 ESamp 则提供了推理阶段的互补方法，可进一步增强上述系统的语义探索与推理多样性。
