PixWorld: Unifying 3D Scene Generation and Reconstruction in Pixel Space

Published: 2026-07-05

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.05373

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses 3D scene generation and reconstruction in computer vision, proposing a unified framework for both tasks. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** It builds on prior latent-space diffusion methods (e.g., Gen3R, FlashWorld) that unify 3D generation and reconstruction, and introduces the new idea of performing diffusion directly in pixel space without a VAE/RAE bottleneck, supervised through differentiable rendering. 3. **❓ Problem:** It tackles the information loss and extra training cost introduced by pretrained VAE/RAE encoders in latent-space methods, and the misalignment between the diffusion objective and the underlying 3D representation. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** PixWorld uses a two-stream DiT that partitions multi-view inputs into clean and noisy subsets, applies flow matching in pixel space, decodes a pixel-aligned 3D Gaussian representation with predicted depth, and adds a geometry perception loss using a frozen 3D foundation model (π3) for structural supervision. 5. **📊 Results:** PixWorld outperforms prior latent-space generation methods and matches state-of-the-art reconstruction methods on RealEstate10K, DL3DV-10K, and WorldScore benchmarks, with the geometry perception loss yielding significant gains in pose accuracy and 3D consistency.

Vision Pretraining for Dense Spatial Perception

Published: 2026-07-05

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.05247

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on self-supervised vision pretraining for dense spatial perception, specifically targeting boundary-aware visual representations for embodied AI and depth estimation tasks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Built upon self-distillation frameworks (DINO, DINOv2, DINOv3, iBOT) and boundary/line-segment detection literature (LSD, attraction field representations), the new idea is making boundaries native learning signals rather than downstream outputs, introducing a boundary-centric masked modeling paradigm with categorical reparameterization.
3. ❓ Problem: Modern visual foundation models prioritize semantic invariance at the expense of fine-grained spatial understanding, and boundaries are treated as outputs requiring expensive supervision rather than being used as organizing signals for learning dense representations from raw images alone.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method proposes masked boundary modeling—a self-distillation framework where teacher-discovered boundary tokens are forced into the student's mask (boundary-forcing masking), boundary fields are reparameterized as categorical distributions over discretized bins, and a-contrario validation provides parameter-free statistical testing; this is scaled to LingBot-Vision (1B ViT-g) and further applied to upgrade LingBot-Depth to LingBot-Depth 2.0.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: LingBot-Vision achieves the best NYU-Depth v2 RMSE (0.296) among all compared models including the 7B DINOv3 (0.309), reaches 70.0/73.5 J&F on DAVIS/YouTube-VOS matching distilled models, achieves leading results on 14 depth completion benchmarks, and its 0.3B distilled student matches the 7B DINOv3's NYUv2 accuracy with ~23× fewer parameters.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文专注于面向密集空间感知的自监督视觉预训练，具体针对边界感知的视觉表示，用于具身智能和深度估计任务。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于自蒸馏框架（DINO、DINOv2、DINOv3、iBOT）以及边界/线段检测文献（LSD、引力场表示），新思路是将边界作为原生学习信号而非下游输出，引入了以边界为中心的掩码建模范式并采用分类重参数化。
3. ❓ 问题: 现代视觉基础模型优先考虑语义不变性而牺牲了细粒度的空间理解能力，边界被视为需要昂贵监督的输出，而非用于从原始图像中学习密集表示的组织信号。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该方法提出了掩码边界建模——一个自蒸馏框架，其中教师发现的边界标记被强制加入学生的掩码集（边界强制掩码），边界场被重新参数化为离散分箱上的分类分布，对立假设验证提供无需参数的统计检验；该方法被扩展为 LingBot-Vision（10亿参数 ViT-g），并进一步用于将 LingBot-Depth 升级为 LingBot-Depth 2.0。
5. 📊 结果与评估: LingBot-Vision 在 NYU-Depth v2 上取得了所有比较模型中最佳的 RMSE（0.296），优于 7B 的 DINOv3（0.309），在 DAVIS/YouTube-VOS 上达到 70.0/73.5 J&F 匹配蒸馏模型，在 14 个深度补全基准上取得领先结果，其 0.3B 蒸馏学生以约 23 倍更少的参数匹配了 7B DINOv3 的 NYUv2 精度。

UI-MOPD: Multi-Platform On-Policy Distillation for Continual GUI Agent Learning

Published: 2026-07-04

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.04425

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on multi-platform Graphical User Interface (GUI) agent learning, specifically addressing continual learning across heterogeneous desktop and mobile environments through on-policy distillation techniques.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The work builds upon prior research in single-platform GUI agents (e.g., OpenCUA, UI-TARS, GUI-Owl), multi-teacher on-policy distillation methods (e.g., MiMo-V2-Flash, GLM-5, Nemotron-Cascade 2, DeepSeek-V4), and existing cross-platform integration strategies like mixed SFT and model merging. The new idea is introducing MOPD into continual GUI agent learning with platform-conditioned teacher routing to preserve platform-specific behavioral priors.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses two key challenges in cross-platform GUI agent learning: (1) the scarcity of high-quality executable cross-platform interaction trajectories, and (2) behavioral pattern mixing, platform-specific capability degradation, and catastrophic forgetting that occur when naively combining heterogeneous platform signals through mixed SFT or model merging.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes UI-MOPD, a two-stage method: Stage 1 performs SFT on Qwen3-VL-32B-Thinking using the Uni-GUI dataset to create platform-specific desktop and mobile teachers; Stage 2 uses multi-teacher on-policy distillation with platform-conditioned teacher routing, employing the K3 KL estimator, adaptive KL masking, and a structured outcome reward combined with clipped policy optimization (GRPO-based DAPO).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: UI-MOPD achieves 38.2% task success rate on OSWorld and 12.0% on MobileWorld, representing 12.7% and 55.8% relative improvements over the base model. It outperforms integration strategies (Mixed-SFT, Weight Averaging, TIES Merging) and preserves GUI grounding capabilities (e.g., 80.05% on AndroidControl⋆, 52.84% on OSWorld-G) while avoiding the degradation caused by static parameter merging.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文聚焦于多平台图形用户界面（GUI）智能体学习，具体研究如何通过在线策略蒸馏技术实现跨异构桌面和移动环境的持续学习。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该工作建立在单平台GUI智能体（如OpenCUA、UI-TARS、GUI-Owl）、多教师在线策略蒸馏方法（如MiMo-V2-Flash、GLM-5、Nemotron-Cascade 2、DeepSeek-V4）以及混合SFT和模型合并等现有跨平台集成策略的研究基础之上。新思路是首次将MOPD引入GUI智能体持续学习，并采用平台条件化教师路由来保留平台特定的行为先验。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了跨平台GUI智能体学习中的两个关键挑战：（1）高质量可执行跨平台交互轨迹的稀缺性；（2）在通过混合SFT或模型合并天真地组合异构平台信号时出现的行为模式混合、平台特定能力退化和灾难性遗忘问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了UI-MOPD，一种两阶段方法：阶段1使用Uni-GUI数据集对Qwen3-VL-32B-Thinking进行SFT以创建平台特定的桌面和移动教师模型；阶段2使用多教师在线策略蒸馏结合平台条件化教师路由，采用K3 KL估计器、自适应KL掩码，以及结构化结果奖励与裁剪策略优化（基于GRPO的DAPO）相结合的方法。
5. 📊 结果与评估: UI-MOPD在OSWorld上达到38.2%的任务成功率，在MobileWorld上达到12.0%，分别比基线模型提升了12.7%和55.8%。它优于各种集成策略（混合SFT、权重平均、TIES合并），同时保留了GUI定位能力（例如AndroidControl⋆上80.05%，OSWorld-G上52.84%），避免了静态参数合并造成的性能退化。
