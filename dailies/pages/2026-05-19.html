Paper 1

LongLive-2.0: An NVFP4 Parallel Infrastructure for Long Video Generation

Published: 2026-05-17

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18739

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents LongLive-2.0, an algorithm–infrastructure co-design system that uses NVFP4 low-precision computation and sequence parallelism to enable efficient training and real-time inference for long, multi-shot, autoregressive video generation. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** It builds upon prior autoregressive video diffusion methods (e.g., Self-Forcing, Causal-Forcing, LongLive) and newly introduces the first end-to-end NVFP4 training and inference pipeline with Balanced Sequence Parallelism, eliminating the need for complex multi-stage ODE initialization and distribution matching distillation. 3. **❓ Problem:** It solves the prohibitive GPU memory consumption, low computational efficiency, and overly complicated multi-stage training pipelines that prevent scalable, high-quality long video generation. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** The authors propose Balanced SP to co-design the sequence-parallel layout with teacher-forcing masks and chunked VAE encoding, apply full NVFP4 quantization (W4A4 weights/activations and KV cache) across training and inference, and use asynchronous streaming VAE decoding with multi-shot attention sinks for deployment. 5. **📊 Results and Evaluation:** LongLive-2.0 achieves up to 2.15× training speedup and 1.84× inference speedup (45.7 FPS for the 5B model), reduces memory from 35.4 GB to 19.4 GB, and attains strong performance on VBench and VBench-Long benchmarks.

Paper 3

SkillsVote: Lifecycle Governance of Agent Skills from Collection, Recommendation to Evolution

Published: 2026-05-17

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.18401

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses lifecycle governance of reusable Agent Skills for long-horizon LLM agents, spanning collection, recommendation, and evolution.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on prior work in agent experience learning, skill ecosystems, and skill evolution, and introduces a closed-loop framework that couples pre-task agentic library search with post-task subtask-level attribution and evidence-gated updates.
3. ❓ Problem: It solves the problem of noisy, environment-bound raw trajectories and ungoverned skill libraries that suffer from redundancy, uneven quality, and indiscriminate updates causing context pollution.
4. 🛠️ Methods: It profiles a million-scale open-source skill corpus, performs task-conditioned agentic search before execution, and after execution decomposes trajectories into skill-linked subtasks for outcome attribution, allowing only successful reusable discoveries to update the library via admissibility, aggregation, and routing gates.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Evaluation on Terminal-Bench 2.0 and SWE-Bench Pro shows offline evolution improves GPT-5.2 by up to 7.9 percentage points and online evolution improves SWE-Bench Pro by up to 2.6 percentage points, demonstrating that governed skill libraries can improve frozen agents without model updates.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文研究面向长程LLM智能体的可复用Agent Skill的全生命周期治理，涵盖收集、推荐与演化。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于智能体经验学习、技能生态与技能演化的已有研究，提出将执行前agentic库搜索与执行后子任务级归因及证据门控更新相结合的闭环框架。
3. ❓ 问题: 解决原始轨迹嘈杂且强绑定环境、开放技能库冗余且质量参差，以及无差别更新导致上下文污染的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 对百万级开源技能语料进行环境需求、质量与可验证性画像，执行前进行任务条件的agentic库搜索，执行后将轨迹分解为技能关联子任务进行结果归因，仅让成功的可复用发现通过可接受性、聚合与路由门控更新技能库。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在Terminal-Bench 2.0和SWE-Bench Pro上的评估表明，离线演化使GPT-5.2提升最高7.9个百分点，在线演化使SWE-Bench Pro提升最高2.6个百分点，证明受治理的技能库可在不更新模型的情况下提升冻结智能体性能。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with the Lance paper, since its lightweight unified multimodal design offers the clearest big-picture view of image and video generation and naturally frames the efficiency challenges that LongLive-2.0 tackles. These two papers form a coherent line from multimodal architecture to systems optimization for long-video inference, while the agent-skills governance paper complements them by addressing scalable, reusable capability management in complex AI systems.
建议从 Lance 读起，其轻量统一的多模态设计为图像与视频生成提供了最清晰的全景视角，也能自然引出 LongLive-2.0 所针对的长视频推理效率问题。这两篇论文串联起从多模态架构到长视频系统优化的完整脉络，而关于 Agent Skill 生命周期治理的论文则通过可复用能力的规模化管控提供了互补视角。
