Published: 2025-09-16

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2509.13309

1. üìò Topic and Domain: Development of an advanced AI research agent (WebResearcher) that can autonomously discover and synthesize knowledge from external sources through web search and tool use.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on previous deep-research systems like OpenAI's Deep Research and Google's Gemini Deep Research, but introduces a novel iterative paradigm instead of the traditional mono-contextual approach for information accumulation.
