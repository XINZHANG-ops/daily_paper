Paper 1

Video Generation Models are General-Purpose Vision Learners

Published: 2026-07-09

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.09024

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper belongs to computer vision and deep learning, specifically focusing on general-purpose vision foundation models and video generation-based pre-training for multi-task perception.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on prior work in perception foundation models (e.g., Segment Anything, Depth Anything), visual representation learning (e.g., MAE, DINO, CLIP), and repurposing diffusion models for perception (e.g., Marigold, Diception). Its new idea is proposing that large-scale text-to-video generation can serve as a universal pre-training paradigm for generalist visual perception, unifying multiple tasks within a single feed-forward architecture.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of a unified, general-purpose vision foundation model analogous to LLMs in NLP, aiming to overcome the limitations of task-specific architectures and to find a universal pre-training objective that internalizes spatio-temporal priors, vision-language alignment, and scalability.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes GenCeption, which repurposes a pre-trained text-to-video diffusion model (WAN 2.1) into a single-step feed-forward perception model. It uses Rectified Flow formulation with timestep fixed to 0, unifies dense tasks via 3-channel RGB representation, handles sparse tasks (e.g., keypoints) through learnable tokens, applies a unified ℓ₂ loss, and trains on synthetic human-centric video data generated with Blender.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: GenCeption achieves state-of-the-art or highly competitive performance across depth estimation, surface normal estimation, camera pose estimation, foreground segmentation, expression-referring segmentation, and 3D keypoint prediction, often matching or surpassing specialized models (e.g., DepthAnything V3, D4RT, VGGT-Ω, SAM 3). It outperforms alternative pre-training paradigms (V-JEPA, VideoMAE V2) with 7× to 500× less training data, demonstrates preliminary scaling properties, and exhibits emergent generalization from synthetic to real-world data and to out-of-distribution categories like animals and robots.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文属于计算机视觉与深度学习领域，聚焦于通用视觉基础模型以及基于视频生成的预训练方法在多任务感知中的应用。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文建立在感知基础模型（如 Segment Anything、Depth Anything）、视觉表征学习（如 MAE、DINO、CLIP）以及扩散模型在感知任务中的复用（如 Marigold、Diception）等已有工作之上。其新思路在于提出大规模文本到视频生成可作为通用视觉感知的统一预训练范式，从而在单一前馈架构中实现多任务统一。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文旨在解决当前计算机视觉领域缺乏类似 NLP 中大语言模型那样的统一通用基础模型的问题，克服任务专属架构的局限，并寻找一种能同时内化时空先验、视觉-语言对齐和可扩展性的通用预训练目标。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了 GenCeption，将预训练的文本到视频扩散模型（WAN 2.1）重构为单步前馈感知模型。该方法采用 Rectified Flow 公式并将时间步固定为 0，通过 3 通道 RGB 表示统一密集任务，利用可学习 token 处理稀疏任务（如关键点），采用统一的 ℓ₂ 损失函数，并主要基于 Blender 生成的合成人类视频数据进行训练。
5. 📊 结果与评估: GenCeption 在深度估计、法向量估计、相机位姿估计、前景分割、引用表达式分割以及 3D 关键点预测等任务上取得了领先或具有强竞争力的性能，常匹配或超越专门模型（如 DepthAnything V3、D4RT、VGGT-Ω、SAM 3）。在相同数据量下，其性能优于 V-JEPA 和 VideoMAE V2 等替代预训练范式，并在数据量少 7× 至 500× 的情况下达到可比效果，展现出初步的规模化特性以及对真实数据和动物、机器人等分布外类别的涌现泛化能力。

Paper 2

Vidu S1: A Real-Time Interactive Video Generation Model

Published: 2026-07-02

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.03118

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on real-time interactive video generation with voice control of digital characters, situated in the domain of computer vision, multimodal generative AI, and diffusion-based video synthesis.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Built upon prior video generation models (Sora, Veo, Wan, Seedance) and autoregressive approaches (Diffusion Forcing, Self Forcing), the paper introduces the new idea of combining speech-guided real-time interaction, infinite-length stable streaming generation, and efficient inference infrastructure (TurboDiffusion and TurboServe) into a unified practical system.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the limitations of existing offline, one-shot video generation paradigms by tackling the lack of real-time user interaction, absence of speech as a direct control signal, error accumulation in long-horizon generation, and inefficient inference infrastructure for practical deployment.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper employs a three-stage training pipeline (bidirectional teacher, causal teacher with hybrid Teacher Forcing and Diffusion Forcing, and DMD with PCM regularization), a progressive data filtering pipeline, sliding-window streaming inference with RoPE Repositioning and TwinCache, and a hardware-software co-designed acceleration stack with multi-GPU parallelism.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Vidu S1 achieves the best performance on Vidu-StreamBench and HDTF benchmarks (CSIM 0.9192, Sync-D 7.847, DOVER 0.5660), generates 540p video at 42 FPS on consumer GPUs exceeding real-time thresholds, and attains a 100% preference rate on subject controllability against HeyGen and LemonSlice in human evaluations.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于语音控制的数字角色实时交互式视频生成，属于计算机视觉、多模态生成式AI和基于扩散模型的视频合成领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于此前的视频生成模型（Sora、Veo、Wan、Seedance）以及自回归方法（Diffusion Forcing、Self Forcing），论文提出了将语音引导的实时交互、无限长度稳定流式生成以及高效推理基础设施（TurboDiffusion和TurboServe）融合为统一实用系统的新思路。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文针对现有离线、一次性视频生成范式的局限性，解决了缺乏实时用户交互、缺少语音作为直接控制信号、长程生成中的误差累积以及实用部署中推理基础设施效率低下等问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文采用了三阶段训练流程（双向教师模型、结合Teacher Forcing和Diffusion Forcing的因果教师模型、以及带PCM正则化的DMD蒸馏）、渐进式数据过滤流水线、结合RoPE Repositioning和TwinCache的滑动窗口流式推理，以及软硬件协同设计的加速堆栈与多GPU并行策略。
5. 📊 结果与评估: Vidu S1在Vidu-StreamBench和HDTF基准测试中取得最佳性能（CSIM 0.9192、Sync-D 7.847、DOVER 0.5660），在消费级GPU上以42 FPS生成540p视频超越实时阈值，并在人类评估中对HeyGen和LemonSlice的主体可控性获得100%的偏好率。

Paper 3

Long-Horizon-Terminal-Bench: Testing the Limits of Agents on Long-Horizon Terminal Tasks with Dense Reward-Based Grading

Published: 2026-07-08

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.08964

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on benchmarking AI agents' long-horizon task execution capabilities in terminal/command-line environments, spanning domains such as software engineering, scientific computing, multimodal analysis, and interactive games.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Built upon Terminal-Bench's containerized task formulation and inspired by APEX-Agents' professional workflows, the paper introduces the new idea of subtask-based dense reward grading to provide partial credit and measure intermediate progress, rather than relying solely on binary pass/fail outcomes used in prior benchmarks like SWE-Bench and Terminal-Bench.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses two key gaps in existing agent benchmarks: (1) tasks are typically too short (minutes to an hour), failing to reflect real long-horizon workflows requiring hundreds of steps, and (2) outcome-only grading produces sparse reward signals that cannot distinguish between agents that make partial progress and those that fail from the start.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors constructed 46 long-horizon terminal tasks across nine categories, each decomposed into graded subtasks evaluated by deterministic, environment-grounded verifiers with binary, continuous, and episode-aggregating scoring schemes. They evaluated 15 frontier LLMs using the Harbor framework with the Terminus-2 agent harness, measuring pass@1 at multiple reward thresholds, mean reward, token usage, episodes, time, and cost.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The strongest model (GPT-5.5) achieved only 15.2% pass rate at the 0.95 reward threshold and 10.9% at 1.0, with the mean pass rate across all 15 models being just 4.3%. Tasks required an average of 231 episodes, 9.9M tokens, and 85.3 minutes per run, with 79% of unresolved runs ending in timeouts, revealing that long-horizon completion—not local reasoning—is the primary bottleneck.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于在终端/命令行环境中对AI智能体长时程任务执行能力进行基准测试，涵盖软件工程、科学计算、多模态分析和交互式游戏等多个领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于Terminal-Bench的容器化任务框架，并受APEX-Agents专业工作流启发，论文提出了基于子任务的密集奖励评分新思路，以提供部分得分并衡量中间进度，而非仅依赖SWE-Bench和Terminal-Bench等先前基准使用的二元通过/失败结果。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有智能体基准测试中的两个关键缺陷：（1）任务通常过短（几分钟到一小时），无法反映需要数百步骤的真实长时程工作流；（2）仅看结果的评分方式产生稀疏奖励信号，无法区分取得部分进展的智能体和从一开始就失败的智能体。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者构建了横跨九个类别的46个长时程终端任务，每个任务被分解为通过确定性、基于环境验证器评分的子任务，采用二元、连续和回合聚合三种评分方案。他们使用Harbor框架配合Terminus-2智能体框架评估了15个前沿大语言模型，测量了多个奖励阈值下的pass@1、平均奖励、token使用量、回合数、时间和成本。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 表现最强的模型（GPT-5.5）在0.95奖励阈值下仅达到15.2%的通过率，在1.0阈值下为10.9%，所有15个模型的平均通过率仅为4.3%。任务平均需要231个回合、990万tokens和85.3分钟，79%未解决的运行因超时结束，揭示出长时程完成能力——而非局部推理——是主要瓶颈。
