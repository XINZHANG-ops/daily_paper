SpatialEvo: Self-Evolving Spatial Intelligence via Deterministic Geometric Environments

Published: 2026-04-14

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14144

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses 3D spatial reasoning in vision-language models (VLMs) for embodied intelligence, focusing on how models can continuously improve spatial understanding without relying on costly geometric annotations.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on self-evolving VLMs and spatial reasoning research. The new idea is the Deterministic Geometric Environment (DGE) that exploits the property that spatial ground truth is a deterministic consequence of geometry computable from point clouds and camera poses, replacing model consensus with objective physical feedback.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing self-evolving methods rely on model voting to construct pseudo-labels, which causes training to reinforce rather than correct the model's own geometric errors; this paper leverages the fact that 3D spatial reasoning ground truth can be computed exactly without any model involvement.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method uses DGE with 16 spatial reasoning task categories and geometric validation rules to transform 3D scenes into zero-noise interactive oracles; a single policy model co-evolves as Questioner and Solver under GRPO with adaptive task scheduling, trained entirely via online reinforcement learning without supervised fine-tuning.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: SpatialEvo achieves the highest average score at both 3B (51.1) and 7B (54.7) scales across nine benchmarks, with VSI-Bench reaching 46.1 (7B) and consistent gains on spatial reasoning tasks while maintaining general visual understanding; ablation confirms DGE physical grounding as the largest contributor.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文涉及视觉语言模型（VLM）在三维空间推理中的能力，聚焦于如何在不依赖昂贵几何标注的情况下持续提升空间理解能力，服务于具身智能。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于自进化VLM和空间推理研究。新思路是提出确定性几何环境（DGE），利用三维空间推理中真值可从点云和相机姿态精确计算这一特性，用客观物理反馈替代模型共识。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有自进化方法依赖模型投票构建伪标签，导致训练反而强化而非纠正模型自身的几何错误；该论文利用三维空间推理的真值可从几何中精确计算这一特性来解决该问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 使用DGE涵盖16类空间推理任务及几何验证规则，将三维场景转化为零噪声交互式预言机；单一策略模型在GRPO框架下同时承担提问者和求解者角色，通过自适应任务调度进行纯在线强化学习训练，无监督微调阶段。
5. 📊 结果与评估: SpatialEvo在9个基准测试中于3B（51.1）和7B（54.7）规模均取得最高平均分，VSI-Bench达46.1（7B），空间推理任务持续提升且保持通用视觉理解能力；消融实验证实DGE物理接地是最大贡献来源。

The Past Is Not Past: Memory-Enhanced Dynamic Reward Shaping

Published: 2026-04-12

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.11297

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses reinforcement learning (RL) for large language models, specifically focusing on improving mathematical reasoning capabilities through dynamic reward shaping.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior work including GRPO, DAPO, and entropy regularization methods; introduces MEDS, a novel memory-enhanced framework that captures historical error patterns via layer-wise logits clustering and dynamically penalizes recurrent failure modes.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the problem of reduced sampling diversity in on-policy RL optimization, where the policy collapses into a narrow "stable error basin" and repeatedly generates similar erroneous behaviors without effectively exploring alternatives.
4. 🛠️ Methods: MEDS extracts logic feature vectors from layer-wise logits of the final answer token, maintains per-prompt error memory updated online, applies HDBSCAN clustering to group similar error patterns, and adjusts rewards by subtracting penalties proportional to cluster size to encourage broader exploration.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Across three models (Qwen3-1.7B, Qwen2.5-Math-7B, Qwen3-8B) on five benchmarks, MEDS consistently outperforms baselines, achieving up to 4.13 pass@1 and 4.37 pass@128 improvements; analysis shows increased behavioral diversity and strong correlation between clustering quality and downstream performance.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文主题是大语言模型的强化学习，具体聚焦于通过动态奖励塑形提升数学推理能力。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于GRPO、DAPO和熵正则化等先前工作；提出MEDS这一新颖的记忆增强框架，通过层级logits聚类捕获历史错误模式，并动态惩罚重复失败模式。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了在线策略强化学习优化中采样多样性下降的问题，即策略陷入狭窄的"稳定误差盆地"，反复生成相似的错误行为，无法有效探索替代方案。
4. 🛠️ 方法: MEDS从最终答案token的层级logits中提取逻辑特征向量，维护在线更新的每个提示错误记忆，使用HDBSCAN聚类将相似错误模式分组，并根据聚类大小按比例扣除惩罚来调整奖励，以鼓励更广泛的探索。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在三个模型（Qwen3-1.7B、Qwen2.5-Math-7B、Qwen3-8B）和五个基准测试中，MEDS始终优于基线方法，获得最高4.13 pass@1和4.37 pass@128的提升；分析表明行为多样性增加，且聚类质量与下游性能之间存在强相关性。
