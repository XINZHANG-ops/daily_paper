Paper 1

GLM-5V-Turbo: Toward a Native Foundation Model for Multimodal Agents

Published: 2026-04-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.26752

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents GLM-5V-Turbo, a native multimodal foundation model for AI agents, integrating vision and language capabilities for perception, reasoning, planning, and execution across diverse modalities like images, videos, webpages, and GUIs.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building upon GLM-4.6V, SigLIP2/DINOv3 vision encoders, and multi-token prediction, the paper introduces: (1) CogViT, a parameter-efficient vision encoder with two-stage pretraining; (2) Multimodal Multi-Token Prediction (MMTP) using <|image|> placeholder tokens; (3) Joint RL optimization across 30+ task categories; and (4) hierarchical agent training with unified multimodal RL infrastructure.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the challenge of building native multimodal agents where perception is a core reasoning component rather than an auxiliary interface, enabling effective integration of heterogeneous visual contexts (images, videos, GUIs) into unified perception–planning–execution loops.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method involves: (1) CogViT with distillation-based masked image modeling followed by contrastive image-text pretraining; (2) MMTP design using shared <|image|> tokens for efficient multimodal multi-token prediction; (3) Deep vision-language integration in pretraining/SFT with emphasis on multimodal coding data; (4) Multi-task RL over 30+ categories spanning perception, reasoning, and agentic capabilities; and (5) Optimized infrastructure with unified task abstraction, asynchronous pipeline, and topology-aware partitioning.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: GLM-5V-Turbo achieves 94.8 on Design2Code (outperforming Claude Opus 4.6), 75.7 on AndroidWorld, 62.3 on OSWorld, 30.7 on ImageMining, 51.9 on BrowseComp-VL, 87.0/80.7 on PinchBench, while preserving strong text-only coding capability (22.8 on CC-Backend), demonstrating balanced multimodal agentic performance.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文介绍了GLM-5V-Turbo，一个面向AI智能体的原生多模态基础模型，整合视觉与语言能力，用于跨图像、视频、网页和GUI等多模态的感知、推理、规划和执行。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于GLM-4.6V、SigLIP2/DINOv3视觉编码器和多token预测，论文提出：(1) CogViT参数高效视觉编码器及两阶段预训练；(2) 使用<|image|>占位符的多模态多token预测(MMTP)；(3) 跨30+任务类别的联合强化学习；(4) 分层智能体训练与统一多模态RL基础设施。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决的核心问题是如何构建原生多模态智能体，使感知成为推理的核心组件而非辅助接口，从而将异构视觉上下文（图像、视频、GUI）有效整合进统一的感知-规划-执行循环。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包括：(1) CogViT采用蒸馏掩码图像建模后接对比图像-文本预训练；(2) MMTP设计使用共享<|image|> token实现高效多模态多token预测；(3) 深度视觉-语言整合于预训练/SFT，强调多模态编码数据；(4) 跨30+类别的多任务RL，覆盖感知、推理和智能体能力；(5) 优化基础设施含统一任务抽象、异步流水线和拓扑感知划分。
5. 📊 结果与评估: GLM-5V-Turbo在Design2Code上达到94.8（超越Claude Opus 4.6），AndroidWorld 75.7，OSWorld 62.3，ImageMining 30.7，BrowseComp-VL 51.9，PinchBench 87.0/80.7，同时保持强文本编码能力（CC-Backend 22.8），展现均衡的多模态智能体性能。

Paper 2

Turning the TIDE: Cross-Architecture Distillation for Diffusion Large Language Models

Published: 2026-04-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.26951

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses knowledge distillation for diffusion large language models (dLLMs), focusing on cross-architecture model compression in the field of natural language generation.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: While existing dLLM distillation methods (CDLM, DDD, LSD, SDTT) only compress inference steps within the same architecture, this paper introduces TIDE, the first framework for cross-architecture distillation where teachers and students differ in architecture, attention mechanism, and tokenizer.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves three fundamental challenges in cross-architecture dLLM distillation: temporal inconsistency (varying teacher reliability across diffusion timesteps), context scarcity under heavy masking, and cross-tokenizer vocabulary misalignment.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The TIDE framework comprises three modular components: TIDAL (dual-axis interpolation across training progress and diffusion timesteps), COMPDEMO (complementary mask splitting to enrich teacher context), and Reverse CALM (bounded gradient chunk-level alignment for cross-tokenizer transfer).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Distilling 8B/16B teachers into a 0.6B student yields +1.53 average improvement across 8 benchmarks; HumanEval achieves 48.78 vs 32.3 AR baseline; 22× memory reduction (1.4GB vs 31.3GB) and 5× inference speedup (41.0 vs 8.2 tokens/s).
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文探讨扩散大语言模型（dLLMs）的知识蒸馏，聚焦于自然语言生成领域中跨架构模型压缩的方向。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 现有dLLM蒸馏方法（CDLM、DDD、LSD、SDTT）仅在同一架构内压缩推理步骤；本文提出TIDE，首个跨架构蒸馏框架，支持教师与学生在架构、注意机制和分词器上存在差异。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了跨架构dLLM蒸馏的三个核心挑战：时序不一致性（教师在不同扩散时间步的可靠性变化）、重掩码下的上下文稀缺、以及跨分词器词汇表不对齐问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: TIDE框架包含三个模块化组件：TIDAL（训练进度与扩散时间步的双轴插值调度）、COMPDEMO（互补掩码分割以丰富教师上下文）、以及Reverse CALM（用于跨分词器迁移的有界梯度分块级对齐）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 将8B/16B教师模型蒸馏至0.6B学生模型，在8个基准测试中平均提升+1.53分；HumanEval达到48.78分对比AR基线的32.3分；内存减少22倍（1.4GB vs 31.3GB），推理速度提升5倍（41.0 vs 8.2 tokens/s）。

Paper 3

ClawGym: A Scalable Framework for Building Effective Claw Agents

Published: 2026-04-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.26904

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents ClawGym, a scalable data-centric framework for developing Claw-style personal AI agents that interact with local workspaces, tools, and files through multi-step workflows.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on OpenClaw and existing agent training/evaluation studies, it introduces a dual-route data synthesis strategy combining persona-driven top-down and skill-grounded bottom-up approaches, along with hybrid code-based and rubric-based verification mechanisms.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of systematic frameworks for generating large-scale, verifiable Claw-style training data and integrating it with agent training and reliable diagnostic evaluation.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The framework consists of three components: ClawGym-SynData (13.5K synthesized tasks with mock workspaces), ClawGym-Agents (trained via SFT on black-box rollout trajectories plus sandbox-parallel RL), and ClawGym-Bench (200-instance benchmark with difficulty-aware filtering and human-LLM review).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Training on synthesized data yields substantial improvements, with Qwen3-8B improving 38.90% on PinchBench and 43.46% on ClawGym-Bench, while ClawGym-30A3B achieves competitive performance (86.00 on PinchBench) by narrowing the gap between compact and large-scale models.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文提出了ClawGym，一个可扩展的数据驱动框架，用于开发爪型（Claw）个人AI智能体，这类智能体通过多步工作流与本地工作空间、工具和文件进行交互。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于OpenClaw及现有的智能体训练与评估研究，该论文引入了双路径数据合成策略，结合了人物驱动自顶向下与技能驱动自底向上的方法，并提出了混合代码验证与量规验证机制。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了缺乏系统性框架来生成大规模、可验证的爪型训练数据，并将其与智能体训练和可靠诊断评估相集成的难题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该框架由三部分组成：ClawGym-SynData（包含模拟工作空间的13.5K合成任务）、ClawGym-Agents（通过黑箱展开轨迹的监督微调训练并结合沙箱并行强化学习）以及ClawGym-Bench（包含200个实例的基准测试，采用难度感知过滤与人类-LLM联合审查）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在合成数据上训练带来了显著的性能提升，Qwen3-8B在PinchBench上提升38.90%，在ClawGym-Bench上提升43.46%；ClawGym-30A3B在PinchBench上达到86.00分，显示出该框架能有效缩小紧凑模型与大规模模型之间的差距。
