Published: 2026-02-04

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2602.04804

2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on existing token compression methods like OmniZip and DyCoke but proposes a modality-asymmetric approach where video tokens guide audio token selection, unlike previous symmetric methods.
