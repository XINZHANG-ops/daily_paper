Paper 1

Geometric Action Model for Robot Policy Learning

Published: 2026-06-14

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.17046

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on language-conditioned robot manipulation policy learning, situated at the intersection of robotics, geometric computer vision, and foundation models for embodied AI.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on Vision-Language-Action models (VLAs), World Action Models (WAMs), and Geometric Foundation Models (GFMs), and proposes the new idea of repurposing a pretrained GFM as a unified shared backbone that jointly performs perception, temporal world modeling, and action decoding in a single token space.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of explicit 3D geometric reasoning in existing 2D-based robot policies, which limits generalization and robustness to environmental perturbations such as camera viewpoint changes.
4. 🛠️ Methods: GAM splits a pretrained GFM at an intermediate layer, uses shallow layers as an observation encoder, inserts a causal transformer as a future predictor conditioned on language, proprioception, and action history, and routes predicted future tokens through the deep GFM blocks for joint future-geometry and action decoding, trained with action, feature, and depth losses.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: GAM achieves 97.6% success on LIBERO and 85.5% on LIBERO-Plus (a 9.7% gain in camera-perturbation robustness over prior best), runs 55× faster than Cosmos-Policy at 6.9 ms/145 Hz with only 1.4B parameters, and also outperforms baselines on real-world manipulation tasks and RoboCasa-Kitchen.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文聚焦于语言条件下的机器人操作策略学习，处于机器人学、几何计算机视觉与具身智能基础模型的交叉领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于视觉-语言-动作模型（VLA）、世界动作模型（WAM）以及几何基础模型（GFM），提出新思路：将被预训练的几何基础模型重新用作统一共享主干，在单一 token 空间中同时实现感知、时序世界建模与动作解码。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有基于 2D 的机器人策略缺乏显式 3D 几何推理能力的问题，该局限导致策略在相机视角变化等环境扰动下的泛化性与鲁棒性不足。
4. 🛠️ 方法: GAM 在预训练 GFM 的中间层将其拆分，将浅层作为观测编码器，插入以语言、本体感知与历史动作条件化的因果 Transformer 作为未来预测器，并将预测的未来 token 送入深层 GFM 模块进行几何与动作联合解码，通过动作、特征与深度三项损失进行端到端训练。
5. 📊 结果与评估: GAM 在 LIBERO 上达到 97.6% 成功率，在 LIBERO-Plus 上达到 85.5%（相机扰动设置较此前最优提升 9.7%），仅需 1.4B 参数即以 6.9 ms（145 Hz）运行、相比 Cosmos-Policy 提速约 55 倍，并在真实机器人任务与 RoboCasa-Kitchen 上均优于基线。

DreamX-World 1.0: A General-Purpose Interactive World Model

Published: 2026-06-14

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.16993

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents an interactive text/image-to-video world model for controllable long-horizon generation, spanning photorealistic, game-style, and stylized visual domains, within computer vision and generative AI.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on Wan2.2-TI2V, PRoPE, causal forcing, DMD distillation, and self-forcing autoregressive video generation, introducing novel components like E-PRoPE (lightweight projective positional encoding on downsampled tokens), Memory-Conditioned Scene Persistence with geometry-based retrieval, residual recycling, Event Instruction Tuning for composable multi-entity events, and RL-based post-distillation alignment.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses three coupled challenges: translating prescribed camera trajectories into consistent viewpoint changes across heterogeneous domains, preserving scene content and identity during long-horizon revisits (mitigating style/color drift in autoregressive generation), and achieving real-time streaming latency without sacrificing quality or controllability.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method combines a multi-source data engine (UE5 with ground-truth camera poses, real-world videos with recovered geometry, and game data), a progressive training pipeline (camera-aware E-PRoPE, memory conditioning with error injection, event instruction tuning, DMD-forcing distillation, and conservative DiffusionNFT RL alignment), and inference optimizations (INT8/FP8 quantization, sequence parallelism, pruned VAE decoding, TeaCache-style residual reuse, and asynchronous pipeline parallelism).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: On 5-second basic evaluation, DreamX-World-1.0-5B achieves a camera-control score of 73.75 and overall score of 84.76, surpassing HY-WorldPlay 1.5 (80.79) and LingBot-World (80.45); on 30-second long-horizon rollouts, it leads with 70.41 overall; in memory revisit consistency it tops pixel, perceptual, semantic, and place-recognition metrics; in human preference it wins 57.5%–61.9% overall; and the system runs up to 16 FPS on 8 RTX 5090 GPUs.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文提出了一个面向可控长时序生成的通用交互式文本/图像到视频世界模型，涵盖写实、游戏和风格化视觉领域，属于计算机视觉与生成式AI方向。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于Wan2.2-TI2V、PRoPE、因果强制、DMD蒸馏和自回归视频生成等研究，提出E-PRoPE（在降采样token上做轻量投影位置编码）、基于几何检索的记忆条件场景持久化、残差回收、可组合多实体事件指令调优以及蒸馏后RL对齐等新思路。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决三个核心难题：在异构领域中将相机轨迹转化为一致视角变化、长时序自回归生成中的场景一致性与身份漂移（风格/颜色漂移），以及在不损失质量与可控性的前提下实现实时流式交互的低延迟。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包括多源数据引擎（带精确相机位姿的UE5、恢复几何的真实视频和游戏数据）、渐进式训练管线（E-PRoPE相机控制、误差注入的记忆条件、事件指令调优、DMD-forcing蒸馏、保守的DiffusionNFT RL对齐）以及推理加速（INT8/FP8量化、序列并行、剪枝VAE解码、TeaCache残差复用和异步流水线并行）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在5秒基础评测中，DreamX-World-1.0-5B相机控制得分为73.75、总分84.76，超过HY-WorldPlay 1.5（80.79）和LingBot-World（80.45）；在30秒长时序评测中以70.41领先；在记忆回访一致性中像素、感知、语义和位置识别指标均最优；人工偏好中总体胜率57.5%–61.9%；系统可在8张RTX 5090 GPU上达到最高16 FPS。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with Paper 1 (GAM) if your interest lies in robotics, or Paper 2 (DreamX-World) if generative video/world modeling is your focus—they are the most tightly connected, both advancing world-model architectures (causal transformers, geometric/spatial reasoning, long-horizon consistency) but in different modalities. Paper 3 (VibeThinker-3B) is more independent, targeting efficient reasoning in small LMs, but its test-time scaling ideas could complement agentic or planning layers in either world model.
如果你的兴趣在机器人方向，建议先读论文1（GAM）；如果关注生成式视频与世界模型，则先读论文2（DreamX-World）——这两篇联系最紧密，都围绕世界模型架构（因果 Transformer、几何/空间推理、长时一致性）展开，只是模态不同。论文3（VibeThinker-3B）相对独立，聚焦小模型的高效推理，但其测试时扩展思想未来有望整合到上述世界模型的规划或智能体决策中。
