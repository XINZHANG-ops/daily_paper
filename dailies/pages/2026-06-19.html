Paper 1

S-Agent: Spatial Tool-Use Elicits Reasoning for Spatial Intelligence

Published: 2026-06-17

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.20515

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on spatial intelligence in vision-language models (VLMs), specifically agentic AI for continuous multi-view image and video spatial reasoning in computer vision.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on prior agentic VLM frameworks (VADAR, SpaceTools) and spatial-specialized models (Cambrian-S, SenseNova-SI), the paper introduces the new idea of reformulating spatial reasoning as iterative spatio-temporal evidence accumulation rather than single-shot frame-level prediction.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the semantic-to-geometric gap in existing VLMs—namely, their inability to maintain persistent object states, integrate evidence across viewpoints and time, and perform stateful reasoning over continuous multi-view or video inputs beyond static, stateless inference.
4. 🛠️ Methods: S-Agent uses a VLM as a semantic planner that invokes a three-level hierarchy of spatial tools/experts (2D visual grounding, 2D-to-3D geometric lifting via Depth-Anything-3, and specialized spatial experts for counting, measurement, orientation, and relations), together with a dual-memory system (Scene Memory for entity tracking and Agent Memory for reasoning history); trajectories are distilled into the S-300K dataset to fine-tune a compact S-Agent-8B via supervised learning.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: S-Agent achieves state-of-the-art zero-shot performance with 46.4% on MMSI-Bench (+1.2% over Gemini 3 Pro, +4.5% over GPT-5.4), 60.0% on ViewSpatial-Bench, and 58.8 on ReVSI; the fine-tuned S-Agent-8B improves Qwen3-VL-8B by 10.5% on MMSI-Bench (31.1%→41.6%) and rivals GPT-5.4/Gemini 3 Pro, with ablations confirming the necessity of Level-3 experts and dual memory.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于视觉语言模型（VLM）的空间智能，具体属于计算机视觉领域中面向连续多视图图像与视频空间推理的智能体AI方向。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有智能体VLM框架（VADAR、SpaceTools）及空间专用模型（Cambrian-S、SenseNova-SI）的研究基础，提出将空间推理重新定义为时空证据累积的迭代过程，而非单次帧级预测的新思路。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文旨在解决现有VLM的语义-几何鸿沟问题，即无法跨视点和时间维持持久对象状态、整合证据，难以对连续多视图或视频输入进行有状态的推理，摆脱静态、无状态的推理局限。
4. 🛠️ 方法: S-Agent采用VLM作为语义规划器，调用三级分层空间工具与专家（2D视觉定位、通过Depth-Anything-3实现的2D到3D几何提升，以及用于计数、测量、朝向和关系的空间专家），配合双记忆系统（用于实体追踪的场景记忆与用于推理历史的智能体记忆）；其轨迹被蒸馏为S-300K数据集，用于监督微调紧凑型S-Agent-8B。
5. 📊 结果与评估: S-Agent在零样本下取得领先性能，在MMSI-Bench上达到46.4%（较Gemini 3 Pro提升1.2%，较GPT-5.4提升4.5%），在ViewSpatial-Bench上为60.0%，在ReVSI上为58.8；微调后的S-Agent-8B使Qwen3-VL-8B在MMSI-Bench上提升10.5%（31.1%→41.6%），可与GPT-5.4和Gemini 3 Pro媲美，消融实验验证了三级专家和双记忆机制的必要性。

Paper 2

MolmoMotion: Forecasting Point Trajectories in 3D with Language Instruction

Published: 2026-06-16

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.18558

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on goal-conditioned 3D point motion forecasting in computer vision and AI, with applications in robotics planning and video generation.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on classical motion perception work (Gibson, Ullman, Lucas-Kanade optical flow) and recent advances in point tracking (CoTracker, AllTracker) and 3D reconstruction (ViPE), as well as motion prediction methods using pixels, parametric 3D models, or 2D point trajectories. The new idea is using object-attached 3D points in world coordinates as a class-agnostic, view-stable, and compact representation for general motion forecasting.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses how to accurately forecast the future 3D trajectories of points on objects given a short visual history, initial query points, and a natural language action description—overcoming limitations of existing representations that are either category-specific, view-dependent, or lack physical grounding.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper introduces a full stack including: (1) MolmoMotion-1M, a 1.16M-video dataset with an automatic annotation pipeline using LLMs, MolmoPoint, SAM3, AllTracker, and ViPE; (2) PointMotionBench, a human-verified benchmark across 111 object categories; and (3) MolmoMotion, a model built on the Molmo2 backbone with two variants—an autoregressive coordinate predictor and a flow-matching trajectory generator.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: MolmoMotion significantly outperforms all baselines on PointMotionBench across ADE, FDE, and PWT metrics; in robotics, it improves pick-and-place success rate from 56.0% to 76.3% on MolmoSpaces and reduces trajectory error on DROID; in video generation, its predicted trajectories guide DaS to outperform much larger models like Wan2.2-I2V-A14B on most metrics.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于计算机视觉和人工智能领域中以目标为条件的3D点运动预测，并应用于机器人规划和视频生成。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于经典运动感知研究（Gibson、Ullman、Lucas-Kanade光流法）以及近期在点跟踪（CoTracker、AllTracker）和3D重建（ViPE）方面的进展，还有使用像素、参数化3D模型或2D点轨迹的运动预测方法。新思路是采用世界坐标系中附着于物体的3D点作为一种类别无关、视角稳定且紧凑的表示方式，用于通用运动预测。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了如何根据简短视觉历史、初始查询点和自然语言动作描述准确预测物体上点的未来3D轨迹的问题，克服了现有表示方法的局限性，这些方法要么类别特定、依赖视角，要么缺乏物理基础。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文引入了一套完整的方法栈：(1) MolmoMotion-1M，一个包含116万个视频的数据集，使用LLM、MolmoPoint、SAM3、AllTracker和ViPE构建自动标注流程；(2) PointMotionBench，涵盖111个物体类别的人工验证基准；(3) MolmoMotion，基于Molmo2骨干网络的模型，包含两种变体——自回归坐标预测器和流匹配轨迹生成器。
5. 📊 结果与评估: MolmoMotion在PointMotionBench上的ADE、FDE和PWT指标上显著优于所有基线方法；在机器人任务中，将MolmoSpaces上的抓放成功率从56.0%提升至76.3%，并在DROID上降低了轨迹误差；在视频生成中，其预测的轨迹可引导DaS在大多数指标上超越Wan2.2-I2V-A14B等更大的模型。

Moebius: 0.2B Lightweight Image Inpainting Framework with 10B-Level Performance

Published: 2026-06-16

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.19195

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on image inpainting within computer vision and deep learning, specifically proposing a lightweight framework based on latent diffusion models.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds upon latent diffusion models (LDM), efficient architectures (DWConv, linear attention, Mix-FFN), and knowledge distillation. Its new ideas include the Local-λMix Interaction (LλMI) block with Local-λ and Interactive-λ modules that summarize contexts into fixed-size linear matrices, combined with an adaptive multi-granularity latent distillation strategy.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the prohibitive computational cost and deployment difficulty of 10B-level industrial inpainting models (e.g., FLUX.1-Fill-Dev), while naive compression causes severe representation bottlenecks.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The methods include: (1) the LλMI block integrating Local-λ (self-attention equivalent), Interactive-λ (cross-attention equivalent for LCG embeddings), and Mix-FFN; (2) Depthwise Residual Blocks for spatial feature extraction; and (3) an adaptive multi-granularity distillation strategy with coarse/fine-grained losses and latent perceptual loss, balanced via gradient-norm-based adaptive weights.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Moebius achieves comparable or superior quality to FLUX.1-Fill-Dev with only 0.22B parameters (vs 11.9B), delivering >15× total inference speedup. It achieves leading FID scores (e.g., 0.92 on Places2 Small, 5.39 on CelebA-HQ, 8.15 on FFHQ), with user studies showing it matches its teacher PixelHacker and surpasses 10B-level industrial models.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于计算机视觉与深度学习领域的图像修复（Image Inpainting），具体提出了一种基于潜在扩散模型的轻量化框架。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于潜在扩散模型（LDM）、高效架构（DWConv、线性注意力、Mix-FFN）和知识蒸馏等先前研究；其新思路在于提出了Local-λMix Interaction（LλMI）模块（含Local-λ和Interactive-λ子模块，将上下文信息压缩为固定大小的线性矩阵），并结合自适应多粒度潜在空间蒸馏策略。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了10B级工业级图像修复模型（如FLUX.1-Fill-Dev）计算成本过高、难以实际部署的问题，以及直接压缩导致的表征能力严重退化（表征瓶颈）问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文采用的方法包括：（1）LλMI模块，集成Local-λ（自注意力等价）、Interactive-λ（用于LCG嵌入的交叉注意力等价）和Mix-FFN；（2）使用深度可分离残差块进行空间特征提取；（3）自适应多粒度蒸馏策略，包括粗粒度/细粒度蒸馏损失及潜在空间感知损失，通过基于梯度范数的自适应权重进行动态平衡。
5. 📊 结果与评估: Moebius仅用0.22B参数（对比11.9B）即可达到与FLUX.1-Fill-Dev相当或更优的质量，总推理速度提升超15倍；在Places2 Small（FID 0.92）、CelebA-HQ（FID 5.39）、FFHQ（FID 8.15）等基准上取得领先成绩，用户研究表明其匹配教师模型PixelHacker并显著超越10B级工业模型。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with S-Agent (Paper 1) for its architecturally novel agentic framework that reformulates spatial reasoning as iterative evidence accumulation, supported by strong zero-shot results surpassing GPT-5.4 and Gemini 3 Pro. It pairs naturally with MolmoMotion (Paper 2), which shares the VLM-based 3D spatial understanding theme but tackles dynamic motion forecasting—both release datasets and benchmarks that could inspire future work bridging spatial reasoning and motion prediction. Save Moebius (Paper 3) for a lighter read; though technically impressive with its 0.22B-parameter inpainting model, it targets a narrower efficiency problem orthogonal to the other two papers.
建议先读 S-Agent（论文1），其智能体框架将空间推理重构为迭代式证据积累，架构新颖，且在零样本设置下超越了 GPT-5.4 和 Gemini 3 Pro，结果令人信服。它与 MolmoMotion（论文2）形成自然呼应——两者都以 VLM 为核心关注三维空间理解，但一个聚焦静态空间推理、一个聚焦动态运动预测，且都发布了数据集和基准，有望共同推动空间-运动联合建模的探索。Moebius（论文3）可以留到最后阅读，虽然 0.22B 参数的修复模型技术上令人惊艳，但聚焦的是与前两篇较为正交的模型效率问题。
