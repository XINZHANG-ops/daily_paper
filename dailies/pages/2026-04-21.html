Paper 2

OneVL: One-Step Latent Reasoning and Planning with Vision-Language Explanation

Published: 2026-04-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.18486

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses autonomous driving trajectory prediction using Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models with latent chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on implicit/latent CoT methods (COCONUT, CODI, SIM-CoT), OneVL introduces dual-modal auxiliary decoders (language decoder for CoT text reconstruction + visual decoder for future-frame prediction as world model supervision) and prefill inference for single-pass latent token generation.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing latent CoT methods consistently underperform explicit CoT because purely linguistic latent representations compress symbolic abstractions rather than the causal dynamics that govern driving scenes.
4. 🛠️ Methods: OneVL employs: (1) visual and language latent tokens as compact reasoning carriers, (2) dual auxiliary decoders supervising latents with future-frame visual tokens and CoT text, (3) a three-stage training pipeline (main model warmup → auxiliary decoder warmup → joint fine-tuning), and (4) prefill inference discarding decoders at test time for answer-only latency.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: OneVL achieves state-of-the-art results across four benchmarks (NAVSIM: 88.84 PDM-score, ROADWork: 12.49 ADE, Impromptu: 1.34 ADE, APR1: 2.62 ADE), being the first latent CoT method to surpass explicit CoT while matching answer-only prediction speed (4.46s vs 4.49s on NAVSIM), with optional 0.24s inference via MLP head for real-world deployment.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文研究的是基于视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型的自动驾驶轨迹预测，重点关注隐式思维链（CoT）推理。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于隐式CoT方法（COCONUT、CODI、SIM-CoT），OneVL创新性地引入双模态辅助解码器（语言解码器用于CoT文本重建，视觉解码器用于预测未来帧作为世界模型监督）以及预填充推理实现单步潜伏令牌生成。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有隐式CoT方法始终不及显式CoT，因为纯语言潜伏表示压缩的是符号抽象，而非真正决定驾驶场景的因果动态。
4. 🛠️ 方法: OneVL采用：(1) 视觉和语言潜伏令牌作为紧凑推理载体，(2) 双辅助解码器通过未来帧视觉令牌和CoT文本监督潜伏表示，(3) 三阶段训练流程（主模型预热→辅助解码器预热→联合微调），(4) 推理时丢弃解码器以实现仅答案预测的延迟。
5. 📊 结果与评估: OneVL在四个基准（NAVSIM: 88.84 PDM分数、ROADWork: 12.49 ADE、Impromptu: 1.34 ADE、APR1: 2.62 ADE）上取得最先进成果，成为首个超越显式CoT的隐式CoT方法，同时匹配仅答案预测速度（NAVSIM上4.46秒 vs 4.49秒），并可通过MLP头实现0.24秒推理用于实际部署。 ===

Agent-World: Scaling Real-World Environment Synthesis for Evolving General Agent Intelligence

Published: 2026-04-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.18292

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on scaling real-world environment synthesis for developing evolving general agent intelligence, situated in the domains of LLM agents, agentic reinforcement learning, and scalable environment construction.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior work including MCP protocols, agentic RL methods (EnvScaler, AWM, ScaleEnv), and tool-use training, the paper introduces: (1) Agentic Environment-Task Discovery that autonomously mines topic-aligned databases and executable tools from real-world sources, and (2) Continuous Self-Evolving Agent Training that combines multi-environment RL with a diagnostic arena for co-evolution of agent policies and environments.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses two key bottlenecks: (1) scalable realism in environment synthesis—existing LLM-generated or limited open-source environments fail to capture real-world interaction logic; and (2) lack of continuous self-evolving training mechanisms to diagnose agent weaknesses and drive continual improvement.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method consists of two tightly coupled components: (1) Agentic Environment-Task Discovery—mining databases from web using deep-research agents, generating executable tools with cross-validation, and synthesizing verifiable tasks via graph-based (DAG + random walk) and programmatic approaches; (2) Continuous Self-Evolving Agent Training—multi-environment RL with GRPO and executable rewards, plus a self-evolving arena that dynamically evaluates agents, diagnoses capability gaps via error traces, and generates targeted training data for iterative improvement.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Agent-World-8B and 14B consistently outperform baselines across 23 benchmarks. Key results include λ2-Bench 61.8%/65.4%, BFCL-V4 51.4%/55.8%, and MCP-Mark 8.9%/13.3% for 8B/14B respectively. Scaling analysis shows positive correlation between environment count and performance (18.4%→38.5%), and self-evolution rounds produce monotonic gains (+3.3% to +8.6% across benchmarks).
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于扩展真实世界环境合成以发展不断进化的通用智能体，属于大语言模型智能体、具身强化学习和可扩展环境构建领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于MCP协议、具身强化学习方法（EnvScaler、AWM、ScaleEnv）及工具使用训练等先前工作，论文提出：(1) 具身环境-任务发现——从真实来源自动挖掘主题对齐的数据库和可执行工具；(2) 持续自进化智能体训练——结合多环境强化学习与诊断竞技场，实现智能体策略与环境的协同演化。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决两个关键瓶颈：(1) 环境合成的可扩展真实性——现有LLM生成或有限的开源环境无法捕捉真实世界的交互逻辑；(2) 缺乏持续自进化训练机制来诊断智能体弱点并推动持续改进。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包含两个紧密耦合的组件：(1) 具身环境-任务发现——利用深度研究智能体从网络挖掘数据库，通过交叉验证生成可执行工具，并通过基于图（ DAG + 随机游走）和编程方法合成可验证任务；(2) 持续自进化智能体训练——使用GRPO和可执行奖励进行多环境强化学习，加上自进化竞技场，通过动态评估智能体、利用错误轨迹诊断能力差距，并为迭代改进生成定向训练数据。
5. 📊 结果与评估: Agent-World-8B和14B在23个基准上始终优于基线。关键结果包括：λ2-Bench 61.8%/65.4%、BFCL-V4 51.4%/55.8%、MCP-Mark 8.9%/13.3%（8B/14B）。扩展性分析显示环境数量与性能呈正相关（18.4%→38.5%），自进化轮次产生单调提升（各基准提升+3.3%至+8.6%）。
