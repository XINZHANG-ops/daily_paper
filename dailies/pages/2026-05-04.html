UniVidX: A Unified Multimodal Framework for Versatile Video Generation via Diffusion Priors

Published: 2026-04-30

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.00658

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents a unified multimodal framework (UniVidX) for versatile video generation using video diffusion model priors, covering tasks like intrinsic decomposition, video matting, and controllable synthesis.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on pre-trained video diffusion models (VDMs), the paper introduces three novel designs: Stochastic Condition Masking (SCM) for omni-directional generation, Decoupled Gated LoRA (DGL) for modality-specific adaptation without parameter interference, and Cross-Modal Self-Attention (CMSA) for cross-modal consistency.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing approaches train separate models for each specific task, locking models into fixed input-output mappings and ignoring joint correlations across visual modalities, leading to cross-modal inconsistencies and limited flexibility.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method unifies diverse video tasks as conditional generation problems by dynamically partitioning modalities into conditions and targets (SCM), using per-modality LoRAs activated only for generation targets (DGL), and sharing keys/values across modalities while keeping queries modality-specific (CMSA).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Instantiated as UniVid-Intrinsic and UniVid-Alpha, the framework achieves state-of-the-art performance across 15 tasks including text-to-intrinsic, inverse rendering, video matting, and relighting, with exceptional data efficiency (trained on <1k videos) and robust out-of-distribution generalization.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文提出了一个统一的多模态框架（UniVidX），利用视频扩散模型先验进行多功能视频生成，覆盖内禀分解、视频抠图和可控合成等任务。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于预训练视频扩散模型（VDMs），论文提出三个创新设计：随机条件掩码（SCM）实现全向生成、解耦门控LoRA（DGL）在无参数干扰情况下进行模态特定适应，以及跨模态自注意力（CMSA）确保模态间一致性。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有方法为每个特定任务训练独立模型，将模型锁定在固定输入输出映射上，忽略了视觉模态间的联合相关性，导致跨模态不一致和有限灵活性。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该方法通过动态地将模态划分为条件与目标（SCM）、使用仅在生成目标时激活的每模态LoRA（DGL）、以及跨模态共享键值但保留模态特定查询（CMSA），将多样化视频任务统一为条件生成问题。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 作为UniVid-Intrinsic和UniVid-Alpha实例化，该框架在15项任务（包括文本到内禀、反向渲染、视频抠图和重光照等）上达到最优性能，具有卓越的数据效率（基于<1k视频训练）和鲁棒的分布外泛化能力。

Programming with Data: Test-Driven Data Engineering for Self-Improving LLMs from Raw Corpora

Published: 2026-04-26

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.24819

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents "Programming with Data," a test-driven data engineering paradigm for improving LLMs through structured knowledge extraction from raw corpora across 16 disciplines in sciences, engineering, biomedicine, and social sciences.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on test-driven software development, synthetic data generation (Alpaca, Orca, Phi), and domain-specific fine-tuning; introduces the key idea that when training data and evaluation share a common knowledge representation extracted from source corpora, the data-engineering lifecycle maps onto the software development lifecycle (training data as source code, training as compilation, benchmarks as unit tests, failure repair as debugging).
3. ❓ Problem: Current domain-specific fine-tuning operates in an open loop—model failures cannot be traced to specific deficiencies in training data, forcing practitioners to add data indiscriminately without targeted repair mechanisms.
4. 🛠️ Methods: Extract a three-level knowledge structure (L1: atomic concepts, L2: relational triples, L3: reasoning chains) from corpus; build benchmarks from L3 chains and training data from L1/L2; implement ProDa framework with Builder (extraction/synthesis), Tester (benchmark), and Debugger (diagnose concept gaps or reasoning deficits, generate targeted patches); follow CORE principles (Contextualized, Organized, Rigorous, Evolving).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: After single debugging iteration, 32B models surpass GPT-5.4, Gemini-3-flash, and DeepSeek-v3.2 on 16-discipline average; V2 models reach 78.84% (Qwen-2.5-32B) and 79.52% (Qwen-3-32B); general capabilities on MMLU and C-Eval remain preserved; ProDa V2 achieves 72.11% at 5K scale vs 56.18% for strongest baseline.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文提出"编程式数据工程"，一种测试驱动的数据工程范式，通过从原始语料中提取结构化知识来提升大语言模型，涵盖自然科学、工程、生物医学和社会科学等16个学科领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于测试驱动软件开发、合成数据生成（Alpaca、Orca、Phi系列）和领域微调；创新性地提出当训练数据和评估共享从源语料中提取的公共知识表示时，数据工程生命周期可映射到软件开发生命周期（训练数据即源代码，训练即编译，基准测试即单元测试，故障修复即调试）。
3. ❓ 问题: 当前领域微调处于开放循环状态——模型失败无法追溯到训练数据中的具体缺陷，导致实践者只能盲目增加数据而缺乏针对性修复机制。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 从语料中提取三层知识结构（L1原子概念、L2关系三元组、L3推理链）；从L3链构建基准测试，从L1/L2构建训练数据；实现ProDa框架含Builder（提取/合成）、Tester（基准测试）和Debugger（诊断概念缺口或推理缺陷，生成针对性补丁）；遵循CORE原则（情境化、组织化、严谨化、迭代化）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 经单轮调试迭代后，32B模型在16学科平均性能上超越GPT-5.4、Gemini-3-flash和DeepSeek-v3.2；V2模型达到78.84%（Qwen-2.5-32B）和79.52%（Qwen-3-32B）；MMLU和C-Eval上的通用能力得以保留；ProDa V2在5K规模下达72.11%，而最强基线仅56.18%。
