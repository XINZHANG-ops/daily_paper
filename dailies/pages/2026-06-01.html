Qwen-VLA: Unifying Vision-Language-Action Modeling across Tasks, Environments, and Robot Embodiments

Published: 2026-05-27

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.30280

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces Qwen-VLA, a unified embodied foundation model in the vision-language-action (VLA) domain for robotics, aiming to generalize across manipulation, navigation, and trajectory prediction tasks, environments, and robot embodiments. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** It builds upon specialized embodied policies and vision-language models, proposing a single generalist model that unifies heterogeneous embodied decision-making through a shared action-and-trajectory prediction space, embodiment-aware prompt conditioning, and a progressive four-stage training recipe. 3. **❓ Problem:** The paper solves the fragmentation and limited cross-task, cross-environment, and cross-embodiment generalization caused by existing embodied systems being specialized to narrow task families or robot platforms. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** The method employs a Qwen3.5 VLM backbone with a DiT-based flow-matching action decoder, using embodiment-aware textual prompts, a unified zero-padded action representation, large-scale joint pretraining on diverse heterogeneous data, and a staged pipeline of text-to-action pretraining, continued pretraining, supervised fine-tuning, and reinforcement learning. 5. **📊 Results and Evaluation:** Qwen-VLA-Instruct achieves strong multi-task performance and out-of-distribution generalization, attaining 97.9% on LIBERO, 73.7% on Simpler-WidowX, 86.1/87.2% on RoboTwin-Easy/Hard, 69.0% OSR on R2R, 59.6% SR on RxR, 76.9% average OOD success on real-world ALOHA, and 26.6% zero-shot success on DOMINO dynamic manipulation, often surpassing specialist models.

COLLEAGUE.SKILL: Automated AI Skill Generation via Expert Knowledge Distillation

Published: 2026-05-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.31264

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents an automated system for distilling heterogeneous human traces into portable, inspectable, and versioned AI skill packages that encode bounded person-grounded expertise and interaction styles for LLM agents.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building upon existing skill frameworks and memory systems, the paper introduces an end-to-end trace-to-skill distillation workflow that treats person-grounded knowledge as inspectable, correctable, and governable artifacts rather than opaque prompts.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the problem of transforming dispersed evidence of human expertise and interaction patterns into structured, reusable AI skill packages with explicit portability, inspectability, and governance constraints.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors propose a dual-track distillation pipeline that generates versioned skill packages separating capability and behavior tracks, supported by natural-language correction, rollback, and multi-host installation mechanisms.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The deployed open-source system has approximately 18.5k repository stars and a public gallery listing 215 skills from 165 contributors with over 100k cumulative stars, though the authors explicitly frame these as distribution metrics rather than behavioral fidelity or task-performance results.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文提出了一种自动化系统，用于将异构的人类痕迹蒸馏成可移植、可检查且版本化的AI技能包，为LLM智能体编码受约束的基于人的专业知识和交互风格。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 在现有技能框架和记忆系统的基础上，本文引入了一种端到端的痕迹到技能蒸馏工作流，将基于人的知识视为可检查、可修正且可治理的工件，而非不透明的提示。
3. ❓ 问题: 本文解决了如何将分散的人类专业知识和交互模式证据转化为具有明确可移植性、可检查性和治理约束的结构化、可复用AI技能包的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者提出了一种双轨蒸馏管道，生成将能力轨道和行为轨道分离的版本化技能包，并支持自然语言修正、回滚和多宿主安装机制。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 该已部署的开源系统拥有约18.5k个仓库星标，其公共画廊收录了来自165位贡献者的215个技能，累计星标超过10万，但作者明确将这些指标视为分发指标，而非行为保真度或任务性能结果。

Paper 3

Representation Forcing for Bottleneck-Free Unified Multimodal Models

Published: 2026-05-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.31604

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces a method in computer vision and multimodal machine learning for unified multimodal models that jointly perform image understanding and generation without structural bottlenecks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on unified multimodal models that combine autoregressive language modeling with diffusion-based image generation, but departs from prior work by eliminating frozen pretrained VAEs through Representation Forcing, which makes visual representation prediction a native capability of the decoder.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the structural bottleneck and quality limitations caused by existing unified multimodal models relying on frozen, separately pretrained VAEs for image generation, where removing the VAE creates a quality gap because the model struggles to learn both high-level semantic structure and low-level pixel details simultaneously from raw pixels.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes Representation Forcing, which uses online vector quantization to discretize visual features from the model's jointly trained understanding encoder into intermediate tokens, then trains the decoder to autoregressively predict these tokens before using them as in-context conditioning to guide pixel-space flow matching diffusion within the same transformer backbone.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Experiments demonstrate that the pixel-space model with Representation Forcing matches state-of-the-art VAE-based unified models on image generation benchmarks while outperforming them on image understanding tasks, confirming that RF closes the quality gap without requiring any external latent space.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文属于计算机视觉与多模态机器学习领域，提出了一种消除结构瓶颈、实现联合图像理解与生成的统一多模态模型方法。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该工作基于将自回归语言建模与扩散图像生成相结合的统一多模态模型，但通过表征强制（Representation Forcing）使视觉表征预测成为解码器的原生能力，从而摒弃了对冻结预训练VAE的依赖。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有统一多模态模型因依赖冻结的独立预训练VAE而产生的结构瓶颈与质量限制，以及直接移除VAE后模型难以从原始像素中同时学习高级语义结构和低级细节所造成的质量差距问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了表征强制方法：通过在线向量量化将模型自身联合训练的视觉编码器特征离散化为中间表征token，训练解码器以自回归方式预测这些token，并使其保留在上下文中以指导同一主干网络内的像素空间流匹配扩散生成。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 实验表明，采用表征强制的像素空间模型在图像生成基准上达到了与最先进的基于VAE的统一模型相当的性能，并在图像理解任务上普遍优于后者，证实了该方法能在无需任何外部隐空间的情况下有效弥合质量差距。
