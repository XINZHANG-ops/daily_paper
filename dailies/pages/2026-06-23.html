EnterpriseClawBench: Benchmarking Agents from Real Workplace Sessions

Published: 2026-06-21

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.23654

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces EnterpriseClawBench, an enterprise agent benchmark constructed from real proprietary workplace sessions, situated in the domain of LLM agent evaluation for enterprise/workspace tasks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on Claw-style benchmarks (WildClawBench, ClawBench, Claw-SWE-Bench) and enterprise benchmarks (Workspace-Bench, EnterpriseBench, TheAgentCompany), and its new idea is to derive reproducible benchmark tasks directly from naturally occurring internal enterprise sessions rather than human-authored or simulated scenarios.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses three gaps: (1) tension between enterprise realism and scalable task construction, (2) the need for multidimensional evaluation beyond single model scores, and (3) the lack of task-class-level skill generalization testing.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes an automated construction pipeline (mechanical gates, fixture recovery, prompt rewriting, taxonomy assignment, hard rules, semantic rubrics), a multidimensional evaluation framework combining hard rules with text/visual LLM judges across 32 harness–model combinations, and a consumer–creator skill injection matrix for skill-transfer testing.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: On the 120-task Lite subset, the best configuration reaches only 0.663 (Codex/GPT-5.5); harness–model coupling significantly affects scores (e.g., Claude-family models drop under Hermes), cost–score shows diminishing returns, GPT-5.5 is the strongest generalist, and visual judges are much less reliable than text judges (MAE 0.303 vs 0.134).
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文介绍了 EnterpriseClawBench，一个基于真实企业内部工作会话构建的企业级智能体基准测试，属于大语言模型智能体在企业工作空间任务中的评估领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于 Claw 系列基准（WildClawBench、ClawBench、Claw-SWE-Bench）及企业基准（Workspace-Bench、EnterpriseBench、TheAgentCompany）等研究，其新思路是直接从企业内部自然产生的会话中导出可复现的基准任务，而非使用人工编写或模拟的场景。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文旨在解决三个空白：(1) 企业真实感与任务可扩展构建之间的矛盾；(2) 需要超越单一模型分数的多维度评估；(3) 缺乏任务类别层面的技能泛化测试。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出自动化构建流水线（机械门控、依赖文件恢复、提示改写、分类法分配、硬性规则、语义评分标准），结合硬性规则与文本/视觉大模型评判器的多维度评估框架（涵盖 32 种 harness–模型组合），以及用于技能迁移测试的消费者–创造者技能注入矩阵。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在 120 个任务的 Lite 子集上，最佳配置仅取得 0.663（Codex/GPT-5.5）；harness–模型耦合显著影响得分（如 Claude 系列模型在 Hermes 下表现下降），成本–得分呈边际递减，GPT-5.5 为最强通用模型，而视觉评判器的可靠性远低于文本评判器（MAE 分别为 0.303 和 0.134）。

Paper 2

KaLM-Reranker-V1: Fast but Not Late Interaction for Compressed Document Reranking

Published: 2026-06-21

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.22807

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on document reranking in information retrieval, specifically proposing an efficient reranking model for large-scale retrieval systems.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds upon existing encoder-based rerankers (e.g., BERT reranking), decoder-based generative rerankers (e.g., RankGPT, BGE, Qwen3-Reranker), and late-interaction models (e.g., ColBERT, ColBERTv2); its new idea is the "Fast But Not Late Interaction" (FBNL) paradigm that decouples passage encoding from query-side relevance modeling while preserving expressive fine-grained interaction through decoder cross-attention.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the efficiency-effectiveness trade-off in existing rerankers—encoder/decoder-based rerankers tightly couple query and passage computation requiring expensive online re-encoding, while late-interaction methods sacrifice fine-grained query-passage interaction for efficiency.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper uses an encoder-decoder architecture (based on T5Gemma2) where the encoder pre-encodes passages offline with Matryoshka Embedding Pooling (MEP) for compression, and the decoder processes system/user instructions and queries, computing relevance through merged self-attention and cross-attention over query context and compressed passage representations; trained via a three-stage pipeline (general reranking, task-specific adaptation, and knowledge distillation).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: KaLM-Reranker-V1 (in Nano/0.27B, Small/1B, Large/4B sizes) achieves state-of-the-art or competitive performance on BEIR, MIRACL, and LMEB benchmarks with significantly lower online computation cost (e.g., 10× efficiency gain over gte-reranker-base, 2× over bge-reranker-v2-gemma); moderate MEP compression (r=2 to r=8) preserves performance with minimal degradation, while the 0.27B Nano model is competitive with 7-12B embedding models on LMEB.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于信息检索中的文档重排序领域，提出了一种面向大规模检索系统的高效重排序模型。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于现有的基于编码器的重排序器（如 BERT 重排序）、基于解码器的生成式重排序器（如 RankGPT、BGE、Qwen3-Reranker）以及晚期交互模型（如 ColBERT、ColBERTv2）；其新思路是提出"快但非晚期交互"（FBNL）范式，通过解码器交叉注意力将段落编码与查询端相关性建模解耦，同时保留表达力强的细粒度交互。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有重排序器中效率与效果之间的权衡问题——基于编码器/解码器的重排序器将查询和段落计算紧密耦合，需要昂贵的在线重新编码，而晚期交互方法为效率牺牲了细粒度的查询-段落交互。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文采用编码器-解码器架构（基于 T5Gemma2），其中编码器离线对段落进行预编码并使用 Matryoshka 嵌入池化（MEP）进行压缩，解码器处理系统/用户指令和查询，通过合并的自注意力和跨注意力对查询上下文与压缩的段落表示进行相关性计算；通过三阶段训练流程（通用重排序学习、任务特定适应、知识蒸馏）进行训练。
5. 📊 结果与评估: KaLM-Reranker-V1（Nano/0.27B、Small/1B、Large/4B 三种规模）在 BEIR、MIRACL 和 LMEB 基准上实现了最优或具有竞争力的性能，同时在线计算成本显著降低（例如比 gte-reranker-base 效率提升约 10 倍，比 bge-reranker-v2-gemma 提升约 2 倍）；适度的 MEP 压缩（r=2 至 r=8）几乎不损失性能，其中 0.27B 的 Nano 模型在 LMEB 上与 7-12B 的嵌入模型具有竞争力。

PlanBench-XL: Evaluating Long-Horizon Planning of LLM Tool-Use Agents in Large-Scale Tool Ecosystems

Published: 2026-06-20

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.22388

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on evaluating long-horizon planning capabilities of LLM tool-use agents in large-scale, retrieval-mediated tool ecosystems, with the benchmark instantiated in the retail domain.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on existing tool-use benchmarks (e.g., ToolBench, MCP-Bench, EscapeBench, ToolGym) that typically assume fixed visible toolsets, clean descriptions, or one-shot retrieval, and introduces PlanBench-XL, which uniquely combines tool retrieval, implicit sub-goal inference, bi-directional exploration (forward/backward anticipation), unreliable tool access, and scalable generation, along with a novel retrieval-time blocking mechanism simulating real-world tool failures.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of benchmarks that evaluate LLM agents' ability to perform adaptive long-horizon planning under partial tool observability and unreliable tool conditions, specifically testing whether agents can iteratively retrieve tools, infer implicit sub-goals, and re-plan when tool-use paths fail.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors construct a benchmark of 327 retail tasks over 1,665 tools using a scalable LLM-based generation pipeline (typed datatypes, executable tools, and backend databases), implement a retriever supporting three modes (input-conditioned, output-conditioned, and input-output-conditioned retrieval) for bi-directional exploration, and design a blocking module with three perturbation types (explicit failure, implicit failure, and semantic misleading) to simulate unreliable tool environments; they evaluate 10 frontier LLMs using metrics spanning task completion, exploration behavior, and execution quality, followed by trajectory-level error analysis.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Results show that massive-tool planning remains highly challenging—even Gemini-3.1-Pro achieves only 77.06% accuracy in default settings while most models fall below 60%; GPT-5.4 collapses from 51.90% to 11.36% under the most severe blocking condition; exploration breadth (Mean EDT) strongly correlates with accuracy (r=0.902), but frequent searching alone is insufficient; implicit (silent) failures and longer recovery paths cause the largest performance drops; most failures stem from "irrecoverable drift" after partial progress rather than from lack of retrieval, and additional test-time compute yields only marginal gains, revealing that current agents lack robust adaptive re-planning capabilities.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于在大规模、检索中介的工具生态系统中评估LLM工具使用智能体的长视野规划能力，其基准测试在零售领域实例化。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于现有工具使用基准（如ToolBench、MCP-Bench、EscapeBench、ToolGym等，这些基准通常假设固定可见的工具集、清晰的工具描述或一次性检索），并提出了PlanBench-XL，独特地融合了工具检索、隐式子目标推理、双向探索（前向/后向预期）、不可靠工具访问及可扩展生成，并引入新颖的检索时阻塞机制来模拟真实世界的工具失败。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有基准未能评估LLM智能体在部分工具可观测性和不可靠工具条件下进行自适应长视野规划能力的问题，具体测试智能体能否迭代地检索工具、推断隐式子目标，并在工具使用路径失败时重新规划。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者构建了一个包含327个零售任务和1,665个工具的基准，使用可扩展的LLM生成管道（类型化数据类型、可执行工具和后端数据库），实现了支持三种模式（输入条件、输出条件和输入-输出条件检索）的检索器以支持双向探索，并设计了一个具有三种扰动类型（显式失败、隐式失败和语义误导）的阻塞模块来模拟不可靠的工具环境；使用涵盖任务完成、探索行为和执行质量的多个指标评估了10个前沿LLM，并进行了轨迹级错误分析。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 结果表明大规模工具规划仍然极具挑战性——即使是Gemini-3.1-Pro在默认设置下也仅达到77.06%的准确率，大多数模型低于60%；GPT-5.4在最严重阻塞条件下从51.90%骤降至11.36%；探索广度（Mean EDT）与准确率高度相关（r=0.902），但仅频繁搜索并不充分；隐式（静默）失败和更长的恢复路径导致最大的性能下降；大多数失败源于部分进展后的"不可恢复漂移"而非检索不足，且额外的测试时计算仅带来边际收益，揭示了当前智能体缺乏稳健的自适应重新规划能力。

Start with PlanBench-XL (Paper 3) — its findings on agent collapse under tool failure and the strong correlation between exploration breadth and accuracy (r=0.902) offer the most actionable insights for LLM agent research. Then read EnterpriseClawBench (Paper 1) as a complementary benchmark focused on enterprise workflows; both papers expose a shared gap: current agents lack robust adaptive re-planning in realistic, failure-prone environments. KaLM-Reranker-V1 (Paper 2) is tangentially related through its focus on efficient retrieval, which underpins the tool-retrieval mechanisms evaluated in PlanBench-XL.
