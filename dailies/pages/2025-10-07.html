Published: 2025-10-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.05094

1. üìò Topic and Domain: Video generation with improved reasoning capabilities through chain-of-visual-thought framework.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on recent video generation models and large language/multimodal models, proposing a novel framework called VChain that combines the reasoning capabilities of multimodal models with video generation.

Published: 2025-10-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2510.05025

1. üìò Topic and Domain: The paper explores imperceptible jailbreaking attacks against Large Language Models (LLMs) using invisible Unicode variation selector characters.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Previous research focused on visible jailbreak modifications, while this paper introduces a novel approach using invisible Unicode characters for the first time.
3. ‚ùì Problem: The paper addresses how to create effective jailbreak prompts without any visible modifications to malicious questions, making attacks harder to detect visually.
4. üõ†Ô∏è Methods: The authors propose a chain-of-search pipeline that optimizes invisible variation selector suffixes through multiple rounds of random search, reusing successful components to improve attack effectiveness.
