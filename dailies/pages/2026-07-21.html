Paper 1

SWE-Pruner Pro: The Coder LLM Already Knows What to Prune

Published: 2026-07-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.18213

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper addresses context pruning for coding agents, specifically focusing on efficient long-context management in multi-turn software engineering workflows using Large Language Models (LLMs).
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on prior context pruning work like SWE-Pruner, which uses a separate scoring model conditioned on an explicit goal-hint query, and general-purpose compressors like LLMLingua2 and LongCodeZip. The new idea is to read the pruning signal directly from the agent backbone's own internal representations (hidden states) rather than relying on an external scoring model, eliminating the need for additional model calls or goal-hint queries.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the problem of redundant long tool outputs accumulating in coding agents' context windows during multi-turn interactions, which inflates costs, triggers long-context degradation, and limits efficiency, while existing pruning methods require extra models or queries that add overhead.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method attaches a lightweight pruning head (FFN with LayerNorm, dropout, and length-aware embedding) to the frozen agent backbone that reads last-layer hidden states from the prefill pass, using a per-sample balanced focal loss and length-aware embedding indexed by line count to produce per-token keep-or-prune logits aggregated into per-line decisions.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: SWE-Pruner Pro saves up to 39% of prompt and completion tokens across two backbones (MiMo-V2-Flash and Qwen3-Coder-Next) and four benchmarks (SWE-Bench Verified, SWE-QA, SWE-QA-Pro, Oolong), while preserving or improving task quality—boosting MiMo-V2-Flash's SWE-Bench Verified resolve rate by +3.8% and Oolong accuracy by +2.2 points, with only 15% added inference overhead.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文聚焦于编码代理的上下文剪枝，属于多轮软件工程工作流中大语言模型（LLM）长上下文高效管理的研究领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于先前上下文剪枝工作，如使用独立评分模型并依赖显式目标提示查询的 SWE-Pruner，以及 LLMLingua2、LongCodeZip 等通用压缩器。创新之处在于直接从代理主干的内部表征（隐藏状态）中读取剪枝信号，无需额外的评分模型、模型调用或目标提示查询。
3. ❓ 问题: 本文解决了编码代理在多轮交互中累积冗长工具输出导致上下文窗口膨胀、推理成本上升及长上下文性能退化的问题，同时克服现有剪枝方法需额外模型或查询带来开销的局限性。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该方法在冻结的代理主干上附加一个轻量级剪枝头（含 LayerNorm、Dropout 的 FFN 及长度感知嵌入），从前缀填充过程中读取最后一层隐藏状态，使用逐样本平衡焦点损失和按行数索引的长度感知嵌入生成逐 token 的保留/剪枝 logits，再聚合成逐行决策。
5. 📊 结果与评估: SWE-Pruner Pro 在两个主干模型（MiMo-V2-Flash 和 Qwen3-Coder-Next）和四个基准测试（SWE-Bench Verified、SWE-QA、SWE-QA-Pro、Oolong）上最多节省 39% 的 prompt 和 completion token，同时保持或提升任务质量——将 MiMo-V2-Flash 在 SWE-Bench Verified 的解决率提升 +3.8%，Oolong 准确率提升 +2.2 分，且仅增加 15% 的推理开销。

RynnBrain 1.1: Towards More Capable and Generalizable Embodied Foundation Model

Published: 2026-07-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.17977

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: Embodied foundation models for robotics, covering perception, spatial reasoning, 3D grounding, contact-point prediction, and Vision-Language-Action (VLA) policy post-training.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Built upon RynnBrain 1.0 and Qwen3.5, introducing contact-point prediction, native 3D grounding, and a unified cross-embodiment action space with embodiment-specific masking to better align representations with robot manipulation.
3. ❓ Problem: How to bring embodied foundation model representations closer to robot manipulation, enable effective VLA post-training across heterogeneous embodiments, and understand how embodied capabilities scale with model size.
4. 🛠️ Methods: A unified spatio-temporal and physically grounded training framework with three model scales (2B, 9B, 122B-A10B), explicit 3D-grounded pretraining using WildDet3D and FoundationPose, a compact contact-centered grasp representation, an 81-dimensional unified action space with embodiment-specific masking, flow-matching VLA architecture, and Real-Time Chunking (RTC) for low-latency control.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: RynnBrain 1.1-122B-A10B surpasses proprietary and open-source models on VSI-Bench (75.0), MMSI (52.0), and RefSpatial-Bench (79.1); achieves 34.28/41.12 AP@15 on SUN RGB-D and 17.36/23.44 AP3D on WildDet3D-Bench for 3D grounding; and RynnBrain-VLA achieves 86.67% success rate on real robots, outperforming Qwen-Based-VLA (60%) and GR00T N1.7 (73.33%), with the generalist version reaching 91.67%.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 面向机器人的具身基础模型，涵盖感知、空间推理、3D定位、接触点预测以及视觉-语言-动作（VLA）策略的后训练。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于RynnBrain 1.0和Qwen3.5，引入接触点预测、原生3D定位以及带有本体特定掩码的统一跨本体动作空间，使表征更好地与机器人操作对齐。
3. ❓ 问题: 如何让具身基础模型的表征更接近机器人操作，如何实现跨异构本体的有效VLA后训练，以及具身能力如何随模型规模扩展。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 统一的时空与物理基础训练框架，覆盖三种模型规模（2B、9B、122B-A10B），使用WildDet3D和FoundationPose进行显式3D预训练，紧凑的接触中心抓取表示，81维统一动作空间配合本体特定掩码，流匹配VLA架构，以及用于低延迟控制的实时分块（RTC）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: RynnBrain 1.1-122B-A10B在VSI-Bench（75.0）、MMSI（52.0）和RefSpatial-Bench（79.1）上超越专有和开源模型；在SUN RGB-D上取得34.28/41.12 AP@15，在WildDet3D-Bench上取得17.36/23.44 AP3D；RynnBrain-VLA在真实机器人上取得86.67%成功率，优于Qwen-Based-VLA（60%）和GR00T N1.7（73.33%），通用版达91.67%。

HOMIE: Human-object Centric Video Personalization via Multimodal Intelligent Enchancement

Published: 2026-07-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.18217

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on Human-Object Centric Video Personalization (HOCVP), a subfield of subject-driven controllable video generation that synthesizes videos featuring diverse human-object interactions from reference images of humans and objects.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds upon DiT-based text-to-video diffusion models (Wan2.1/2.2-14B) and prior MLLM-integration strategies, which either align MLLM features with text embeddings (UmT5) or replace the text encoder entirely. The new idea is to preserve the original text encoder while using MLLM (Qwen3-VL-2B-Thinking) solely for extracting reference-level relationships, introducing two novel modules: Global Multimodal Guidance (GMG) and Modality-Reference Embedding (MRE).
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses two key limitations: (1) existing inter-subject personalization methods struggle to balance subject fidelity with accurate interaction patterns, especially for abstract concepts like logos that require inferential reasoning, and (2) existing methods lack effective mechanisms to understand latent correspondences in intra-subject references such as OCR maps and multi-view inputs.
4. 🛠️ Methods: HOMIE employs a unified multimodal input paradigm concatenating video tokens, reference image tokens, and MLLM feature tokens. The Global Multimodal Guidance (GMG) module applies feature-wise affine transformations on video token queries and keys using pooled MLLM representations to inject semantic guidance into self-attention. The Modality-Reference Embedding (MRE) module adds learnable embeddings to distinguish tokens by modality type and to bind intra-subject references sharing the same identity while separating inter-subject references.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: HOMIE achieves state-of-the-art performance on multiple HOCVP metrics, including a 21.8% relative improvement in OCR accuracy compared to SkyReels-V3, the best DINO-I score (0.543), and the best object similarity (0.891) among open-source methods. A user study with 40 participants showed HOMIE was preferred 64.7% overall, 66.1% for subject consistency, and 57.9% for text following over baselines including Kling, Phantom, SkyReels-V3, and UniVideo.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本论文聚焦于以人-物为中心的视频个性化（HOCVP），这是受主体驱动的可控视频生成子领域，旨在从人类和物体的参考图像合成包含多样化人-物交互的视频。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于基于DiT的文生视频扩散模型（Wan2.1/2.2-14B）以及先前的MLLM集成策略——要么将MLLM特征与文本嵌入（UmT5）对齐，要么完全替换文本编码器。新思路在于保留原始文本编码器的同时，将MLLM（Qwen3-VL-2B-Thinking）专门用于提取参考级关系，并引入了两个新模块：全局多模态引导（GMG）和模态-参考嵌入（MRE）。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了两个关键局限性：（1）现有的跨主体个性化方法难以在主体保真度与准确的人-物交互模式之间取得平衡，尤其是对于需要推理能力的抽象概念（如标志）；（2）现有方法缺乏理解主体内参考（如OCR图谱和多视图输入）潜在对应关系的有效机制。
4. 🛠️ 方法: HOMIE采用统一的多模态输入范式，将视频标记、参考图像标记和MLLM特征标记进行拼接。全局多模态引导（GMG）模块使用池化的MLLM表示对视频标记的查询和键应用特征仿射变换，将语义引导注入自注意力机制。模态-参考嵌入（MRE）模块添加可学习的嵌入以按模态类型区分标记，并绑定共享同一身份的主体内参考，同时分离跨主体参考。
5. 📊 结果与评估: HOMIE在多项HOCVP指标上取得了最先进的性能，包括相比SkyReels-V3在OCR准确率上实现了21.8%的相对提升，以及在开源方法中取得最佳DINO-I分数（0.543）和最佳物体相似度（0.891）。一项40名参与者参与的用户研究表明，HOMIE在整体质量（64.7%）、主体一致性（66.1%）和文本遵循（57.9%）方面均优于Kling、Phantom、SkyReels-V3和UniVideo等基线方法。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with RynnBrain 1.1 for the broadest impact—it's a comprehensive embodied foundation model spanning 3D grounding, VLA post-training, and cross-embodiment control, offering the most to digest. Then move to SWE-Pruner Pro if you care about practical LLM efficiency, as its insight of reading pruning signals directly from a frozen backbone's hidden states is elegant and likely generalizable. A unifying thread across all three papers is their use of Qwen-family backbones (Qwen3-Coder, Qwen3.5, Qwen3-VL) and the growing trend of leveraging internal representations or auxiliary lightweight modules to extend foundation model capabilities without retraining the core.
