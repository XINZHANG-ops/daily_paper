HY-World 2.0: A Multi-Modal World Model for Reconstructing, Generating, and Simulating 3D Worlds

Published: 2026-04-14

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14268

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents HY-World 2.0, a multi-modal world model framework for reconstructing, generating, and simulating 3D worlds from diverse inputs including text prompts, single-view images, multi-view images, and videos.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on HY-World 1.0/1.5, the paper introduces a novel four-stage pipeline and key innovations including HY-Pano 2.0 with adaptive perspective-to-equirectangular transformation, WorldNav for semantic-aware trajectory planning, WorldStereo 2.0 operating in a keyframe latent space with consistent memory mechanisms, and WorldMirror 2.0 with normalized position encoding for flexible resolution inference.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the longstanding gap between 3D world generation (synthesizing explorable scenes from sparse inputs) and world reconstruction (recovering geometric structures from dense multi-view inputs) that currently bifurcates existing solutions.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The framework employs a four-stage process: (1) panorama generation via HY-Pano 2.0, (2) trajectory planning using WorldNav with five heuristic modes, (3) world expansion through WorldStereo 2.0 with Global-Geometric Memory and Spatial-Stereo Memory++, and (4) world composition using WorldMirror 2.0 and 3D Gaussian Splatting with MaskGaussian.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: HY-World 2.0 achieves state-of-the-art performance among open-source approaches across multiple benchmarks, including text-to-panorama, image-to-panorama, single-view reconstruction, and multi-view reconstruction tasks, delivering results comparable to the closed-source model Marble while completing end-to-end world generation in approximately 10 minutes.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文提出了HY-World 2.0，一个多模态世界模型框架，能够从文本提示、单视角图像、多视角图像和视频等多种输入中重建、生成和模拟3D世界。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于HY-World 1.0/1.5，该论文引入了新颖的四阶段流程和关键创新，包括具有自适应透视到等矩形变换的HY-Pano 2.0、语义感知的WorldNav轨迹规划、在关键帧潜在空间中运行且具有一致记忆机制的WorldStereo 2.0，以及具有归一化位置编码以实现灵活分辨率推理的WorldMirror 2.0。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了现有世界模型中长期存在的3D世界生成（从稀疏输入合成可探索场景）与世界重建（从密集多视角输入恢复几何结构）之间的差距，当前解决方案通常只能专注于其中一项。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该框架采用四阶段流程：（1）通过HY-Pano 2.0进行全景生成；（2）使用具有五种启发式模式的WorldNav进行轨迹规划；（3）通过具有全局几何记忆和空间立体记忆++的WorldStereo 2.0进行世界扩展；（4）使用WorldMirror 2.0和带有MaskGaussian的3D高斯泼溅进行世界组合。
5. 📊 结果与评估: HY-World 2.0在多个基准测试中实现了开源方法中最先进的性能，包括文本到全景、图像到全景、单视角重建和多视角重建任务，其结果可与闭源模型Marble相媲美，同时端到端世界生成仅需约10分钟完成。

RationalRewards: Reasoning Rewards Scale Visual Generation Both Training and Test Time

Published: 2026-04-12

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.11626

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses reward modeling for visual generation (text-to-image and image editing), combining reinforcement learning and visual generation evaluation.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on scalar reward models (ImageReward, EditReward, PickScore) that collapse judgments into single scores; introduces reasoning-based reward models that generate structured multi-dimensional critiques before scoring.
3. ❓ Problem: Current reward models discard the reasoning underlying human preference, leading to reward hacking, opaque evaluation, and inability to actively improve generators through either training-time or test-time optimization.
4. 🛠️ Methods: Proposes RationalRewards (8B), a reasoning-based reward model, and PARROT framework that trains it using preference-anchored rationale generation, consistency filtering, and distillation; enables dual-space optimization: parameter space via RL and prompt space via Generate–Critique–Refine loop.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Achieves SOTA preference prediction among open-source reward models (competitive with Gemini-2.5-Pro) using 10–20× less training data; consistently outperforms scalar baselines as RL reward; test-time prompt tuning alone matches or exceeds RL fine-tuning on multiple benchmarks.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于视觉生成（文本到图像及图像编辑）的奖励建模，结合强化学习与视觉生成评估。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于标量奖励模型（ImageReward、EditReward、PickScore）将评判压缩为单一分数；提出推理奖励模型，在评分前生成结构化多维度批评。
3. ❓ 问题: 当前奖励模型丢弃了人类偏好背后的推理，导致奖励黑客攻击、评估不透明，无法通过训练时或测试时优化主动改进生成器。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 提出RationalRewards（8B）推理奖励模型及PARROT框架，通过偏好锚定理由生成、一致性过滤和蒸馏进行训练；实现双空间优化：参数空间通过强化学习，提示空间通过生成-批评-改进循环。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在开源奖励模型中达到SOTA偏好预测水平（与Gemini-2.5-Pro相当），使用少10-20倍的训练数据；作为强化学习奖励持续超越标量基线；测试时提示调优单独即可在多个基准上匹配或超越强化学习微调效果。

DR^{3}-Eval: Towards Realistic and Reproducible Deep Research Evaluation

Published: 2026-04-15

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.14683

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses the evaluation of Deep Research Agents (DRAs) capable of performing complex, long-horizon research tasks involving planning, retrieval, multimodal understanding, and citation-grounded report generation.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing benchmarks like DeepResearch Bench, DRBench, and DeepResearchGym, the paper introduces DR3-Eval which uniquely combines authentic multimodal user files with a controlled static sandbox corpus containing supportive documents, distractors, and noise, along with a reverse-construction methodology ensuring verifiable solution paths.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the challenge of evaluating deep research agents in a way that balances realism, controllability, and reproducibility, addressing issues of dynamic web environments, ambiguous task definitions, and the gap between real-world research complexity and evaluation settings.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper constructs DR3-Eval through a five-stage pipeline (grounding in real-world needs, distilling search paths, building research sandbox, constructing queries, quality control), employs a multi-agent system (DR3-Agent) with hierarchical architecture, and uses a multi-dimensional evaluation framework measuring Information Recall, Factual Accuracy, Citation Coverage, Instruction Following, and Depth Quality.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: DR3-Eval proves highly challenging with Claude Sonnet 4 achieving the best performance; experiments reveal that longer contexts degrade performance, better instruction following does not guarantee factual accuracy, and hallucination is the primary failure mode across models, with significant performance variations across different domains.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于深度研究智能体（DRAs）的评估，这些智能体能够执行涉及规划、检索、多模态理解和引用支撑报告生成的复杂、长期研究任务。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有的DeepResearch Bench、DRBench和DeepResearchGym等基准测试，该论文提出了DR3-Eval，其创新之处在于将真实的多模态用户文件与受控静态沙盒语料库相结合，包含支持性文档、干扰项和噪声，并采用反向构建方法论以确保可验证的解决路径。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了如何在现实性、可控性和可重复性之间取得平衡来评估深度研究智能体的挑战，应对了动态网络环境、模糊任务定义以及现实研究复杂性与评估设置之间差距等问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该论文通过五阶段流程构建DR3-Eval（基于现实需求、提取搜索路径、构建研究沙盒、构建查询、质量控制），采用分层架构的多智能体系统（DR3-Agent），并使用多维评估框架，衡量信息召回率、事实准确性、引用覆盖率、指令遵循和深度质量。
5. 📊 结果与评估: DR3-Eval被证明极具挑战性，Claude Sonnet 4表现最佳；实验表明更长的上下文会导致性能下降，更好的指令遵循并不保证更高的事实准确性，幻觉是各模型的主要失败模式，且不同领域间存在显著的性能差异。
