Paper 1

Video-MME-v2: Towards the Next Stage in Benchmarks for Comprehensive Video Understanding

Published: 2026-04-05

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.05015

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on video understanding benchmarks for multimodal large language models (MLLMs), specifically evaluating the robustness and faithfulness of video MLLMs in comprehensive video comprehension tasks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior video MLLM benchmarks (Video-MME, MVBench, etc.), the paper introduces three key innovations: (1) a progressive tri-level capability hierarchy spanning information aggregation, temporal dynamics, and complex reasoning; (2) a group-based evaluation strategy assessing capability consistency and reasoning coherence; and (3) a non-linear scoring mechanism that penalizes fragmented correct answers and rewards consistent performance.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the widening gap between inflated leaderboard scores and real-world model capabilities in existing video understanding benchmarks, which overly rely on per-question accuracy without evaluating consistent and coherent comprehension across related queries.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors constructed Video-MME-v2 with 800 videos and 3,200 questions through a rigorous human annotation pipeline (12 annotators, 50 reviewers, 3,300 human-hours), implementing consistency-based groups for breadth evaluation and coherence-based groups for depth assessment, with non-linear scoring using quadratic rewards for consistency and first-error truncation for reasoning chains.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The benchmark reveals a substantial gap between the best model (Gemini-3-Pro at 49.4 Non-Lin Score) and human experts (90.7), uncovers hierarchical bottlenecks where errors in lower levels propagate to higher reasoning, and demonstrates that thinking modes improve performance with subtitles but can degrade it in visual-only settings, highlighting models' over-reliance on language priors.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于视频理解基准测试领域，针对多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）在全面视频理解任务中的鲁棒性和真实性评估。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有的视频MLLM基准测试（如Video-MME、MVBench等），论文提出三个创新点：(1)渐进式三级能力层次结构，涵盖信息聚合、时序动态和复杂推理；(2)基于组的评估策略，评估能力一致性和推理连贯性；(3)非线性评分机制，对碎片化正确答案进行惩罚，对一致表现给予奖励。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文旨在解决现有视频理解基准测试中排名分数虚高与实际模型能力之间的日益扩大差距，这些测试过度依赖单题准确率，忽视了对相关查询的一致性和连贯性评估。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者通过严格的众包标注流程（12名标注员、50名审核员、3300人时）构建了Video-MME-v2，包含800个视频和3200个问题，采用一致性组评估广度、连贯性组评估深度，并使用二次方评分和首错截断等非线性评分方法。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 基准测试显示最佳模型（Gemini-3-Pro得分49.4）与人类专家（90.7）之间存在巨大差距，揭示了底层错误会逐级传播至高层推理的层级瓶颈，并表明思维模式在有字幕时提升性能，但在纯视觉环境下可能降低性能，体现了模型对语言先验的过度依赖。

Claw-Eval: Toward Trustworthy Evaluation of Autonomous Agents

Published: 2026-04-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.06132

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on the evaluation of LLM-based autonomous agents capable of executing multi-step workflows in real-world software environments.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on existing agent benchmarks (AgentBench, GAIA, SWE-bench, WebArena, etc.) but introduces three novel contributions: full-trajectory auditing with three independent evidence channels (execution traces, audit logs, environment snapshots), integrated multi-dimensional scoring (Completion, Safety, Robustness), and unified cross-modal coverage across 300 tasks spanning General service orchestration, Multimodal perception/generation, and Multi-turn professional dialogue.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing agent benchmarks suffer from three critical limitations: trajectory-opaque grading that only checks final outputs (allowing reward hacking), underspecified safety and robustness evaluation (not under realistic goal-directed pressure), and narrow modality coverage (each benchmark targets only one modality or interaction paradigm).
4. 🛠️ Methods: Claw-Eval employs a three-phase execution lifecycle (Setup, Execution, Judge) in isolated Docker containers, records evidence through three independent channels, uses a fine-grained rubric system with 2,159 rubric items, evaluates three orthogonal dimensions (Completion, Safety as multiplicative gate, Robustness via controlled error injection), and reports three complementary metrics (Average Score, Pass@3, Pass^3) across 3 trials.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Experiments on 14 frontier models reveal that: trajectory-opaque evaluation misses 44% of safety violations and 13% of robustness failures; error injection primarily degrades consistency (Pass^3 drops up to 24%) while Pass@3 remains stable; question quality (r=0.87) predicts multi-turn success far better than conversation length (r=0.07); and no single model dominates all modalities, with video tasks being substantially harder than document/image or code tasks.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文专注于评估基于大型语言模型的自主智能体，这些智能体能够在真实软件环境中执行多步骤工作流程。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该论文基于现有的智能体基准测试（如AgentBench、GAIA、SWE-bench、WebArena等），但提出了三个创新贡献：全轨迹审计（通过执行追踪、审计日志、环境快照三个独立证据通道）、集成多维度评分（完成度、安全性、鲁棒性），以及统一跨模态覆盖（涵盖300个任务，包括通用服务编排、多模态感知与生成、多轮专业对话）。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有智能体基准测试存在三个关键局限：轨迹不透明的评分（仅检查最终输出，导致奖励黑客攻击）、安全性和鲁棒性评估不足（未在真实目标导向压力下评估）、模态覆盖范围窄（每个基准测试仅针对单一模态或交互范式）。
4. 🛠️ 方法: Claw-Eval采用三阶段执行生命周期（设置、执行、评判），在隔离的Docker容器中运行，通过三个独立通道记录证据，使用包含2,159个评分项的细粒度评分标准，从三个正交维度评估（完成度、安全性作为乘法门控、鲁棒性通过受控错误注入），并报告三个互补指标（平均分、Pass@3、Pass^3）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 对14个前沿模型的实验表明：轨迹不透明的评估会遗漏44%的安全违规和13%的鲁棒性问题；错误注入主要降低一致性（Pass^3下降高达24%）而Pass@3保持稳定；问题质量（r=0.87）比对话轮数（r=0.07）更能预测多轮对话成功；且没有单一模型在所有模态上均占主导，视频任务比文档/图像或代码任务难度大得多。

Beyond Accuracy: Unveiling Inefficiency Patterns in Tool-Integrated Reasoning

Published: 2026-04-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.05404

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses efficiency evaluation in Tool-Integrated Reasoning (TIR), where Large Language Models combine internal reasoning with external tool calls.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Previous TIR research focused on accuracy metrics and naive token/tool-call counts; the paper proposes PTE (Prefill Token Equivalents), a hardware-aware metric that unifies compute-bound prefill and memory-bound decode costs by modeling their asymmetric nature in TIR scenarios.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing efficiency metrics fail to capture real inference latency because they don't account for KV-Cache eviction during tool calls and the memory overhead from long tool responses that inflate context length and slow down subsequent decode steps.
4. 🛠️ Methods: PTE is calculated as the sum of prefill tokens plus a γ coefficient (ratio of decode to prefill cost) multiplied by cumulative sequence length and decode tokens, validated through high-concurrency experiments on an 8×H200 cluster across five TIR benchmarks.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: PTE shows strong correlation with wall-clock latency (r=0.9253) compared to token counts (r=-0.3750), maintains consistent model rankings across diverse hardware profiles (Spearman ρ>0.95), and reveals that higher PTE costs correlate with lower reasoning correctness, identifying four inefficiency patterns with cost multipliers ranging from 1.77× to 2.42×.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于工具集成推理（TIR）中的效率评估，其中大型语言模型结合内部推理与外部工具调用。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 先前研究主要关注准确率指标和简单的token/工具调用计数；本文提出PTE（预填充令牌当量），一种硬件感知的指标，通过建模TIR场景中计算密集型预填充与内存密集型解码的非对称成本来统一两者的度量。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有效率指标无法捕捉真实推理延迟，因为它们未考虑工具调用期间的KV-Cache驱逐，以及长工具响应导致上下文膨胀，从而拖慢后续解码步骤的内存开销。
4. 🛠️ 方法: PTE计算为预填充token之和加上γ系数（解码与预填充成本之比）乘以累积序列长度与解码token的乘积，通过8×H200集群的高并发实验在五个TIR基准测试上进行验证。
5. 📊 结果与评估: PTE与实际时钟时间高度相关（r=0.9253），远优于token计数（r=-0.3750），在不同硬件配置下保持一致模型排名（Spearman ρ>0.95），并揭示更高PTE成本与更低推理正确率的相关性，识别出四种低效模式，成本倍增系数从1.77×到2.42×不等。
