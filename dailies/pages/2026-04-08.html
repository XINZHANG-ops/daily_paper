Paper 1

Claw-Eval: Toward Trustworthy Evaluation of Autonomous Agents

Published: 2026-04-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.06132

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses trustworthy evaluation of LLM-based autonomous agents that execute multi-step workflows in real-world software environments.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing agent benchmarks (AgentBench, GAIA, τ-bench, SWE-bench, WebArena, OSWorld), the paper introduces Claw-Eval with three new design principles: full-trajectory auditing via three independent evidence channels, integrated multi-dimensional scoring (Completion, Safety, Robustness), and unified cross-modal coverage across 300 tasks.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing agent benchmarks suffer from three critical gaps: (1) trajectory-opaque grading that only checks final outputs and misses 44% of safety violations, (2) underspecified safety and robustness evaluation, and (3) narrow modality coverage limited to single interaction paradigms.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The framework uses a three-phase execution lifecycle (Setup, Execution, Judge) with isolated Docker containers, three evidence channels (execution traces, audit logs, environment snapshots), 2,159 fine-grained rubric items across 300 tasks spanning 9 categories and 3 groups, and multi-trial evaluation (k=3) reporting Average Score, Pass@3, and Pass^3.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Across 14 frontier models, experiments reveal that trajectory-opaque evaluation misses 44% of safety violations and 13% of robustness failures; error injection degrades Pass^3 by up to 24% while Pass@3 remains stable; question quality (r=0.87) predicts multi-turn success far better than quantity (r=0.07); and no single model dominates all modalities, with each domain (Video, Doc & Image, Code) led by a different model.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于可信赖的评估方法，旨在评估在真实软件环境中执行多步骤工作流程的基于大语言模型的自主代理。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有代理基准测试（AgentBench、GAIA、τ-bench、SWE-bench、WebArena、OSWorld），论文引入了Claw-Eval，包含三个新设计原则：通过三条独立证据通道实现全轨迹审计、整合式多维度评分（完成度、安全性、鲁棒性），以及跨300个任务的统一跨模态覆盖。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有代理基准测试存在三个关键缺陷：(1) 仅检查最终输出的轨迹不透明评分，遗漏44%的安全违规；(2) 安全性和鲁棒性评估不足；(3) 模态覆盖范围窄，仅限于单一交互范式。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该框架采用三阶段执行生命周期（设置、执行、评判），使用隔离的Docker容器、三条证据通道（执行轨迹、审计日志、环境快照）、300个任务中的2159个细粒度评分项（涵盖9个类别和3个分组），以及多轮评估协议（k=3）报告平均分、Pass@3和Pass^3。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在14个前沿模型的实验中发现：轨迹不透明评估遗漏44%的安全违规和13%的鲁棒性问题；错误注入使Pass^3下降高达24%而Pass@3保持稳定；问题质量（r=0.87）比问题数量（r=0.07）更能预测多轮对话成功；没有单一模型在所有模态中均占主导，每个领域（视频、文档与图像、代码）由不同模型领先。

Beyond Accuracy: Unveiling Inefficiency Patterns in Tool-Integrated Reasoning

Published: 2026-04-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.05404

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses efficiency evaluation in Tool-Integrated Reasoning (TIR) for Large Language Models, focusing on the asymmetry between prefill (compute-bound) and decode (memory-bound) phases during inference.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing TIR benchmarks and naive metrics like token/toolcall counts, the paper introduces PTE (Prefill Token Equivalents), a hardware-aware metric that models the physical costs of transformer inference including KV-Cache eviction and long tool responses.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing efficiency metrics fail to reflect true runtime latency because they ignore KV-Cache eviction during tool-call pauses and the memory-bound decode overhead that grows with cumulative context length.
4. 🛠️ Methods: PTE quantifies total inference cost as the sum of prefill tokens and the product of a coefficient γ (ratio of decode-to-prefill costs), cumulative sequence length, and decode tokens, validated across 5 benchmarks and multiple hardware profiles.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: PTE achieves r=0.925 correlation with wall-clock latency (vs r=-0.375 for token counts), reveals 4 inefficiency patterns with 1.77×-2.42× cost multipliers, and shows that trajectories with higher PTE tend to have lower reasoning correctness.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文探讨了大语言模型在工具集成推理（TIR）中的效率评估，重点关注推理过程中预填充阶段（计算受限）和解码阶段（内存受限）之间的不对称性。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有的TIR基准测试和简单的token数量、工具调用次数等指标，论文提出了PTE（预填充 Token 等价物），这是一种考虑硬件因素的效率指标，综合考虑KV-Cache驱逐和长工具响应带来的物理计算成本。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有效率指标无法反映实际运行延迟，因为它们忽视了工具调用暂停期间的KV-Cache驱逐问题，以及随累积上下文长度增长而加剧的内存受限解码开销。
4. 🛠️ 方法: PTE将总推理成本量化为预填充token数与系数γ（解码与预填充成本之比）、累积序列长度和解码token数乘积之和，在5个基准测试和多种硬件配置上进行了验证。
5. 📊 结果与评估: PTE与墙钟延迟的相关性达到r=0.925（而token数量仅为r=-0.375），揭示了4种效率低下的模式，其成本增幅达1.77×至2.42×，并表明PTE越高的轨迹往往推理正确率越低。

Paper 3

Self-Distilled RLVR

Published: 2026-04-02

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.03128

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards (RLVR) for training large language models on multimodal reasoning tasks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on on-policy self-distillation (OPSD), the paper proposes RLSD, which repurposes self-distillation from distribution matching to token-level credit assignment while anchoring update directions to environment rewards.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the failure of OPSD, which suffers from privileged information leakage and performance degradation due to an irreducible mutual information gap caused by information asymmetry between teacher and student.
4. 🛠️ Methods: RLSD uses the privileged information gain (ratio of teacher to student log-probabilities) to modulate token-level update magnitudes, while using the environment reward to determine update directions, combined with clipped credit assignment and a mixing coefficient schedule.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: RLSD achieves the highest average accuracy across five multimodal reasoning benchmarks (56.18%), outperforming the base LLM by 4.69% and GRPO by 2.32%, with faster convergence and sustained improvement without degradation.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于强化学习与可验证奖励（RLVR）用于训练大语言模型在多模态推理任务上的应用。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于同策略自蒸馏（OPSD）的研究，该论文提出RLSD，将自蒸馏从分布匹配重新定位为令牌级信用分配，同时将更新方向锚定在环境奖励上。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了OPSD的失败问题，OPSD由于师生之间的信息不对称导致特权信息泄漏和性能退化，这是由不可约的互信息差距引起的。
4. 🛠️ 方法: RLSD使用特权信息增益（教师与学生对数概率的比值）来调节令牌级更新幅度，同时使用环境奖励来确定更新方向，结合截断信用分配和混合系数调度。
5. 📊 结果与评估: RLSD在五个多模态推理基准上达到最高平均准确率（56.18%），分别优于基线LLM 4.69%和优于GRPO 2.32%，具有更快的收敛速度和持续的改进而无退化。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with Claw‑Eval, as it defines the core evaluation gaps (44 % missed safety violations, etc.) that any future agent work—including the efficiency analysis in PTE and the training improvements in RLSD—must address. PTE’s hardware‑aware cost metric can be directly plugged into Claw‑Eval’s scoring to give a more realistic measure of agent performance, while RLSD demonstrates a training technique that can raise scores on the very benchmarks Claw‑Eval introduces. Reading them in this order links evaluation, efficiency, and training into a coherent workflow.
先读Claw‑Eval，了解LLM代理评估的核心缺陷（安全违规遗漏44%等），这些缺陷是后续研究必须解决的问题。PTE的硬件感知成本指标可以直接集成到Claw‑Eval的评分体系中，提供更真实的性能评估；而RLSD则展示了一种能够提升Claw‑Eval所考察任务得分的训练方法。三篇论文形成从评估、效率到训练的完整闭环。
