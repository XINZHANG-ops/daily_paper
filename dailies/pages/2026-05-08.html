Paper 1

OpenSearch-VL: An Open Recipe for Frontier Multimodal Search Agents

Published: 2026-05-05

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.05185

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper introduces OpenSearch-VL, a fully open-source recipe for training frontier multimodal deep search agents capable of active evidence search and multi-step reasoning over visual and textual content.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on multimodal LLMs, search-augmented agents (e.g., Search-R1), and GRPO, it proposes a transparent Wikipedia-based data curation pipeline, a diverse tool environment unifying retrieval with active visual perception, and a fatal-aware GRPO algorithm that preserves useful pre-failure reasoning via one-sided advantage clamping.
3. ❓ Problem: It solves the reproducibility crisis of top-tier multimodal search agents caused by proprietary data and training recipes, along with the challenges of cascading tool failures and degraded real-world visual inputs in long-horizon agentic RL.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method comprises curated multi-hop VQA data via Wikipedia path sampling and fuzzy rewriting, a unified tool suite for retrieval and image enhancement, and two-stage training via SFT followed by multi-turn fatal-aware GRPO with composite rewards.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Results show over 10-point average gains across seven benchmarks (e.g., 47.8→61.6 for 30B-A3B), achieving competitive or superior performance compared to proprietary models like Gemini-2.5-Pro and GPT-4o.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文介绍了OpenSearch-VL，一个用于训练能够主动搜索证据并对视觉与文本内容进行多步推理的前沿多模态深度搜索代理的完全开源方案。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于多模态大模型、搜索增强代理和GRPO，本文提出了基于Wikipedia的透明数据整理流程、融合检索与主动视觉感知的多样化工具环境，以及通过单侧优势钳制保留失败前有效推理的致命感知GRPO算法。
3. ❓ 问题: 本文解决了顶级多模态搜索代理因数据与训练配方闭源而难以复现的瓶颈，以及长程智能体强化学习中工具级联失败与真实世界视觉输入退化的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包括通过Wikipedia路径采样与模糊重写构建高质量多跳VQA数据、设计涵盖检索与图像增强的统一工具集，以及通过监督微调与多回合致命感知GRPO（含复合奖励）进行两阶段训练。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 实验结果显示，在七个基准测试中平均提升超过10分（30B-A3B模型从47.8提升至61.6），并在多个任务上达到或超过Gemini-2.5-Pro和GPT-4o等专有商业模型的性能。

Published: 2026-05-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06416

MARBLE: Multi-Aspect Reward Balance for Diffusion RL

Published: 2026-05-06

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.06507

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses multi-objective reinforcement learning fine-tuning for text-to-image diffusion models in the computer vision and generative AI domain.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building upon diffusion RL methods (e.g., DiffusionNFT, FlowGRPO) and multi-task gradient harmonization techniques, the paper proposes MARBLE, which replaces scalar reward aggregation with per-reward advantage decomposition and quadratic-programming-based gradient balancing.
3. ❓ Problem: It solves the "specialist sample" problem where naive weighted-sum reward aggregation dilutes supervision signals because individual rollout samples are typically informative for only a subset of reward dimensions, causing gradient misalignment and performance degradation in multi-reward diffusion RL.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method computes normalized per-reward policy gradients from independent advantage estimators, harmonizes them via a convex quadratic program into a single update direction, and uses an amortized formulation exploiting the affine NFT loss structure together with EMA-smoothed balancing coefficients to reduce computational overhead and stabilize training.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Evaluated on SD3.5 Medium with five rewards, MARBLE simultaneously improves all reward dimensions, turns the worst-reward gradient cosine from negative in 80% of mini-batches under weighted summation to consistently positive, achieves the highest composite score across eight metrics, and trains at 0.97× the baseline speed.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文属于计算机视觉与生成式AI领域，研究文本到图像扩散模型的多目标强化学习微调。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于扩散强化学习（如DiffusionNFT、FlowGRPO）与多任务梯度协调技术，论文提出MARBLE框架，以逐奖励优势分解和二次规划梯度协调替代标量奖励聚合。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了“专家样本”导致的加权求和奖励聚合失效问题：单个样本往往仅对部分奖励维度有信息量，加权会稀释监督信号并引发梯度冲突，导致多奖励扩散RL性能下降。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法为每维奖励维护独立优势估计器，计算归一化的逐奖励策略梯度，通过求解凸二次规划得到统一更新方向，并利用DiffusionNFT损失的仿射结构实现摊销计算，结合EMA平滑系数以降低开销、稳定训练。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在SD3.5 Medium的五个奖励上，MARBLE同时提升所有维度，将加权求和下80%小批量中最差奖励的负梯度余弦转为持续正值，在八项指标的综合评分中排名第一，且摊销版本训练速度达基线的0.97倍。
