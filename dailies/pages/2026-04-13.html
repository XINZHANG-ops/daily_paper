Paper 1

Rethinking Generalization in Reasoning SFT: A Conditional Analysis on Optimization, Data, and Model Capability

Published: 2026-04-07

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.06628

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper investigates cross-domain generalization in supervised fine-tuning (SFT) for long chain-of-thought (CoT) reasoning tasks, falling within LLM post-training, reasoning, and generalization research.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior work showing "SFT memorizes, RL generalizes" and limited cross-domain transfer in math reasoning SFT. The new insight is that cross-domain generalization in reasoning SFT is **conditional** rather than absent—it depends jointly on three factors: (1) optimization sufficiency (sufficient training epochs), (2) data quality and structure (verified long-CoT traces with procedural reasoning patterns), and (3) base-model capability.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses why previous studies found weak cross-domain generalization from reasoning SFT, investigating under what conditions generalization occurs and at what cost (safety degradation).
4. 🛠️ Methods: Systematic experiments varying: (1) **Optimization**: training duration, learning rates, schedules (cosine decay vs constant LR); (2) **Data**: long-CoT vs no-CoT, verified vs unverified answers, procedural Countdown data vs domain-specific math data; (3) **Model capability**: comparing model sizes (1.7B to 14B). Evaluations span in-domain math benchmarks (MATH500, AIME24), OOD reasoning (LCB v2, GPQA-D), general capabilities (MMLU-Pro, IFEval), and safety (HEx-PHI).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Key findings: (1) Cross-domain generalization emerges after extended training following a "dip-and-recovery" dynamic; short-epoch checkpoints underestimate gains. (2) Verified long-CoT data with procedural reasoning patterns yields stronger transfer than unverified or no-CoT data. (3) Higher-capability models internalize transferable reasoning patterns (backtracking, self-correction) more effectively than smaller models that only imitate verbosity. (4) Generalization is asymmetric—reasoning gains consistently accompany safety degradation due to procedural patterns enabling workarounds of safety guardrails.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文研究长链式思维（CoT）推理任务的监督微调（SFT）中的跨域泛化，属于大语言模型后训练、推理与泛化研究领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于先前"SFT记忆化、RL泛化"的研究结论及数学推理SFT跨域迁移有限的发现。创新点在于：推理SFT的跨域泛化是**有条件的**而非缺失的，它取决于三个因素的共同作用：（1）优化充分性（足够的训练轮次）；（2）数据质量与结构（经验证的长CoT轨迹与程序性推理模式）；（3）基础模型能力。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文探讨为何先前研究发现在推理SFT中跨域泛化能力较弱，研究泛化在何种条件下发生，以及伴随的代价（安全性下降）。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 系统性实验变量包括：（1）**优化策略**：训练时长、学习率、调度方式（余弦退火vs常数学习率）；（2）**数据**：长CoT vs 无CoT、经验证 vs 未验证答案、程序性Countdown数据 vs 领域特定数学数据；（3）**模型能力**：比较不同规模模型（1.7B到14B）。评估涵盖领域内数学基准（MATH500、AIME24）、域外推理（LCB v2、GPQA-D）、通用能力（MMLU-Pro、IFEval）及安全性（HEx-PHI）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 核心发现：（1）跨域泛化在延长训练后显现，遵循"先降后恢复"的动态模式，短期检查点会低估最终收益；（2）经验证的含程序性推理模式的长CoT数据比未验证或无CoT数据产生更强的迁移；（3）高能力模型比仅模仿冗长形式的小模型更有效地内化可迁移的推理模式（回溯、自我修正）；（4）泛化具有非对称性——推理提升始终伴随安全性下降，因为程序性模式使绕过安全护栏成为可能。

Paper 2

WildDet3D: Scaling Promptable 3D Detection in the Wild

Published: 2026-04-08

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.08626

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses open-vocabulary monocular 3D object detection for general-purpose spatial understanding in real-world environments.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior open-vocabulary 3D detection work (3D-MOOD, DetAny3D, Omni3D) and promptable visual models (SAM, CLIP); introduces a unified promptable architecture accepting text/point/box prompts with optional depth input, and curates WildDet3D-Data with 138× more categories than existing benchmarks.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing monocular 3D detectors are limited to single prompt types, lack mechanisms to incorporate depth cues, and current datasets cover only narrow categories, hindering open-world generalization.
4. 🛠️ Methods: WildDet3D employs dual-vision encoders (image + RGBD) with a depth fusion module, a promptable detector supporting text/point/box/exemplar prompts, and a deeply-supervised 3D detection head with unambiguous rotation normalization; WildDet3D-Data is built via multi-model candidate generation followed by VLM and human selection.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: WildDet3D achieves 34.2 AP3D on Omni3D (text prompts) and 36.4 AP3D (oracle), 22.6 AP3D on the new WildDet3D-Bench, 40.3/48.9 ODS zero-shot on Argoverse 2/ScanNet, with +20.7 AP average gain when depth is available—all with 6–10× fewer training epochs than prior methods.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于开放词汇的单目3D目标检测，旨在实现真实场景下的通用空间理解能力。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于先前开放词汇3D检测工作（3D-MOOD、DetAny3D、Omni3D）和可提示视觉模型（SAM、CLIP）；创新性地提出统一架构支持文本/点/框提示并可选融合深度信息，并构建了包含比现有基准多138倍类别的WildDet3D-Data数据集。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有单目3D检测器仅支持单一提示类型、缺乏深度线索整合机制，且数据集类别覆盖有限，难以泛化至开放世界场景。
4. 🛠️ 方法: WildDet3D采用双视觉编码器（图像+RGBD）配合深度融合模块、可提示检测器支持四类提示、以及具有明确旋转归一化的深度监督3D检测头；WildDet3D-Data通过多模型候选生成后经VLM和人工筛选构建。
5. 📊 结果与评估: WildDet3D在Omni3D上达到34.2 AP3D（文本提示）和36.4 AP3D（oracle），在WildDet3D-Bench上达22.6 AP3D，零样本迁移至Argoverse 2/ScanNet达40.3/48.9 ODS，提供深度时平均提升+20.7 AP，且训练轮数仅为先前方法的1/6至1/10。

LPM 1.0: Video-based Character Performance Model

Published: 2026-04-08

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.07823

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents LPM 1.0, a video-based character performance model for single-person full-duplex audio-visual conversational performance in interactive AI agents, live streaming, and game NPCs.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on diffusion-based video generation models, the paper introduces the "performance trilemma" (expressiveness, real-time inference, long-horizon identity stability) and proposes a full-stack framework combining a large-scale multimodal dataset, a 17B-parameter Diffusion Transformer (Base LPM), and a distilled causal streaming generator (Online LPM).
3. ❓ Problem: Existing video models struggle to jointly achieve expressive quality, real-time inference, and long-horizon identity stability, lacking listening behavior, multimodal controllability, sufficient character specification, and a human-centric video foundation model.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method includes building a curated multimodal dataset with speaking-listening audio-video pairing and identity-aware multi-reference extraction, training a 17B-parameter Base LPM with interleaved speak/listen audio injection and multi-identity conditioning, and distilling it into an Online LPM using a four-stage autoregressive curriculum with backbone-refiner architecture.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: LPM 1.0 achieves state-of-the-art results, with Base LPM (720P) preferred over Kling-Avatar-2 (64.3%) and OmniHuman-1.5 (42.5%), and Online LPM (480P) preferred over LiveAvatar (82.5%) and SoulX (64.1%), while maintaining real-time inference and identity stability over extended durations.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文提出LPM 1.0，一个用于单人全双工视听对话表演的视频角色性能模型，应用于交互式AI代理、直播角色和游戏NPC。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于扩散视频生成模型，论文引入"表演三难困境"（表现力、实时推理、长期身份稳定性），并提出完整框架：大规模多模态数据集、170亿参数扩散Transformer（Base LPM）和蒸馏后的因果流式生成器（Online LPM）。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有视频模型难以同时实现高质量表现力、实时推理和长期身份稳定性，缺乏倾听行为建模、多模态可控性、充足的角色规范和以人为中心的视频基础模型。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法包括构建包含说话-倾听音视频配对和身份感知多参考提取的精选多模态数据集，训练具有交替说话/倾听音频注入和多身份条件的170亿参数Base LPM，以及使用四阶段自回归课程和骨干-细化器架构将其蒸馏为Online LPM。
5. 📊 结果与评估: LPM 1.0达到最先进水平，Base LPM（720P）相比Kling-Avatar-2（64.3%）和OmniHuman-1.5（42.5%）更受青睐，Online LPM（480P）相比LiveAvatar（82.5%）和SoulX（64.1%）更受青睐，同时保持实时推理和长期身份稳定性。
