WorldMark: A Unified Benchmark Suite for Interactive Video World Models

Published: 2026-04-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21686

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses benchmarking interactive Image-to-Video (I2V) world models that function as interactive environments responding to user actions and camera controls.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing interactive world models (Genie, YUME, HY-World, Matrix-Game) and evaluation benchmarks (VBench, WorldScore, MIND), the paper introduces WorldMark as the first unified benchmark providing standardized scenes, action sequences, and a control-interface translation layer for cross-model comparison.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the fragmented evaluation problem where each interactive world model uses proprietary control interfaces, scenes, and evaluation protocols, making fair cross-model comparison impossible—no existing benchmark supplies standardized test conditions for interactive models.
4. 🛠️ Methods: WorldMark uses a unified action-mapping layer that translates shared WASD+L/R action vocabulary into each model's native format, a hierarchical test suite of 500 cases across 50 images with 15 action sequences, and a modular evaluation toolkit assessing Visual Quality, Control Alignment, and World Consistency.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Evaluations across six models reveal that visual quality and world consistency are largely uncorrelated, Genie 3 dominates world consistency while YUME 1.5 leads in visual quality, third-person view degrades rotation error by ~20x compared to first-person, and automated metrics achieve ρ>0.9 correlation with human preferences.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于交互式图像到视频（I2V）世界模型的基准测试，这些模型作为交互式环境，能够响应用户动作和相机控制。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有的交互式世界模型（Genie、YUME、HY-World、Matrix-Game）和评估基准（VBench、WorldScore、MIND），论文提出WorldMark——首个统一基准测试，通过标准化场景、动作序列和控制接口翻译层实现跨模型比较。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了当前碎片化评估问题：每个交互式世界模型使用专有的控制接口、场景和评估协议，导致公平跨模型比较不可行——现有基准均未提供交互式模型的标准化测试条件。
4. 🛠️ 方法: WorldMark采用统一动作映射层将共享的WASD+L/R动作词汇翻译为各模型原生格式，构建包含50张图像和15种动作序列的500个评估案例层次化测试套件，以及评估视觉质量、控制对齐和世界一致性的模块化评估工具包。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 对六个模型的评估表明，视觉质量与世界一致性高度不相关，Genie 3在世界一致性上领先而YUME 1.5在视觉质量上领先，第三人称视角的旋转误差比第一人称恶化约20倍，自动评估指标与人类偏好达到ρ>0.9的相关性。

StyleID: A Perception-Aware Dataset and Metric for Stylization-Agnostic Facial Identity Recognition

Published: 2026-04-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.21689

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on stylization‑agnostic facial identity recognition across diverse artistic rendering styles, situated in computer vision and face recognition. 2. **💡 Previous Research and New Ideas:** It builds on existing face‑recognition encoders and introduces perception‑calibrated datasets (StyleBench‑H and StyleBench‑S) along with a style‑robust identity model (StyleID) trained via synthetic supervision aligned with human judgments. 3. **❓ Problem:** It addresses the brittleness of current identity encoders that misjudge identity on stylized portraits due to texture, color, and geometric variations. 4. **🛠️ Methods:** The authors construct perception‑aligned benchmarks, generate synthetic training data using psychometric curves, fine‑tune a CLIP‑based encoder with LoRA adapters and angular‑margin plus contrastive losses, and evaluate with verification metrics. 5. **📊 Results and Evaluation:** StyleID consistently outperforms baselines on StyleBench‑H and SKSF‑A, achieving higher true‑positive rates and AUROC, better alignment with human perception, and serves as a more reliable identity loss for stylization pipelines.

Start with WorldMark as it introduces a unified benchmark for interactive image‑to‑video world models, establishing standardized test conditions and evaluation protocols that can guide future research in generative vision tasks. The other two papers (StyleID and DeVI) share a focus on synthetic supervision and human‑aligned metrics, echoing WorldMark's emphasis on automated evaluation that correlates with human preferences.
建议先阅读WorldMark，因为它提出了首个统一的交互式图像到视频世界模型基准，提供标准化测试场景和评价协议，可为后续的风格化人脸识别和手部交互动作生成研究提供方法论参考。另外，StyleID和DeVI同样采用合成监督和人类对齐评价，与WorldMark的评估框架在理念上相呼应。
