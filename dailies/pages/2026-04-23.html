Paper 1

Near-Future Policy Optimization

Published: 2026-04-21

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.20733

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses reinforcement learning with verifiable rewards (RLVR) for reasoning models, specifically focusing on mixed-policy optimization and post-training strategies for multimodal reasoning.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing mixed-policy RLVR approaches that either import external teacher trajectories or replay historical trajectories, this paper proposes Near-Future Policy Optimization (NPO), which uses a near-future checkpoint from the same training run as a natural source of auxiliary trajectories that simultaneously satisfies the strong enough and close enough conditions.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the challenge of finding optimal auxiliary trajectories for RLVR—existing methods either have high quality but high variance (external teachers) or low variance but limited quality (historical replay), neither achieving the optimal trade-off between signal quality Q and variance cost V to maximize learning signal S=Q/V.
4. 🛠️ Methods: NPO modifies rollout groups by replacing one trajectory slot with a verified-correct trajectory from a near-future checkpoint Δ steps ahead of the current policy; AutoNPO further automates this by monitoring training signals (reward stagnation, entropy decline) to trigger interventions and select the checkpoint distance that maximizes the empirical effective signal Ŝ(Δ).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: On Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct with GRPO, NPO improves average multi-modal reasoning performance from 57.88 to 62.84 (+4.96), and AutoNPO further reaches 63.15 (+5.27), demonstrating ~2.1× faster convergence and higher performance ceiling across eight benchmarks including MMMU-Pro, MathVista, and MathVision.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文涉及可验证奖励的强化学习（RLVR），专注于推理模型的后训练策略，具体为多模态推理中的混合策略优化。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有的混合策略RLVR方法（外部教师轨迹或历史重放），本文提出近未来策略优化（NPO），利用同一训练运行中稍后的检查点作为辅助轨迹的自然来源，同时满足"足够强"和"足够近"的条件。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决的核心挑战是如何为RLVR找到最优的辅助轨迹——现有方法要么质量高但方差大（外部教师），要么方差小但质量受限（历史重放），都无法实现信号质量Q与方差成本V之间的最优权衡以最大化学习信号S=Q/V。
4. 🛠️ 方法: NPO通过用当前策略未来Δ步检查点的已验证正确轨迹替换轮换组中的一个轨迹槽来实现改进；AutoNPO进一步自动化这一过程，通过监控训练信号（奖励停滞、熵下降）触发干预，并选择最大化经验有效信号Ŝ(Δ)的检查点距离。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在Qwen3-VL-8B-Instruct上使用GRPO，NPO将多模态推理的平均性能从57.88提升至62.84（+4.96），AutoNPO进一步达到63.15（+5.27），在MMMU-Pro、MathVista、MathVision等八个基准测试上实现了约2.1倍的收敛加速和更高的性能上限。

SmartPhotoCrafter: Unified Reasoning, Generation and Optimization for Automatic Photographic Image Editing

Published: 2026-04-20

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.19587

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses automatic photographic image editing, situated at the intersection of computational photography, image quality assessment, and generative AI using multimodal large language models and diffusion models.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds upon automatic image editing with MLLMs and diffusion models, image quality assessment methods, and image retouching techniques. Its novel contribution is a tightly coupled reasoning-to-generation framework that combines an MLLM-based Image Critic for aesthetic analysis with a Photographic Artist for high-fidelity editing, trained through a multi-stage pipeline including coordinated reinforcement learning.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the challenge of enabling automatic photographic enhancement without explicit user instructions, addressing the limitations of existing methods that require users to know what and how to edit, and lack the ability to perceive image quality deficiencies and reason about improvement strategies.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The method uses a unified reasoning-to-generation architecture with two modules: an Image Critic for interpreting image quality through chain-of-thought reasoning, edit suggestions, and quality scoring; and a Photographic Artist for generating enhanced images conditioned on reasoning latents. Training involves three stages: foundation pretraining, reasoning-conditioned adaptation, and coordinated RL with multi-level rewards (semantic compliance, photometric control, and perceptual consistency).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The method achieves state-of-the-art performance on automatic photographic enhancement, outperforming baselines across metrics including DINO (0.98), CLIP (0.96), FID (27.96), and LPIPS (0.10). It also demonstrates superior multi-edit instruction adherence (PSNR 21.05, SSIM 0.82) and image restoration tasks (deblurring and dehazing), with ablations confirming the effectiveness of the photometric control reward design.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦自动摄影图像编辑，属于计算摄影、图像质量评估与生成式AI的交叉领域，主要利用多模态大语言模型和扩散模型技术。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 研究基于自动图像编辑、多模态大语言模型的图像质量评估及图像修图技术。创新之处在于提出了一个紧密耦合的"推理到生成"框架，结合基于MLLM的图像评论家进行美学分析，与摄影艺术家进行高保真图像编辑，并通过多阶段训练管道（含协调强化学习）进行优化。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了如何实现无需显式用户指令即可自动进行摄影增强的挑战，克服了现有方法需要用户明确知道"编辑什么"和"如何编辑"的局限性，以及缺乏感知图像质量缺陷和推理改进策略能力的不足。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 方法采用统一的"推理到生成"架构，包含两个模块：图像评论家用于通过思维链推理、编辑建议和质量评分来解读图像质量；摄影艺术家用于基于推理潜变量条件生成增强图像。训练分为三个阶段：基础预训练、推理条件适配及协调强化学习，采用多层次奖励设计（语义合规、光度控制及感知一致性）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 该方法在自动摄影增强任务上达到最优性能，在DINO（0.98）、CLIP（0.96）、FID（27.96）和LPIPS（0.10）等指标上优于基线模型。同时在多编辑指令遵循（PSNR 21.05、SSIM 0.82）和图像恢复任务（去模糊、去雾）中表现优异，消融实验验证了光度控制奖励设计的有效性。
