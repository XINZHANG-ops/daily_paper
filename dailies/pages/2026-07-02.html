Paper 1

Agentic Abstention: Do Agents Know When to Stop Instead of Act?

Published: 2026-06-26

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.28733

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper studies agentic AI systems, focusing on when LLM-based tool-using agents should stop acting and abstain under uncertainty across web shopping, terminal, and question-answering scenarios.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on prior work in single-turn LLM abstention (AbstentionBench, MARVEL), agent benchmarks (WebShop, Terminal-Bench), and context engineering (ACE, Reflexion), while introducing the new concept of "Agentic Abstention" as a sequential decision problem in multi-turn interactive environments.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of agent judgment in recognizing infeasible tasks, where agents often continue taking unnecessary actions instead of abstaining, leading to wasted resources, lower efficiency, and potentially misleading outputs.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors formalize agentic abstention as a POMDP, construct a benchmark of over 28,000 tasks spanning web, terminal, and QA settings, evaluate 13 LLM-as-agent systems with 2 scaffolds using AbsRec@K and SPL metrics, and propose CONVOLVE, a context engineering method that distills interaction trajectories into reusable stopping rules.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The evaluation reveals that most agents achieve less than 50% overall abstention recall and less than 40% timely recall; CONVOLVE substantially improves timely abstention on WebShop (raising Llama-3.3-70B's AbsRec@1 from 26.7 to 57.4 without updating model parameters), and the study shows that model scale, reasoning effort, and agent scaffolds each affect abstention behavior in different, sometimes counterintuitive ways.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本论文研究智能体AI系统，聚焦于基于LLM的工具使用智能体在不确定情境下应何时停止行动并选择弃答的问题，涵盖网页购物、终端和问答三个场景。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于单轮LLM弃答研究（AbstentionBench、MARVEL）、智能体基准（WebShop、Terminal-Bench）以及上下文工程（ACE、Reflexion）等方面的前期工作，并创新性地提出"智能体弃答（Agentic Abstention）"概念，将其作为多轮交互环境中的序贯决策问题。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了智能体缺乏对不可行任务的判断能力的问题——智能体在任务不可行时常常继续执行不必要的操作，导致资源浪费、效率降低以及可能产生误导性输出。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者将智能体弃答形式化为POMDP，构建了涵盖网页、终端和问答场景的28,000余条任务基准，使用AbsRec@K和SPL等指标评估了13个LLM智能体系统和2个脚手架，并提出了CONVOLVE方法——一种将交互轨迹提炼为可复用停止规则的上下文工程技术。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 评估显示大多数智能体的整体弃答召回率低于50%，及时弃答召回率低于40%；CONVOLVE在WebShop上显著提升了及时弃答性能（将Llama-3.3-70B的AbsRec@1从26.7提升至57.4，且无需更新模型参数），研究表明模型规模、推理能力和智能体脚手架均以不同方式影响弃答行为，部分影响呈现反直觉的特征。

Paper 2

MemSyco-Bench: Benchmarking Sycophancy in Agent Memory

Published: 2026-06-30

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.01071

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on benchmarking sycophancy (memory-induced sycophancy) in long-term memory systems of LLM-based agents, within the domain of AI agent evaluation and natural language processing.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on prior LLM sycophancy research (Sharma et al., 2024; Malmqvist, 2025) and existing memory benchmarks (LongMemEval, LoCoMo, STALE, PersonaMem), introducing the new concept of "memory-induced sycophancy" where retrieved historical memories improperly influence agent reasoning beyond the current interaction.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the underexplored failure mode where retrieved memories cause agents to over-align with historical user beliefs/preferences at the cost of factual accuracy, since existing memory benchmarks only evaluate retrieval success rather than post-retrieval memory-use decisions.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes MemSyco-Bench, constructed via a four-step pipeline (memory-decision schema construction, question instantiation, multi-turn dialogue simulation, and multi-stage quality validation) with five task categories: Objective Fact Judgment, Contextual Scope Control, Memory-Evidence Conflict, Valid Memory Selection, and Personalized Memory Use.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Experiments on seven memory systems (Mem0, A-Mem, LightMem, MemGPT, MemoryBank, SuperMemory, NaiveRAG) show that 61–62% of errors occur after relevant memory is successfully retrieved, and current memory systems often increase sycophancy rates and degrade accuracy compared to no-memory or full-dialogue baselines.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于LLM智能体长期记忆系统中的谄媚性（记忆诱发的谄媚）基准测试，属于AI智能体评估与自然语言处理领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该研究基于现有的LLM谄媚性研究（Sharma等人，2024；Malmqvist，2025）和记忆基准（LongMemEval、LoCoMo、STALE、PersonaMem），提出了"记忆诱发谄媚性"的新概念，即检索到的历史记忆在当前交互之外不当影响智能体推理。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了检索到的记忆使智能体过度迎合用户历史信念/偏好而牺牲事实准确性的未被充分探索的失败模式，因为现有记忆基准仅评估检索成功与否，而未评估检索后的记忆使用决策。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该论文提出了MemSyco-Bench，通过四步流水线（记忆决策模式构建、问题实例化、多轮对话模拟和多阶段质量验证）构建，包含五个任务类别：客观事实判断、上下文范围控制、记忆-证据冲突、有效记忆选择和个性化记忆使用。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在七种记忆系统（Mem0、A-Mem、LightMem、MemGPT、MemoryBank、SuperMemory、NaiveRAG）上的实验表明，61–62%的错误发生在相关记忆被成功检索之后，且当前记忆系统常常增加谄媚性比率并降低准确率，相较于无记忆或全对话基线表现更差。

Paper 3

ELDR: Expert-Locality-Aware Decode Routing for PD-Disaggregated MoE Serving

Published: 2026-06-30

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2607.00466

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on LLM inference serving systems, specifically expert-locality-aware decode routing for prefill-decode (PD) disaggregated serving of Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) models.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on prior work in PD-disaggregated LLM serving, expert parallelism load balancing, and MoE expert specialization, and introduces the novel idea of using prefill-derived expert signatures to route decode requests by expert locality (not just load) to reduce per-step active-expert counts.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing decode routers only balance load across workers, but for MoE models, equally loaded workers can have very different latencies because MoE decode cost is dominated by the union of distinct experts a batch activates, which depends on request composition (expert locality) rather than just request count.
4. 🛠️ Methods: ELDR constructs an expert signature from each request's prefill expert activations (using IDF-reweighted top-K counts over a greedy-selected layer subset), partitions signature space offline into one centroid per decode worker via Hungarian-balanced K-means, routes online by selecting the least-loaded worker within a similarity band of the best centroid, and maintains a block-granular signature cache co-indexed with the KV cache to stay coherent under prefix caching.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Implemented in vLLM and evaluated on up to 40 GPUs across three MoE models and two workloads, ELDR reduces median TPOT by 5.9–13.9% over the strongest load-balancing baseline with outputs unchanged, while also generalizing to large MoE deployments with expert parallelism and composing additively with prefix caching at under 1% KV cache overhead.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于LLM推理服务系统，具体研究面向专家混合（MoE）模型的预填充-解码（PD）分离架构中的专家局部性感知的解码路由。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于PD分离LLM服务、专家并行负载均衡和MoE专家特化等已有研究，提出新思路：利用从预填充阶段派生的专家签名（而非仅负载）来路由解码请求，通过专家局部性缩小每步激活专家数。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有解码路由器仅在工作节点间做负载均衡，但对MoE模型而言，负载相同的工作节点延迟可能差异很大，因为MoE解码耗时由批次激活的不同专家集合的并集决定（取决于请求组合的专家局部性），而不仅仅是请求数量。
4. 🛠️ 方法: ELDR从每个请求的预填充专家激活构建专家签名（采用IDF加权的top-K计数并基于贪心选择的层子集），离线通过匈牙利平衡K均值将签名空间划分为每个解码工作节点一个质心，在线通过在最佳质心相似度带内选择负载最低的工作节点进行路由，并通过与KV缓存共索引的块粒度签名缓存在前缀缓存下保持一致性。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在vLLM中实现并在多达40个GPU的部署上针对三种MoE模型和两种工作负载进行评估，ELDR在保持模型输出不变的情况下将中位TPOT相比最强负载均衡基线降低5.9–13.9%，同时可推广到带专家并行的大规模MoE部署并与前缀缓存以低于1% KV缓存开销实现可叠加的组合。
Q1
1. Why does ELDR use IDF-reweighted discrete top-K counts rather than continuous gate softmax probabilities for its expert signature?
Continuous scores spread softmax mass over all experts, including those never loaded by the top-K, assigning magnitude to irrelevant experts and diluting the discriminative signal that distinguishes requests.
IDF reweighting is a mathematical operation that can only be applied to discrete integer counts.
Continuous probabilities are too computationally expensive to compute during the prefill phase.
1. 为什么 ELDR 选择使用 IDF 加权的离散 top-K 计数,而不是使用门控网络的连续 softmax 概率作为专家签名?
连续分数会将 softmax 质量分散到所有专家上,包括那些 top-K 永远不会加载的专家,从而给无关专家分配了权重,稀释了区分请求的判别信号。
IDF 加权是一种数学运算,只能应用于离散的整数计数。
连续概率在 prefill 阶段计算时计算开销过高,无法承受。

2. How does ELDR's signature cache maintain accuracy when a request only partially hits the prefix cache?
It completely recomputes the expert signature from scratch by re-running prefill on the full prompt.
It stores per-block expert signatures co-indexed with the KV cache, summing cached block signatures (from the request that first prefilled them) with fresh block signatures.
It disables prefix caching whenever a signature is needed and falls back to full prefill.
2. 当请求仅部分命中前缀缓存时,ELDR 的签名缓存如何保持签名的准确性?
它通过对完整 prompt 重新运行 prefill 来从头开始完全重新计算专家签名。
它以 KV 块粒度存储逐块的专家签名,并与 KV 缓存联合索引,将缓存块签名(来自首次填充它们的请求)与新生成的块签名相加。
它禁用了前缀缓存,只要需要签名就回退到完整的 prefill。
Q3
3. What is the fundamental reason that two equally-loaded decode workers can exhibit very different latencies when serving MoE models?
MoE decode cost is dominated by fetching the weights of every distinct expert the batch activates, so latency depends on the union of experts rather than the count of requests.
Different GPUs in a cluster have inherent hardware differences that cause latency variation independent of workload.
Workers in a disaggregated deployment use different MoE gating networks based on their assigned role.
3. 在服务 MoE 模型时,两个负载相同的 decode 工作节点表现出截然不同时延的根本原因是什么?
MoE decode 的代价主要取决于加载批次中每个不同专家的权重,因此时延由激活专家的并集决定,而不是由请求数量决定。
集群中不同的 GPU 存在固有的硬件差异,这些差异会导致与工作负载无关的时延变化。
在分离式部署中,工作节点根据其分配的角色使用不同的 MoE 门控网络。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with Paper 1 on Agentic Abstention—it's the most broadly relevant and introduces a clean new framing of when agents should stop acting, with a large-scale benchmark and a practical method (CONVOLVE) that improves abstention without retraining. It pairs naturally with Paper 2, which tackles a complementary agent failure mode: memory-induced sycophancy; together they form a coherent picture of 'agent judgment' problems (knowing when to stop vs. knowing when retrieved memory should be overridden). Save Paper 3 (ELDR) for after, as it's a specialized MoE serving systems contribution orthogonal to the agent-behavior theme.
建议先读第一篇关于"代理式弃答（Agentic Abstention）"的论文——它提出了一个清晰的新概念，探讨智能体应在何时停止行动，并附带大规模基准和实用方法 CONVOLVE，无需重新训练即可提升弃答能力。它与第二篇（记忆诱导的谄媚性）高度互补，二者共同勾勒出"智能体判断力"这一主题的不同侧面：何时该停止 vs. 何时应覆盖检索到的记忆。第三篇 ELDR 是面向 MoE 推理服务的系统优化，与前两篇的主题正交，建议最后阅读。
