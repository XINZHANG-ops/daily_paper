Published: 2026-01-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2601.15876

1. üìò Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on developing native computer use agents (CUA) that can autonomously interact with graphical user interfaces, advancing multimodal AI capabilities for desktop automation.
