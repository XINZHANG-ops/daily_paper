Wan-Streamer v0.1: End-to-end Real-time Interactive Foundation Models

Published: 2026-06-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.25041

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents an end-to-end, real-time, full-duplex multimodal foundation model (Wan-Streamer) for interactive audio-visual communication, situated at the intersection of multimodal AI, real-time interactive systems, and digital human/avatar technology.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on multimodal language models, video generation models, and causal/streaming generation methods; the new idea is unifying language, audio, and video as both inputs and outputs within a single Transformer with block-causal attention, eliminating reliance on external cascaded modules (VAD, ASR, TTS, animation, video generation).
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the high latency, error accumulation, and lack of true full-duplex behavior in cascaded interactive systems that stitch together separate perception, language, speech, and video generation modules.
4. 🛠️ Methods: It employs strictly causal audio/video VAEs, causal audio-visual encoders and decoders, a single Transformer with block-causal attention, conditional flow matching for audio-visual generation, and a two-GPU thinker-performer streaming inference pipeline that overlaps perception, state update, latent generation, and decoding.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The model achieves approximately 200 ms model-side response latency and approximately 550 ms total interaction latency (with 350 ms network budget), supporting 160 ms streaming units at 25 FPS for sub-second duplex audio-visual communication, validated at 192p output resolution.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文提出了一个用于实时全双工音视频交互的端到端流式多模态基础模型（Wan-Streamer），属于多模态人工智能、实时交互系统和数字人/虚拟形象技术交叉领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于多模态语言模型、视频生成模型以及因果/流式生成方法；新思路是在单个Transformer中以块因果注意力将语言、音频和视频统一为输入与输出，消除对外部级联模块（VAD、ASR、TTS、动画、视频生成）的依赖。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了级联交互系统中由独立感知、语言、语音和视频生成模块拼接所导致的高延迟、错误累积以及缺乏真正全双工行为的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 采用严格因果的音视频VAE、因果音视频编解码器、配备块因果注意力的单一Transformer、用于音视频生成的条件流匹配，以及在双GPU上实现感知、状态更新、潜变量生成和解码重叠的思考者-执行者（thinker-performer）流式推理流水线。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 模型实现了约200毫秒的模型端响应延迟和约550毫秒的总交互延迟（含350毫秒网络预算），支持25 FPS、160毫秒流式单元的亚秒级全双工音视频通信，并在192p输出分辨率上得到验证。

Paper 2

In-Context World Modeling for Robotic Control

Published: 2026-06-24

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26025

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper addresses Vision-Language-Action (VLA) models for robotic manipulation, specifically focusing on generalization to novel system configurations (e.g., unseen camera viewpoints and robot morphologies) in embodied AI.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on VLA models (RT-2, OpenVLA, π0), In-Context Learning (ICL) for robotics, and world modeling approaches. Its new idea is to repurpose the context window for **system identification** (how the system operates) rather than behavior specification (what task to do), enabling the robot to autonomously infer system dynamics from self-generated, task-agnostic exploratory interactions.
3. ❓ Problem: Modern VLA models fail to generalize to novel setups (e.g., altered camera viewpoints or robot morphologies) because they implicitly assume a fixed system configuration absorbed into model parameters, requiring costly data-intensive fine-tuning for every new environment.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The proposed In-Context World Modeling (ICWM) framework reformulates the VLA policy as π_θ(a_t | Ψ(T), o_t, l), where T represents a short history of task-agnostic random interaction clips (start frame, action, end frame). At test time, the robot performs active probing via random target poses, and the Transformer attends to T to implicitly recover the latent system configuration Ψ before predicting task actions, all without parameter updates or task-specific demonstrations.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: ICWM significantly outperforms baselines on LIBERO simulation (improving OOD success rate by 13.0% over Multi-View BC, 9.5% over Explicit Configuration, and 26.3% on LIBERO-Long) and on a real UR5e robot platform (average 71% vs. 17% for the MV baseline under novel viewpoints). It also generalizes to semantic perturbations (distractors, novel textures) and morphological changes (rigid spacers, shortened link lengths on WindowX), with minimal inference overhead via KV caching.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文涉及机器人操作中的视觉-语言-动作（VLA）模型，专注于具身智能中向新系统配置（如未见过的相机视角和机器人形态）的泛化能力。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于VLA模型（RT-2、OpenVLA、π0）、机器人领域的上下文学习（ICL）以及世界建模方法。其新思路是将上下文窗口重新用于**系统辨识**（系统如何运作），而非行为规范（要执行什么任务），使机器人能够从自主生成、与任务无关的探索性交互中推断系统动力学。
3. ❓ 问题: 现代VLA模型无法泛化到新设置（如改变的相机视角或机器人形态），因为它们隐式假设系统配置固定并被吸收到模型参数中，每遇到新环境都需要代价高昂的密集数据微调。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 所提出的上下文世界建模（ICWM）框架将VLA策略重新表述为 π_θ(a_t | Ψ(T), o_t, l)，其中T表示一小段与任务无关的随机交互片段（起始帧、动作、结束帧）。在测试时，机器人通过随机目标姿态执行主动探测，Transformer在预测任务动作前关注T以隐式恢复潜在的系统配置Ψ，整个过程无需参数更新或任务特定的演示。
5. 📊 结果与评估: ICWM在LIBERO仿真（比多视角BC基线提升OOD成功率13.0%，比显式配置提升9.5%，在LIBERO-Long上提升26.3%）和真实UR5e机器人平台（新颖视角下平均71%对比MV基线17%）上显著优于基线方法。它还能泛化到语义扰动（干扰物、新颖纹理）和形态变化（刚性垫片、WindowX上缩短的连杆长度），并通过KV缓存保持最小的推理开销。

Paper 3

OPID: On-Policy Skill Distillation for Agentic Reinforcement Learning

Published: 2026-06-24

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.26790

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on agentic reinforcement learning for large language models, proposing an on-policy skill distillation framework for long-horizon interactive agents operating in embodied, web, and search-based environments.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on outcome-based RL methods like GRPO, on-policy self-distillation, and skill-conditioned agent learning with external skill libraries, and introduces the new idea of extracting hierarchical hindsight skills directly from completed on-policy trajectories without requiring external skill memories or retrieved privileged context at inference time.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the sparse reward and poor credit-assignment problem of outcome-based agentic RL, as well as the limitations of existing skill-distillation methods that rely on costly external skill libraries and may suffer from state-distribution mismatch with the current policy.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes OPID, which extracts episode-level and step-level hierarchical skills from completed trajectories using an LLM-based analyzer, employs critical-first routing to select the appropriate skill granularity at each step, computes a token-level self-distillation advantage from the log-probability shift between original and skill-augmented contexts, and combines it with GRPO-style group-relative outcome advantages in a clipped policy optimization objective.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: OPID consistently outperforms GRPO and other skill-distillation baselines across ALFWorld, WebShop, and Search-based QA on multiple model scales (Qwen2.5-3B/7B, Qwen3-1.7B), achieving up to +12.8 points gain on ALFWorld and +26.5 on WebShop for smaller backbones, with better sample efficiency (60% data reaches GRPO's full-data performance), improved cross-domain generalization (+7.7 points on unseen split), and reduced repetitive or invalid behaviors without requiring skills at inference time.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本文聚焦于大语言模型的智能体强化学习，提出了一种面向长视野交互式智能体（在具身、网络和搜索环境中运行）的在策略技能蒸馏框架。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 本文基于基于结果的强化学习方法（如GRPO）、在策略自蒸馏以及依赖外部技能库的条件技能智能体学习，并提出了从已完成的在策略轨迹中直接提取分层后视技能的新思路，无需在推理时依赖外部技能记忆或检索的特权上下文。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了基于结果的智能体强化学习奖励稀疏和信用分配差的问题，以及现有技能蒸馏方法依赖昂贵的外部技能库、可能与当前策略状态分布不匹配的限制。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了OPID方法，使用基于LLM的分析器从已完成的轨迹中提取情节级和步骤级分层技能，采用关键优先路由机制在每一步选择合适的技能粒度，通过原始上下文和技能增强上下文之间的对数概率偏移计算词元级自蒸馏优势，并将其与GRPO风格的组相对结果优势结合，用于裁剪策略优化。
5. 📊 结果与评估: OPID在ALFWorld、WebShop和基于搜索的问答任务上，在多个模型规模（Qwen2.5-3B/7B、Qwen3-1.7B）上均持续优于GRPO和其他技能蒸馏基线，在较小骨干网络上ALFWorld上获得最高+12.8分、WebShop上+26.5分的提升，样本效率更佳（60%数据即可达到GRPO全数据性能），跨域泛化能力更好（未见分割上+7.7分），且无需在推理时使用技能即可减少重复或无效行为。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Start with Paper 2 (ICWM). Its insight—repurposing the in-context window for system identification rather than behavior specification—is conceptually elegant and broadly transferable, and it backs the idea with both simulation (LIBERO) and real-robot (UR5e) evidence. A nice thread connects all three papers: each replaces a brittle cascaded pipeline with a unified mechanism—ICWM eliminates per-setup fine-tuning, OPID removes external skill libraries via on-policy hindsight extraction, and Wan-Streamer collapses VAD/ASR/TTS/video stacks into a single block-causal Transformer. Reading OPID next would be natural if you care about RL agents, since its critical-first routing and token-level self-distillation advantage complement ICWM's parameter-free adaptation theme.
建议先读第二篇（ICWM）。其核心洞见——将上下文窗口用于“系统辨识”而非“行为指定”——概念上既优雅又具普适性，且论文同时给出了仿真（LIBERO）和真实机器人（UR5e）的实验支撑。三篇论文之间有一条清晰的共同主线：都用统一的机制取代脆弱的级联流水线——ICWM 消除了对每个新环境重新微调的需求，OPID 通过在线轨迹的事后提炼去掉了外部技能库，Wan-Streamer 则把 VAD/ASR/TTS/视频生成等模块压缩进单个块因果 Transformer。若你更关注强化学习智能体，可以接着读 OPID，其“关键优先路由”和 token 级自蒸馏优势设计与 ICWM “免参数适配”的理念相呼应。
