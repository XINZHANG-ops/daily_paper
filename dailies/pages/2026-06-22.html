Multi-LCB: Extending LiveCodeBench to Multiple Programming Languages

Published: 2026-06-17

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.20517

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper belongs to the domain of LLM evaluation and benchmarking, specifically focused on multilingual code generation assessment across diverse programming languages.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds upon LiveCodeBench (Jain et al., 2024), a contamination-aware Python-only code benchmark, and introduces the new idea of extending it to 12 programming languages through a unified STDIN/STDOUT format conversion pipeline.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the critical question of whether LLMs can generalize coding competence across multiple programming languages, rather than being overfitted to Python, which is the primary limitation of existing code benchmarks.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors designed an automatic conversion pipeline that transforms LeetCode's functional-format tasks into STDIN/STDOUT format, applied zero-shot prompting with language-specific system messages, and evaluated 24 LLMs using Pass@1 metric averaged over 10 runs with temperature 0.2.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The evaluation revealed substantial performance gaps across languages (Python averaging ~48% vs Scala ~29%), evidence of Python overfitting, language-specific contamination patterns, and demonstrated that Python performance is not a reliable proxy for cross-lingual competence.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文属于大语言模型评估与基准测试领域，专注于多编程语言代码生成的综合评估。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文基于LiveCodeBench（Jain等人，2024年）这一污染感知的Python专用代码基准测试，并引入通过统一STDIN/STDOUT格式转换管道将其扩展至12种编程语言的新思路。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了LLM是否能够在多种编程语言间泛化编码能力（而非仅仅过拟合于Python）这一关键问题，这也是现有代码基准测试的主要局限性。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者设计了一个自动转换管道，将LeetCode的函数式格式任务转换为STDIN/STDOUT格式，采用包含语言特定系统消息的零样本提示策略，并使用10次平均的Pass@1指标（温度为0.2）对24个LLM进行评估。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 评估揭示了跨语言间的显著性能差距（Python平均约48%对比Scala约29%），发现了Python过拟合、语言特定污染的证据，并证明Python性能并不能可靠代表跨语言编码能力。

Paper 2

PerceptionDLM: Parallel Region Perception with Multimodal Diffusion Language Models

Published: 2026-06-16

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.19534

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on multimodal large language models (MLLMs) and diffusion language models, specifically targeting efficient fine-grained visual perception and parallel region captioning tasks in computer vision and multimodal AI.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The work builds on autoregressive region-specific MLLMs (DAM, GAR, PixelRefer) and discrete diffusion language models (LLaDA, LLaDA-V, MMaDA); its new idea is to leverage the inherent non-autoregressive parallel decoding nature of diffusion language models to jointly caption multiple image regions in a single denoising process.
3. ❓ Problem: It addresses the linear growth in inference cost and latency of autoregressive MLLMs when perceiving and captioning multiple regions, as well as the underdeveloped perceptual capabilities and unexplored parallelism of existing multimodal diffusion language models.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes PerceptionDLM-Base as a strong diffusion VLM baseline, then introduces a parallel region perception framework combining learnable region prompting, RoI-aligned feature replay, and structured attention masking, trained on the newly constructed ParaCaption-5.7M dataset and evaluated on the new ParaDLC-Bench.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: PerceptionDLM achieves 62.4% average accuracy on ParaDLC-Bench (nearly doubling LLaDA-V's 35.2%), a Tokens-Per-Forward of 2.9 versus 1 for AR baselines, up to 3.5× throughput speedup in dense perception, and outperforms LLaDA-V on 15 of 16 multimodal benchmarks while remaining competitive with AR region-specific models.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于多模态大语言模型（MLLMs）与扩散语言模型领域，具体研究高效的细粒度视觉感知及多区域并行图像描述生成任务。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该工作基于自回归区域专用模型（DAM、GAR、PixelRefer）和离散扩散语言模型（LLaDA、LLaDA-V、MMaDA）；其新思路是利用扩散语言模型固有的非自回归并行解码能力，在单次去噪过程中联合为多个图像区域生成描述。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文旨在解决自回归MLLMs在多区域感知中推理成本和延迟随区域数量线性增长的问题，以及现有多模态扩散语言模型感知能力不足且并行性未被充分挖掘的难题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文首先构建了强大的扩散视觉语言模型基线PerceptionDLM-Base，随后提出结合可学习区域提示、RoI对齐特征回放和结构化注意力掩码的并行区域感知框架，并基于新构建的ParaCaption-5.7M数据集训练、在新提出的ParaDLC-Bench基准上进行评估。
5. 📊 结果与评估: PerceptionDLM在ParaDLC-Bench上达到62.4%的平均准确率（几乎是LLaDA-V 35.2%的两倍），Tokens-Per-Forward为2.9（自回归基线为1），在密集感知场景下吞吐量加速高达3.5倍，并在16个多模态基准中的15个上超越LLaDA-V，同时与自回归区域专用模型保持有竞争力的性能。

Paper 3

Playful Agentic Robot Learning

Published: 2026-06-16

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.19419

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on Playful Agentic Robot Learning, combining robotics, agentic AI, and continual skill acquisition through Code-as-Policy frameworks.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds on Code-as-Policy (CaP) agents, developmental robotics, and intrinsic motivation (curiosity-driven learning), proposing the new idea of using self-directed play as a pre-deployment skill-acquisition stage for agentic robots.
3. ❓ Problem: Current agentic robot systems remain task-driven, acquiring reusable skills only after receiving explicit instructions, lacking a proactive mechanism for accumulating capabilities before downstream tasks arrive.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper introduces RATS (Robotics Agent Teams), a multi-agent system with a Task Proposer (using a Goldilocks novelty-learnability rule), an Execution team (Planner, Policy Writer, Verifier, Diagnoser), and a Memory-Management team, all engaging in autonomous play to distill successful executions into a reusable Code-as-Policy skill library.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: RATS achieves 20.6 and 17.0 percentage-point gains over CaP-Agent0 on LIBERO-PRO and MolmoSpaces respectively, with learned skills transferring to RoboSuite (+8.9 pp) and real-world tasks (+8.8 pp) without fine-tuning the underlying model.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于"玩趣式智能体机器人学习"（Playful Agentic Robot Learning），融合机器人技术、智能体AI与基于代码即策略（Code-as-Policy）的持续技能习得。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于代码即策略（CaP）智能体、发展型机器人学与内在动机驱动的好奇心学习，提出新思路——将自主游戏作为下游任务到来前的预先技能习得阶段。
3. ❓ 问题: 当前智能体机器人系统仍以任务驱动为主，仅在收到外部指令后才习得可复用技能，缺乏在下游任务出现前主动积累能力的机制。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出RATS（机器人智能体团队），一个多智能体系统，包含任务提议者（采用"金发姑娘"新奇-可学习性规则）、执行团队（规划器、策略生成器、验证器、诊断器）和记忆管理团队，通过自主游戏将成功执行提炼为可复用的代码技能库。
5. 📊 结果与评估: RATS在LIBERO-PRO和MolmoSpaces上分别比CaP-Agent0提升20.6和17.0个百分点，所学技能可在不微调底层模型的情况下迁移至RoboSuite（+8.9 pp）和真实世界任务（+8.8 pp）。

Start with Paper 1 on multilingual code generation evaluation—its finding that Python performance is not a reliable proxy for cross-lingual coding competence is a wake-up call for anyone deploying LLMs, and the automatic STDIN/STDOUT conversion pipeline is a reusable methodology. Read Paper 2 next for its striking results—62.4% accuracy (nearly 2× LLaDA-V) with 3.5× throughput—offering a compelling efficiency-angle complement to Paper 1's generalization concerns.
