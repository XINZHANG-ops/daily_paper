Published: 2026-06-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.30534

BlockPilot: Instance-Adaptive Policy Learning for Diffusion-based Speculative Decoding

Published: 2026-06-29

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.31315

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: This paper focuses on accelerating LLM inference through diffusion-based speculative decoding, specifically within the domain of efficient large language model inference and parallel decoding strategies.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on speculative decoding (e.g., EAGLE-3) and diffusion language model-based drafting (e.g., DFlash), which use a fixed block size inherited from training. The new idea is to treat block size selection as a learnable, instance-adaptive policy problem rather than a static hyperparameter, recognizing that optimal block sizes vary across samples.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the suboptimality of fixed block sizes in diffusion-based speculative decoding, where a single block size is used for all inputs despite sample-dependent variability in predictability, leading to missed acceleration opportunities and accumulated drafting errors.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes BlockPilot, which formulates block size selection as a lightweight classification problem. A two-layer MLP predictor uses the raw predictive distribution of the last token after prefilling as input to predict the optimal block size from a small local candidate set {B−k, ..., B+k} centered at the training block size, with prediction performed only once per sample.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: BlockPilot achieves 5.92 acceptance length and 4.20× speedup on Qwen3-4B at T=1, and consistently outperforms EAGLE-3 and all DFlash variants across Qwen3-4B, Qwen3-8B, Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct, and Qwen3-Coder-30B on Math, Code, and Chat benchmarks, with only millisecond-level predictor overhead.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本论文专注于通过基于扩散模型的推测解码来加速大语言模型推理，属于高效大语言模型推理与并行解码策略领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文建立在推测解码（如EAGLE-3）和基于扩散语言模型的草稿生成（如DFlash）的基础上，这些方法沿用训练时的固定块大小。新思路是将块大小选择视为一个可学习的、实例自适应的策略问题，而非静态超参数，认识到最优块大小因样本而异。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了基于扩散模型的推测解码中使用固定块大小的次优性问题，即对所有输入使用单一块大小，尽管可预测性因样本而异，这导致加速机会的丧失和草稿生成错误的累积。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了BlockPilot，将块大小选择建模为一个轻量级分类问题。一个两层MLP预测器使用预填充阶段最后一个token的原始预测分布作为输入，从以训练块大小为中心的局部候选集{B−k, ..., B+k}中预测最优块大小，每个样本仅预测一次。
5. 📊 结果与评估: BlockPilot在Qwen3-4B上于T=1时实现了5.92的接受长度和4.20×的加速比，并在Qwen3-4B、Qwen3-8B、Llama-3.1-8B-Instruct和Qwen3-Coder-30B等模型上的数学、代码和聊天基准测试中一致优于EAGLE-3和所有DFlash变体，且预测器仅带来毫秒级开销。

DOPD: Dual On-policy Distillation

Published: 2026-06-28

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2606.30626

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on on-policy distillation (OPD) for large language models (LLMs) and vision-language models (VLMs), situated in the domain of knowledge distillation and post-training model compression.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: The paper builds on existing OPD paradigms (standard strong-to-weak distillation, self-distillation, and adaptive distillation) and introduces a novel "dual distillation" paradigm that addresses the newly identified "privilege illusion" problem, where naively injecting privileged information creates apparent but non-transferable performance gaps.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the problem of "privilege illusion" in on-policy distillation—where privileged information (e.g., verified hints, visual annotations) creates a teacher-student performance gap stemming from information asymmetry rather than transferable capability, leading to entropy collapse, shortcut learning, and poor distillation effectiveness, compounded by non-uniform token-level supervision.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes DOPD, an advantage-aware dual distillation paradigm that dynamically routes token-level supervision between a privileged teacher and a privileged student policy into four token regimes based on the "privilege advantage gap" and relative predicted probabilities, applying differentiated objectives (full-vocabulary JS divergence for high-advantage tokens, Top-K reverse KL with light/weak coefficients for other tokens).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: DOPD outperforms Vanilla OPD by 7.5 points on LLM-based and 6.0 points on VLM-based setups on average across eight benchmarks, surpasses the strongest baselines (ExOPD, Uni-OPD, EOPD) by 4.4-5.3 points, recovers 89.8% of the teacher-student gap, maintains 11.1-14.1 point gains across five model-size pairs, and demonstrates superior continual learning, out-of-distribution generalization, and training stability.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文聚焦于大语言模型（LLM）和视觉语言模型（VLM）的在策略蒸馏（On-Policy Distillation），属于知识蒸馏与模型后训练压缩领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 论文建立在现有在策略蒸馏范式（标准强弱蒸馏、自蒸馏、自适应蒸馏）的基础上，针对新发现的"特权幻觉"问题提出了一种新颖的"双蒸馏"范式，即朴注入特权信息会产生表面但不可迁移的性能差距。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了在策略蒸馏中的"特权幻觉"问题——特权信息（如验证提示、视觉标注）造成的师生性能差距源自信息不对称而非可迁移能力，导致熵坍缩、短路学习以及蒸馏效果不佳，这一问题因 token 级别监督的非均匀性而进一步加剧。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出了 DOPD，一种优势感知的双蒸馏范式，根据"特权优势差距"和相对预测概率将 token 级别监督动态分配到特权教师策略和特权学生策略之间，划分为四类 token 区间，并对不同区间施加差异化目标（高优势 token 使用全词表 JS 散度，其余 token 使用轻量/弱量级系数的 Top-K 反向 KL）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: DOPD 在八个基准上平均比 Vanilla OPD 在 LLM 和 VLM 设置上分别高出 7.5 分和 6.0 分，超越最强基线（ExOPD、Uni-OPD、EOPD）4.4-5.3 分，恢复 89.8% 的师生差距，在五组模型对中保持 11.1-14.1 分的稳定提升，并在持续学习、分布外泛化以及训练稳定性方面均表现出显著优势。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

Read DOPD first—it identifies a fundamental 'privilege illusion' problem in on-policy distillation and presents an elegant dual-distillation solution with strong empirical gains (7.5 points over vanilla OPD, recovering 89.8% of the teacher-student gap), making it broadly relevant for anyone working on LLM/VLM compression. BlockPilot is a natural follow-up if you're focused on inference efficiency, as it offers a lightweight, practical method (5.92 acceptance length, 4.20× speedup) for diffusion-based speculative decoding. All three papers share a theme of pushing efficiency boundaries—Orca via a unified world latent, BlockPilot via adaptive decoding, and DOPD via smarter distillation—making them a coherent trio for a day of reading on efficient and generalizable model design.
建议先读 DOPD——它揭示了在线策略蒸馏中一个根本性的"特权幻觉"问题，并提出了优雅的双蒸馏解决方案，实证效果显著（比标准 OPD 平均提升 7.5 分，恢复了 89.8% 的师生差距），对从事 LLM/VLM 压缩的研究者具有广泛参考价值。若你关注推理效率，BlockPilot 是顺理成章的第二选择，它为扩散式投机解码提供了一种轻量实用的方法（接受长度 5.92、4.20× 加速比）。三篇论文共同围绕"效率边界拓展"这一主题：Orca 通过统一世界潜空间，BlockPilot 通过自适应解码，DOPD 通过更智能的蒸馏，是一天高效阅读的连贯组合。
