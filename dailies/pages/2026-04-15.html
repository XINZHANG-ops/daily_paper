Paper 1

KnowRL: Boosting LLM Reasoning via Reinforcement Learning with Minimal-Sufficient Knowledge Guidance

Published: 2026-04-13

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12627

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses reinforcement learning (RL) for enhancing large language model (LLM) reasoning, specifically in mathematical problem-solving.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on RLVR (Reinforcement Learning with Verifiable Rewards) and existing hint-based RL methods, the paper introduces a minimal-sufficient guidance perspective, treating hints as atomic knowledge points (KPs) rather than long solution prefixes or full templates.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the reward sparsity problem in RLVR by selecting minimal-sufficient knowledge guidance instead of using redundant, inconsistent, or computationally expensive hints.
4. 🛠️ Methods: KnowRL decomposes hints into atomic knowledge points (KPs), uses Constrained Subset Search (CSS) to construct compact, interaction-aware KP subsets, and identifies a pruning interaction paradox to optimize robust subset curation.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: KnowRL-Nemotron-1.5B achieves 70.08 average accuracy without KP hints (+9.63 over baseline) and 74.16 with selected KPs, establishing new state-of-the-art at the 1.5B scale across eight reasoning benchmarks.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文涉及利用强化学习提升大语言模型（LLM）的推理能力，主要聚焦于数学问题求解领域。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于可验证奖励的强化学习（RLVR）和现有提示式强化学习方法，论文提出了最小充分指导视角，将提示分解为原子知识要点（KPs），而非冗长的解决方案前缀或完整模板。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文旨在解决RLVR中的奖励稀疏问题，通过选择最小充分的知识引导，避免使用冗余、不一致或计算开销大的提示。
4. 🛠️ 方法: KnowRL将提示分解为原子知识要点（KPs），使用约束子集搜索（CSS）构建紧凑且交互感知的KP子集，并识别剪枝交互悖论以优化鲁棒的子集筛选。
5. 📊 结果与评估: KnowRL-Nemotron-1.5B在不使用KP提示时达到70.08平均准确率（较基线提升+9.63），使用选定KPs时达到74.16，在8个推理基准上确立了1.5B规模的最先进性能。

Paper 2

ClawGUI: A Unified Framework for Training, Evaluating, and Deploying GUI Agents

Published: 2026-04-12

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.11784

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents ClawGUI, a unified open-source framework for the development, evaluation, and deployment of GUI agents that interact with applications through visual interfaces.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on prior work in GUI grounding, navigation models, and online reinforcement learning for GUI agents, ClawGUI introduces the novel integration of scalable RL training, standardized reproducible evaluation, and production-ready real-device deployment within a single coherent open-source system.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses three critical gaps in GUI agent research: (1) the lack of open-source RL training infrastructure validated on both virtual and physical devices, (2) poorly aligned evaluation protocols causing irreproducible results across papers, and (3) the absence of deployment pathways to bring trained agents to real users on real devices.
4. 🛠️ Methods: ClawGUI comprises three modules: ClawGUI-RL provides scalable online RL with GiGPO advantage estimation and a Process Reward Model for dense step-level supervision across parallel virtual and real device environments; ClawGUI-Eval enforces a standardized three-stage Infer→Judge→Metric pipeline across 6 benchmarks and 11+ models; and ClawGUI-Agent enables deployment via hybrid CLI-GUI control through 12+ chat platforms with persistent personalized memory.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: ClawGUI-2B achieves 17.1% Success Rate on MobileWorld GUI-Only, outperforming the same-scale MAI-UI-2B baseline by 6.0 absolute points; GiGPO with dense rewards yields a 17.9% relative improvement over episode-level GRPO; and ClawGUI-Eval achieves a 95.8% reproduction rate against official baselines across benchmarks and models.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文提出了ClawGUI，一个统一的开源框架，用于GUI智能体的开发、评估和部署，这些智能体通过视觉界面与应用进行交互。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于先前在GUI定位、导航模型和在线强化学习方面的研究，ClawGUI创新性地将可扩展的强化学习训练、标准化可复现评估和生产级真实设备部署整合到一个统一的开源系统中。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决了GUI智能体研究中的三个关键缺口：(1) 缺乏在虚拟和物理设备上验证的开源强化学习训练基础设施，(2) 评估协议不一致导致论文间结果不可复现，(3) 缺乏将训练好的智能体部署到真实用户真实设备的路径。
4. 🛠️ 方法: ClawGUI包含三个模块：ClawGUI-RL通过GiGPO优势估计和过程奖励模型提供可扩展的在线强化学习，在并行虚拟和真实设备环境中提供密集的步骤级监督；ClawGUI-Eval在6个基准测试和11个以上模型上强制执行标准化的三阶段Infer→Judge→Metric流程；ClawGUI-Agent通过12个以上聊天平台使用混合CLI-GUI控制进行部署，并配备持久化个性化记忆。
5. 📊 结果与评估: ClawGUI-2B在MobileWorld GUI-Only上达到17.1%的成功率，比同规模的MAI-UI-2B基线高出6.0个百分点；GiGPO结合密集奖励相比基于episode级别的GRPO产生17.9%的相对提升；ClawGUI-Eval在跨基准测试和模型的官方基线上达到95.8%的复现率。

Rethinking On-Policy Distillation of Large Language Models: Phenomenology, Mechanism, and Recipe

Published: 2026-04-13

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.13016

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper investigates on-policy distillation (OPD) for large language models, focusing on understanding the conditions under which OPD succeeds or fails during knowledge transfer from teacher to student models.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing OPD research showing it improves LLM post-training but lacks systematic analysis of failure modes, this paper introduces two governing conditions for OPD success: thinking-pattern consistency and genuine new knowledge beyond the student's training data, while also revealing the token-level mechanism through progressive alignment on high-probability overlap tokens.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves why OPD sometimes completely fails even with stronger teachers, and investigates the underlying token-level mechanism that drives successful versus unsuccessful distillation.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors conduct reverse distillation experiments, monitor dynamic metrics (overlap ratio, overlap-token advantage, entropy gap), ablate optimization support, and propose two recovery strategies: off-policy cold start using teacher-generated rollouts and teacher-aligned prompt selection to bridge thinking-pattern gaps.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Successful OPD exhibits progressive alignment on high-probability tokens (97-99% of probability mass), and optimizing only overlap tokens matches full top-K performance; off-policy cold start and teacher-aligned prompts recover failing OPD; however, reward quality degrades with trajectory depth, revealing fundamental limitations for long-horizon distillation.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文研究大型语言模型的在策略蒸馏（OPD），聚焦于理解知识从教师模型向学生模型迁移时OPD成功或失败的条件。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于现有OPD研究证明其能改进LLM后训练但缺乏失败模式的系统分析，本文提出了OPD成功的两个关键条件：思维模式一致性和超越学生训练数据的真正新知识，同时揭示了通过高概率重叠 token 进行渐进对齐的 token 级机制。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文解决的问题是：为何即使使用更强的教师模型OPD有时也会完全失败，以及成功与失败的蒸馏背后的 token 级机制是什么。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者进行反向蒸馏实验，监控动态指标（重叠率、重叠 token 优势、熵差），对优化支持进行消融实验，并提出两种恢复策略：使用教师生成 rollout 的离策略冷启动，以及教师对齐的提示选择来弥合思维模式差距。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 成功的OPD表现出高概率 token（占概率质量的97-99%）的渐进对齐，仅优化重叠 token 即可匹配完整 top-K 性能；离策略冷启动和教师对齐提示可恢复失败的OPD；然而，奖励质量随轨迹深度下降，揭示了长视野蒸馏的根本局限性。
