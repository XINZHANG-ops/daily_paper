Paper 1

KnowRL: Boosting LLM Reasoning via Reinforcement Learning with Minimal-Sufficient Knowledge Guidance

Published: 2026-04-13

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.12627

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses reinforcement learning for improving large language model reasoning, specifically in mathematical problem-solving.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on RLVR (Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards) which suffers from reward sparsity, the paper introduces a minimal-sufficient guidance perspective that decomposes hints into atomic knowledge points rather than treating hint design as quantity expansion.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper solves the reward sparsity problem in RL training for LLMs, where existing hint-based methods introduce redundancy and inconsistency by adding more tokens rather than selecting minimal sufficient guidance.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The paper proposes KnowRL, which uses Constrained Subset Search (CSS) to identify minimal-sufficient knowledge point subsets, addresses the pruning interaction paradox through interaction-aware selection, and injects compact hints during RL training.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: KnowRL-Nemotron-1.5B achieves 70.08 average accuracy without hints (+9.63 over baseline) and 74.16 with selected hints, establishing new state-of-the-art at the 1.5B scale across eight reasoning benchmarks.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文聚焦于强化学习提升大语言模型推理能力的研究领域，具体应用于数学问题求解任务。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于RLVR（可验证奖励强化学习）存在奖励稀疏问题，论文提出最小充分指导视角，将提示分解为原子级知识要点，而非将提示设计视为数量扩展问题。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了RL训练中LLM面临的奖励稀疏问题，现有基于提示的方法通过添加更多token而非选择最小充分指导来引入冗余和不一致性。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 论文提出KnowRL框架，使用约束子集搜索（CSS）识别最小充分知识要点子集，通过交互感知选择处理剪枝交互悖论，并在RL训练期间注入紧凑提示。
5. 📊 结果与评估: KnowRL-Nemotron-1.5B在无提示推理时达到70.08平均准确率（比基线提升9.63分），使用选定提示时达到74.16，在8个推理基准上确立1.5B规模的新最优性能。

Paper 2

ClawGUI: A Unified Framework for Training, Evaluating, and Deploying GUI Agents

Published: 2026-04-12

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.11784

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents ClawGUI, a unified open-source framework for training, evaluating, and deploying GUI agents that interact with applications through visual interfaces (taps, swipes, keystrokes) rather than programmatic APIs.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior work in GUI grounding models, navigation agents, and online RL for GUI tasks, the paper introduces the novel concept of a unified full-stack infrastructure that integrates scalable online RL training, standardized reproducible evaluation, and real-device deployment into a single coherent pipeline.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses three critical gaps: (1) the lack of open-source RL infrastructure for GUI agents with validated real-device support, (2) misaligned evaluation protocols across papers that prevent reliable comparison, and (3) the broken deployment loop from research to real users on real devices.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The framework consists of three integrated modules: ClawGUI-RL provides scalable online RL training using GiGPO with a Process Reward Model for dense step-level rewards across parallel virtual and real environments; ClawGUI-Eval enforces a standardized three-stage pipeline (Infer→Judge→Metric) across 6 benchmarks and 11+ models; and ClawGUI-Agent enables deployment through hybrid CLI-GUI control and personalized memory across Android, HarmonyOS, and iOS via 12+ chat platforms.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: ClawGUI-2B trained within the framework achieves 17.1% Success Rate on MobileWorld GUI-Only, outperforming the same-scale MAI-UI-2B baseline by 6.0% absolute points; replacing episode-level GRPO with step-level GiGPO yields a 2.6% improvement (14.5%→17.1%); and ClawGUI-Eval achieves a 95.8% reproduction rate against official baselines across benchmarks and models.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 本论文提出了ClawGUI，一个统一的开源框架，用于训练、评估和部署GUI智能体，这些智能体通过视觉界面（点击、滑动、按键）而非程序化API与应用进行交互。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于先前在GUI基础模型、导航智能体及GUI任务在线强化学习方面的研究，本文引入了一个创新概念——统一的全栈基础设施，将可扩展的在线强化学习训练、标准化可复现评估和真实设备部署整合到单一协调流程中。
3. ❓ 问题: 本文解决了三个关键差距：（1）缺乏支持真实设备验证的开源GUI智能体强化学习基础设施，（2）各论文间评估协议不一致导致无法可靠比较，（3）从研究到真实用户的部署闭环断裂。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该框架由三个集成模块组成：ClawGUI-RL通过GiGPO和过程奖励模型提供可扩展的在线强化学习训练，在并行虚拟和真实环境中提供密集步级奖励；ClawGUI-Eval在6个基准测试和11个以上模型上强制执行标准化三阶段流程（推理→评判→指标）；ClawGUI-Agent通过混合CLI-GUI控制和个性化记忆，通过12个以上聊天平台在Android、HarmonyOS和iOS上实现部署。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在框架内训练的ClawGUI-2B在MobileWorld GUI-Only上达到17.1%成功率，比同规模的MAI-UI-2B基线绝对提升6.0个百分点；将回合级GRPO替换为步级GiGPO带来2.6%的提升（14.5%→17.1%）；ClawGUI-Eval在跨基准测试和模型上达到95.8%的官方基线复现率。
