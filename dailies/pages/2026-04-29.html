AutoResearchBench: Benchmarking AI Agents on Complex Scientific Literature Discovery

Published: 2026-04-27

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25256

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper presents AutoResearchBench, a benchmark for evaluating AI agents on autonomous scientific literature discovery tasks within computer science domains.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on prior work in LLM-based AI scientists and general agentic web-browsing benchmarks (e.g., GAIA, BrowseComp), this paper introduces a novel research-oriented, literature-focused, and open-ended benchmark with two complementary task paradigms: Deep Research and Wide Research.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the lack of specialized benchmarks for evaluating whether AI agents can effectively search scientific corpora, verify fine-grained technical conditions across full papers, and determine when searches are complete—capabilities not captured by general web-browsing benchmarks.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The benchmark comprises 1,000 queries (600 Deep, 400 Wide) built through a full-text-first human-machine pipeline with multi-stage verification, evaluated using a ReAct-based agent framework over a controlled corpus of 3+ million arXiv papers via the DeepXiv search tool.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: Top-performing models achieve only 9.39% accuracy on Deep Research and 9.31% IoU on Wide Research, with most models falling below 5%, revealing a substantial performance gap compared to general web-browsing benchmarks and highlighting key bottlenecks in scientific reasoning, evidence utilization, and constraint verification.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文介绍了AutoResearchBench，一个用于评估AI智能体在计算机科学领域进行自主科研文献发现任务的基准测试。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于此前LLM驱动的AI科学家和通用智能体网页浏览基准（如GAIA、BrowseComp）的研究，该论文提出了一个新颖的、以研究为导向的、以文献为中心的开放性基准，包含两个互补的任务范式：深度研究（Deep Research）和广度研究（Wide Research）。
3. ❓ 问题: 该论文针对当前缺乏专门基准来评估AI智能体能否有效搜索科学语料库、验证全文中的细粒度技术条件、以及判断搜索是否完成等问题——这些能力在通用网页浏览基准中未被考察。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 该基准包含1,000个查询（600个深度、400个广度），通过全文优先的人机协作流程构建并经过多阶段验证，使用基于ReAct框架的智能体在包含300多万篇arXiv论文的受控语料库上通过DeepXiv搜索工具进行评估。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 表现最佳的模型在深度研究任务上仅达到9.39%的准确率，在广度研究任务上仅达到9.31%的IoU，且大多数模型低于5%，这揭示了与通用网页浏览基准相比存在的显著性能差距，并凸显了科学推理、证据利用和约束验证方面的关键瓶颈。

Refinement via Regeneration: Enlarging Modification Space Boosts Image Refinement in Unified Multimodal Models

Published: 2026-04-27

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.25636

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses image refinement in unified multimodal models (UMMs) for text-to-image generation, focusing on improving prompt-image alignment through regeneration rather than editing.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on refinement-via-editing (RvE) approaches that use editing instructions, the paper proposes a novel refinement-via-regeneration (RvR) framework that treats refinement as conditional image generation without intermediate editing instructions or pixel-level consistency constraints.
3. ❓ Problem: Existing RvE methods suffer from two key limitations: (1) editing instructions only coarsely describe semantic gaps, leading to incomplete refinement, and (2) pixel-level preservation constraints unnecessarily restrict the modification space, limiting the upper bound of refinement performance.
4. 🛠️ Methods: RvR regenerates images conditioned on the target prompt and semantic ViT tokens of the initial image, discarding editing instructions and VAE features; training uses independently generated T2I samples with varying alignment levels rather than editing pairs, emphasizing semantic correction.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: RvR achieves significant improvements: Geneval 0.78→0.91, DPGBench 84.02→87.21, UniGenBench++ 61.53→77.41, consistently outperforming RvE baselines and state-of-the-art T2I models across multiple benchmarks.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文涉及统一多模态模型（UMMs）在文生图（T2I）任务中的图像优化领域，聚焦于通过重建而非编辑来提升图像与提示的对齐度。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于先前通过编辑指令进行优化的方法（RvE），该论文提出了全新的"通过重建进行优化"（RvR）框架，将优化重新定义为有条件的图像生成过程，无需中间编辑指令或像素级一致性约束。
3. ❓ 问题: 现有RvE方法存在两个主要局限：(1) 编辑指令只能粗略描述语义差距，导致优化不完整；(2) 像素级保留约束不必要地限制了可修改空间，制约了优化性能的上限。
4. 🛠️ 方法: RvR基于目标提示和初始图像的语义ViT tokens进行图像重建，丢弃编辑指令和VAE特征；训练采用独立生成的、具有不同对齐程度的T2I样本而非编辑对数据，强调语义纠正而非外观保持。
5. 📊 结果与评估: RvR取得显著提升：Geneval从0.78提升至0.91，DPGBench从84.02提升至87.21，UniGenBench++从61.53提升至77.41，在多个基准测试中一致超越RvE基线方法和现有最先进的文生图模型。

From Skills to Talent: Organising Heterogeneous Agents as a Real-World Company

Published: 2026-04-23

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2604.22446

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper addresses AI multi-agent organization design, treating heterogeneous AI agents as employees in a structured "AI company" with talent management, task decomposition, and self-evolution.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on modular skills/tool integrations for individual agents and multi-agent collaboration through message passing. New idea: introducing "AI Organisation" as an organisation-level abstraction that governs workforce assembly, coordination, and evolution—decoupled from individual agent capabilities.
3. ❓ Problem: Current multi-agent systems suffer from fixed team structures, tightly coupled coordination, inability to interoperate across heterogeneous backends, and lack persistent self-improvement, leaving a fundamental gap in how agent workforces should be structured and managed.
4. 🛠️ Methods: Three pillars: (1) Talent–Container architecture with six typed organisational interfaces and a Talent Market for on-demand agent recruitment; (2) Explore-Execute-Review (E2R) tree search with DAG-based task execution providing formal termination and deadlock-freedom guarantees; (3) Self-evolution via individual reflection, project retrospectives, and formal HR pipeline (periodic reviews, PIP, automated offboarding).
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: On PRDBench (50 project-level software tasks), OMC achieves 84.67% success rate, surpassing all baselines by 15.48 percentage points, at ~$6.91 per task. Cross-domain case studies (content generation, game development, audiobook production, research survey) demonstrate generality without domain-specific configuration.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文涉及AI多智能体组织设计，将异构AI智能体视为结构化"AI公司"中的员工，具备人才管理、任务分解和自我进化机制。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于单个智能体的模块化技能/工具集成及通过消息传递进行多智能体协作。新思路：引入"AI组织"作为组织级抽象概念，治理智能体工作力的组装、协作和演进——与个体智能体能力相解耦。
3. ❓ 问题: 当前多智能体系统受限于固定团队结构、紧耦合协作、无法跨异构后端互操作，以及缺乏持续自我改进的能力，导致智能体工作力如何结构化和管理存在根本性缺口。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 三大支柱：(1) Talent-Container架构，具备六类组织接口和按需招募智能体的Talent Market；(2) Explore-Execute-Review (E2R)树搜索与基于DAG的任务执行，提供形式化的终止和死锁自由保证；(3) 自我进化机制，包括个体反思、项目回顾及正式HR流程（定期评估、PIP、自动裁员）。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在PRDBench（50个项目级软件任务）上，OMC达到84.67%成功率，超越所有基线15.48个百分点，每任务约$6.91成本。跨领域案例研究（内容生成、游戏开发、有声书制作、研究调研）证明其通用性，无需领域特定配置。
