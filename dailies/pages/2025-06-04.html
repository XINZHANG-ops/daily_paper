Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2506.03143

2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Previous research relied on coordinate-based methods for GUI interaction; this paper proposes a novel coordinate-free approach using attention mechanisms and a dedicated token to directly ground actions to visual regions.

4. üõ†Ô∏è Methods: The method uses an attention-based action head with a special token to attend to relevant visual patches, multi-patch supervision for training, and a grounding verifier to select optimal action regions.
