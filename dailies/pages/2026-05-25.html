Paper 1

TransitLM: A Large-Scale Dataset and Benchmark for Map-Free Transit Route Generation

Published: 2026-05-20

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.22355

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper introduces TransitLM, a dataset and benchmark for map-free public transit route generation using large language models in the urban mobility and transportation domain.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: It builds upon classical graph-based routing algorithms and general-purpose LLMs, proposing that end-to-end transit route planning can be learned entirely from data without structured maps or routing engines.
3. ❓ Problem: It solves the problem that existing transit route planning systems rely heavily on structured map infrastructure and complex routing engines, while current datasets and general-purpose LLMs lack the complete route structures and behavioral annotations needed for data-driven end-to-end planning.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The authors construct a large-scale dataset of 13+ million planning records from four Chinese cities, release a continual pre-training corpus and supervised fine-tuning benchmark for three tasks, and train LLMs with expanded vocabularies to internalize transit topology and spatial relationships.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: The trained model achieves over 97% connectivity, 98% station grounding, and 71–74% route exact match across benchmarks, demonstrating feasible end-to-end map-free generation with implicit spatial grounding from GPS coordinates and successful generalization across planning objectives without negative transfer.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 论文提出了TransitLM，一个面向无地图公共交通路线生成的大规模数据集和基准，属于城市出行与交通领域的应用研究。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 该研究基于经典图算法和通用大语言模型的局限性，提出完全从数据中学习端到端公交路线规划的新思路，无需依赖结构化地图或路由引擎。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了现有公交路线规划系统严重依赖地图基础设施和复杂路由引擎、且当前数据集与通用大语言模型均缺乏完整路线结构与行为标注以支持数据驱动端到端规划的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 作者构建了来自四个中国城市超过1300万条规划记录的数据集，发布了持续预训练语料和三个任务的监督微调基准，并通过扩展词汇表训练大语言模型以内化交通网络拓扑与空间关系。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 实验结果表明，训练后的模型在连通性、站点定位和路线精确匹配等指标上达到90%以上，验证了无需地图即可实现端到端路线生成，且能从GPS坐标隐式完成空间定位，单一联合训练模型即可在多个任务上达到或超过专用模型性能。

Paper 3

DelTA: Discriminative Token Credit Assignment for Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards

Published: 2026-05-19

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2605.21467

1. 📘 Topic and Domain: The paper studies token-level credit assignment in Reinforcement Learning from Verifiable Rewards (RLVR) for large language models, focusing on improving reasoning capabilities in mathematical reasoning and code generation domains.
2. 💡 Previous Research and New Ideas: Building on sequence-level RLVR methods like DAPO and observations of sparse token-level distributional shifts, the paper introduces a novel discriminator view showing that policy-gradient updates implicitly act as linear discriminators over token-gradient vectors, and proposes DelTA to reshape this implicit discrimination for better credit assignment.
3. ❓ Problem: The paper addresses the problem that standard sequence-level RLVR update directions are formed by side-wise centroids of token-gradient vectors that get dominated by shared high-frequency patterns (e.g., formatting tokens), thereby diluting sparse yet discriminative directions that better distinguish high-reward responses from low-reward ones.
4. 🛠️ Methods: The proposed DelTA method estimates token-level discriminative coefficients by iteratively refining side-wise centroids via entropy-regularized soft assignment, then uses these bounded coefficients to reweight a self-normalized clipped RLVR surrogate, amplifying side-specific token-gradient directions while downweighting shared or weakly discriminative ones.
5. 📊 Results and Evaluation: DelTA outperforms the strongest same-scale baselines by 3.26 and 2.62 average points on seven mathematical benchmarks using Qwen3-8B-Base and Qwen3-14B-Base, respectively, and demonstrates consistent improvements in code generation, alternative backbone architectures (Olmo3-7B-Base), and out-of-domain evaluations.
1. 📘 主题与领域: 该论文研究可验证奖励强化学习（RLVR）中的令牌级信用分配问题，致力于提升大型语言模型在数学推理与代码生成等领域的推理能力。
2. 💡 先前研究与新思路: 基于DAPO等序列级RLVR方法及RLVR引发稀疏令牌分布偏移的观察，论文提出了策略梯度更新隐式地对令牌梯度向量进行线性判别的全新视角，并据此设计了DelTA方法来重塑该隐式判别过程以优化信用分配。
3. ❓ 问题: 论文解决了标准序列级RLVR中由正负侧令牌梯度质心构成的更新方向易被共享的高频令牌模式（如格式令牌）主导，从而稀释了区分高奖励与低奖励响应所需的稀疏判别性方向的问题。
4. 🛠️ 方法: 所提出的DelTA方法通过熵正则化软分配对侧向质心进行迭代精化以估计令牌级判别系数，进而利用这些有界系数对自归一化的裁剪RLVR替代目标进行重加权，从而放大侧向特定的令牌梯度方向并抑制共享或弱判别性方向。
5. 📊 结果与评估: 在七个数学基准测试上，DelTA分别在Qwen3-8B-Base和Qwen3-14B-Base上比最强同规模基线平均提升3.26分和2.62分，并在代码生成、其他骨干模型（Olmo3-7B-Base）及域外评估中均展现出一致的性能提升与良好的泛化能力。

Today's Reading Tips 今日阅读推荐

I recommend starting with the personality perception paper (Paper 2), as its discovery of a stark 'Prejudice Gap'—models producing correct ratings without retrieving behavioral evidence—offers a critical lens for evaluating whether models in the other papers truly understand or merely correlate. This tension between superficial pattern-matching and genuine grounded reasoning directly parallels TransitLM's challenge of implicit spatial grounding and DelTA's attempt to amplify discriminative tokens over shared high-frequency noise.
建议优先阅读第二篇关于人格感知的论文，其揭示的'偏见鸿沟'——模型在不检索行为证据的情况下给出正确评分——为评判另外两项研究中模型是真理解还是伪相关提供了关键视角。这种表面模式匹配与真实 grounded 推理之间的张力，与 TransitLM 的隐式空间 grounding 挑战以及 DelTA 强调判别性 token 而非共享高频噪声的技术目标形成了直接呼应。
