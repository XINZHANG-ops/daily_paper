Published: 2025-12-01

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2512.02014

1. üìò Topic and Domain: The paper presents TUNA, a unified multimodal AI model for joint visual understanding and generation tasks in computer vision and natural language processing.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on previous unified multimodal models that use either decoupled or unified visual representations, TUNA proposes a novel unified representation approach by cascading a VAE encoder with a representation encoder.
3. ‚ùì Problem: The paper addresses the limitations of existing unified multimodal models that either suffer from representation format mismatches or favor one task over another, leading to suboptimal performance.
4. üõ†Ô∏è Methods: TUNA employs a three-stage training pipeline using a VAE encoder connected to a representation encoder to create unified visual representations, which are then processed by an LLM decoder for both understanding and generation tasks.
