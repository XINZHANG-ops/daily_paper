Published: 2025-10-15

3. ‚ùì Problem: The paper addresses the challenge of understanding how LLMs internally structure their reasoning and aims to improve reinforcement learning by making credit assignment more targeted and effective.
4. üõ†Ô∏è Methods: The authors analyze attention patterns using two metrics (WAAD and FAI) to identify critical reasoning nodes, then implement three RL strategies that amplify credit assignment to these key tokens during training.
