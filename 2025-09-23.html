TempSamp-R1: Effective Temporal Sampling with Reinforcement Fine-Tuning for Video LLMs

Published: 2025-09-22

Link: http://arxiv.org/pdf/2509.18056

1. üìò Topic and Domain: The paper focuses on improving temporal video understanding in multimodal large language models (MLLMs) through a reinforcement learning framework called TempSamp-R1.
2. üí° Previous Research and New Ideas: Based on existing Group Relative Policy Optimization (GRPO) methods, the paper proposes integrating off-policy supervision with on-policy sampling and introduces non-linear soft advantage estimation for more stable training.
3. ‚ùì Problem: The paper addresses the limitations of current reinforcement learning methods in temporal video grounding tasks, where large temporal search spaces make it difficult to identify accurate temporal solutions.
4. üõ†Ô∏è Methods: The authors implement TempSamp-R1 which combines on-policy generation with off-policy guidance from ground-truth annotations, uses soft advantage computation, and employs a hybrid Chain-of-Thought training paradigm.
5. üìä Results and Evaluation: TempSamp-R1 outperformed baselines on multiple benchmarks, achieving improvements on Charades-STA (R1@0.7: 52.9%), ActivityNet Captions (R1@0.5: 56.0%), and QVHighlights (mAP: 30.0%), while showing strong few-shot generalization capabilities.
